## 📋 Topic Matrix (10 Topics per Grade)

Each grade has **10 thematic bundles** aligned with ICFES common topics but elevated to international standards.

### Grade 3 (A1) - Foundational
1. **Greetings & Introductions** - "Hello, my name is..."
2. **Colors & Shapes** - Basic adjectives
3. **Family Members** - Vocabulary building
4. **Animals** - Simple descriptions
5. **Food & Drinks** - "I like..."
6. **Numbers 1-100** - Counting, age
7. **School Objects** - "This is a..."
8. **Body Parts** - "I have..."
9. **Weather** - "It is sunny"
10. **Days & Months** - Calendar vocabulary

### Grade 6 (A2+) - Pre-Intermediate
1. **Travel & Tourism** - Airport, hotel vocab
2. **Technology** - Devices, internet
3. **Sports & Hobbies** - Leisure activities
4. **Health & Medicine** - Doctor visits
5. **Environment** - Recycling, pollution
6. **Shopping** - Prices, comparisons
7. **Jobs & Careers** - Professions
8. **Celebrations** - Holidays, traditions
9. **Transportation** - Public transport
10. **Rules & Regulations** - Modal verbs

### Grade 9 (B2) - Upper-Intermediate
1. **Social Media** - Digital citizenship
2. **Global Issues** - Poverty, inequality
3. **Science & Innovation** - Discoveries
4. **Arts & Culture** - Museums, music
5. **Education Systems** - Comparing countries
6. **Crime & Justice** - Legal vocabulary
7. **Economics** - Supply/demand basics
8. **Psychology** - Human behavior
9. **History** - World events
10. **Philosophy** - Ethics, morality

### Grade 11 (B2/C1) - Advanced
1. **Global Citizenship** ✅ (Already created)
2. **Artificial Intelligence** - Ethics, future
3. **Climate Change** - Scientific discourse
4. **Democracy & Governance** - Political systems
5. **Globalization** - Trade, culture
6. **Human Rights** - Universal declarations
7. **Media Literacy** - Fake news, bias
8. **Entrepreneurship** - Business concepts
9. **Space Exploration** - STEM vocabulary
10. **Literature Analysis** - Themes, symbolism
