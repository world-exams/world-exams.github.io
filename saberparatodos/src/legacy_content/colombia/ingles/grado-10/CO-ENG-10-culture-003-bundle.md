## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-culture-003-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Migration.
"A person who moves from one country to live permanently in another is an **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct word.

### Options
- [x] A) immigrant
- [ ] B) tourist
- [ ] C) pilot
- [ ] D) soldier

### Explicación Pedagógica
An **immigrant** (inmigrante) is someone who arrives in a new country to settle there.

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-culture-003-v3`

### Contexto
Used to (Past habit/state).
"Migration was different in the past. People **________** (not / move) as fast as they do today."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct form for a past habit or state that is no longer true.

### Options
- [x] A) didn't use to move
- [ ] B) weren't used to moving
- [ ] C) hadn't used to move
- [ ] D) didn't use to moving

### Explicación Pedagógica
We use **didn't use to + verb** to talk about things that were true in the past but aren't anymore.

Be used to (Familiarity with a situation).
"I've lived in Spain for 5 years, so I **________** (be / used to) eating dinner late."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct form to express that an action is now normal or familiar.

### Options
- [x] A) am used to
- [ ] B) used to

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Be used to + verb-ing** means that something is no longer new or strange; it has become familiar.

## Question 8 (Difícil B - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-culture-003-v8`

### Contexto
Used to vs Be used to (Mixed).
"When I was a child, I **________** (dream) of traveling the world. Now that I am an immigrant, I **________** (live) in a big city."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pair of verb forms.

### Options
- [x] A) used to dream / am used to living
- [ ] B) was used to dreaming / used to live
- [ ] C) used to dreaming / am used to live
- [ ] D) am used to dream / used to living

### Explicación Pedagógica
"**Used to dream**" is a past habit. "**Am used to living**" is a present familiarity with a situation.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-culture-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"I am used to wake up early."

### Enunciado
What is the error in this sentence?

### Options
- [x] A) After 'be used to', the verb must be in the gerund form (-ing). It should be 'waking up'.
- [ ] B) It should be 'I used to wake up'.
- [ ] C) 'Early' should be 'late'.
- [ ] D) There is no error.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Be used to** and **Get used to** are followed by the **gerund** (-ing) or a noun. "I am used to **waking up** early."
