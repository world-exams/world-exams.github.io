## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-nature-004-v1`

Vocabulary: Environment.
"Energy that comes from natural resources and doesn't run out is **________** energy."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct adjective.

### Options
- [x] A) renewable
- [ ] B) fossil
- [ ] C) limited
- [ ] D) dirty

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Renewable** energy (like solar, wind) is sustainable.

### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) Despite
