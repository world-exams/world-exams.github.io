### Enunciado
What is an **Urban Park**?

### Options
- [x] A) A large open area within a city designed for recreation and environmental protection.
- [ ] B) A special type of high-speed train that only travels underground at night.
- [ ] C) A machine that automatically paints the walls of a house with green color.
- [ ] D) A historical document that explains how to build a stone bridge in Italy.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Urban parks** (parques urbanos) provide ecological benefits and recreation in dense cities.

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-cities-001-v10`

### Contexto
The concept of "Tactical Urbanism".

### Enunciado
What characterizes **Tactical Urbanism**?

### Options
- [x] A) Low-cost, temporary changes to the built environment intended to improve local neighborhoods and city gathering places.
- [ ] B) A military strategy where soldiers use very large tanks to build roads and houses in 15 minutes.
- [ ] C) A type of video game where players compete to destroy as many buildings as possible in one day.
