## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-art-001-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying primary literary devices.

### Enunciado
What is a **Metaphor**?

### Options
- [x] A) A figure of speech that describes an object or action in a way that isn't literally true, but helps explain an idea.
- [ ] B) A special type of brush used only by professional painters in Europe.
- [ ] C) The name of a very small and fast animal that lives in the jungle.
- [ ] D) A mathematical rule for calculating the area of a circle.

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **metaphor** (metáfora) is a comparative figure of speech (e.g., "Time is money").

## Question 5 (Media A - Difficulty 3)

Using the present perfect for artistic influence.
"This novel **________ ________** translated into over 50 languages and has influenced millions of readers."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct present perfect passive for an ongoing legacy.

### Options
- [x] A) has been
- [ ] B) was being
- [ ] C) is been
- [ ] D) had been

### Explicación Pedagógica
The **Present Perfect Passive** ("has been + past participle") connects a past completion to a present state of influence.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-art-001-v9`

### Contexto
Analyzing a literary metaphor's depth.
"The writer describes the city as a **'giant, hungry animal that never sleeps'**."

### Enunciado
What does this metaphor imply about the city?

### Options
- [x] A) The city is dangerous, full of life, and demands a lot of energy and resources.
- [ ] B) The city is literally an animal that only eats glass and steel buildings.
- [ ] C) The city has no electricity at night because all the animals are sleeping.
- [ ] D) Most people in the city are professional vets who take care of large animals.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Complex metaphors require **symbolic interpretation** (interpretación simbólica) beyond the literal meaning.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-art-001-v10`

### Contexto
The concept of "Aesthetic".

### Enunciado
When people discuss the **Aesthetic** of a movie, they are referring to ________.

### Options
- [x] A) Its visual style, beauty, and the overall artistic atmosphere it creates.
- [ ] B) The number of people who bought tickets during the first weekend of the release.
- [ ] C) The type of popcorn that is sold in the cinema where the movie is shown.
- [ ] D) The total weight of all the cameras and equipment used by the director.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Aesthetic** (estética) refers to a set of principles underlying and guiding the work of a particular artist or artistic movement.
