## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-media-001-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying primary media formats.

### Enunciado
Which of these is a **Podcast**?

### Options
- [x] A) A digital audio file made available on the internet for downloading to a computer or mobile device.
- [ ] B) A special type of paper used for printing very large maps of the world.
- [ ] C) A machine that automatically washes clothes and dries them in 5 minutes.
- [ ] D) A historical document that describes how to build a wooden boat.

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **podcast** (pódcast) is a popular digital audio format for news, education, and entertainment.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-media-001-v9`

### Contexto
Analyzing a situation of "Deepfake" technology.
"A video appears online showing a CEO announcing that his company is going bankrupt. Millions of people believe it, but later it's revealed the video was generated by AI."

### Enunciado
What is the primary danger illustrated by this scenario?

### Options
- [x] A) The erosion of trust in digital media and the potential for market manipulation.
- [ ] B) The fact that the CEO didn't have a good haircut in the fake video.
- [ ] C) That computers are becoming too expensive for people to buy them in Europe.
- [ ] D) That people are forgetting how to use traditional paper and pens to write news.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Deepfakes** (ultrafalsos) pose a significant threat to **media credibility** and social stability.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-media-001-v10`

### Contexto
The concept of "Media Literacy".

### Enunciado
How can a person demonstrate high **Media Literacy** skills?

### Options
- [x] A) By cross-referencing information from multiple reliable sources before forming an opinion.
- [ ] B) By believing every single post they see on social media as long as it has many 'likes'.
- [ ] C) By throwing their phone and computer in the trash to avoid all types of news.
- [ ] D) By learning the names of the top 100 most famous YouTubers in the world in one day.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Media literacy** (alfabetización mediática) is the ability to access, analyze, evaluate, and create media in various forms.
