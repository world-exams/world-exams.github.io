## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-rights-001-v1`

### Contexto
Defining a basic political system.

### Enunciado
What is a **Democracy**?

### Options
- [x] A) A system of government where the citizens exercise power by voting.
- [ ] B) A system where one king has total power and no one else can vote.
- [ ] C) A type of music that is played only during elections in Europe.
- [ ] D) A special tool used to measure the height of mountains.

### Explicación Pedagógica
In a **democracy** (democracia), the people are the source of political authority.

Understanding the "Separation of Powers".

### Enunciado
Why is the **Separation of Powers** (Legislative, Executive, Judicial) important?

### Options
- [x] A) To prevent any one person or branch from having too much power (Checks and Balances).
- [ ] B) To make sure that the government has three different types of cars for the president.
- [ ] C) To allow the citizens to vote three times every day for their favorite food.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-rights-001-v9`

### Contexto
Analyzing a political scenario.
"A country's leader decides to close all newspapers that criticize his government and arrests any person who protests in the street."

### Enunciado
What is this an example of?

### Options
- [x] A) Authoritarianism (or Dictatorship).
- [ ] B) Direct Democracy.
- [ ] C) Professional Journalism.
- [ ] D) International Cooperation.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Authoritarianism** (autoritarismo) is the opposite of a free democracy, characterized by high central power and limited freedoms.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-rights-001-v10`

### Contexto
The concept of "Non-violent Resistance".

### Enunciado
What was the philosophy of leaders like Mahatma Gandhi regarding social change?

### Options
- [x] A) Non-violent resistance (civil disobedience) to achieve justice without using physical force.
- [ ] B) Using as many weapons as possible to destroy the entire country.
- [ ] C) Asking the citizens to stay in their houses and never talk about politics ever again.
- [ ] D) Building a giant wall around the country to keep all new ideas outside.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Non-violent resistance** (resistencia no violenta) is a powerful tool for social justice and political change.
