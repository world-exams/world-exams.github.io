## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-global-002-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary.
"The process by which businesses or other organizations develop international influence is **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct term.

### Options
- [x] A) globalization
- [ ] B) isolation
- [ ] C) protection
- [ ] D) migration

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Globalization** (globalización) is the term for the increasing connectivity of the world's economies and cultures.

Vocabulary.

### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) multinational
- [ ] B) local

- [ ] D) silent

### Explicación Pedagógica

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

Reading comprehension.
"The 'Digital Divide' refers to the gap between those who have easy access to the internet and those who do not. Although globalization has been accelerating technological spread, this divide remains a significant barrier to equal opportunity."

### Enunciado
What is the 'Digital Divide' according to the text?

### Options
- [x] A) The inequality in internet access between different groups.
- [ ] B) The speed at which globalization is accelerating.
- [ ] C) A new type of computer designed for developing countries.
- [ ] D) The geographical distance between two technological hubs.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text defines it as the "**gap** between those who have easy access... and those who do not".
