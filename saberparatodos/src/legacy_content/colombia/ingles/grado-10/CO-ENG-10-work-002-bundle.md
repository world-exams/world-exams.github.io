## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-work-002-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary.
"The moral principles that govern a person's behavior in a professional environment are professional **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct term.

### Options
- [x] A) ethics
- [ ] B) clothes
- [ ] C) machines
- [ ] D) holidays

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Professional ethics** (ética profesional) refers to the conduct expected of people in a work setting.

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-work-002-v3`

### Contexto
Second Conditional review (Condition).
"If I **________** (see) a coworker stealing from the office, I would report it."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct past tense form for an imaginary condition.

### Options
- [x] A) saw
- [ ] B) see
- [ ] C) would see
- [ ] D) have seen

### Explicación Pedagógica
The Second Conditional use the **Simple Past** in the "if" clause.

Second Conditional review (Result).
"If the company were unethical, I **________** for them."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct negative result form.

### Options
- [x] A) wouldn't work

### Contexto
Ethical dilemma.
"If a client offered me a bribe (illegal money), I **________** (refuse) it immediately."

### Enunciado
Complete the second conditional sentence.

### Options
- [x] A) would refuse
- [ ] B) will refuse
- [ ] C) refused
- [ ] D) refuse

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Would refuse** (rechazaría) is the correct result for an imaginary situation.

## Question 6 (Media B - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-work-002-v6`

### Contexto
Hypothetical scenario.
"If you **________** (be) the manager, how would you handle a liar on the team?"

### Enunciado
Choose the correct form of \'be\' for a formal imaginary condition.

### Options
- [x] A) were
- [ ] B) was
- [ ] C) am
- [ ] D) has been

### Explicación Pedagógica
In the second conditional, "**were**" is preferred for all subjects in formal English.

Workplace values.
"If employees **________** (treat) with respect, they would be more productive."

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-work-002-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"A 'whistleblower' is someone who reports illegal or unethical activities within their organization. While they often face retaliation, their actions are crucial for corporate accountability. If whistleblowers didn't exist, many scandals would remain hidden."

### Enunciado
According to the text, what is the importance of a whistleblower?

### Options
- [x] A) They help hold corporations accountable for their actions.
- [ ] B) They help the company hide scandals from the public.
- [ ] C) They receive large rewards from their organizations.
- [ ] D) They are responsible for illegal activities in the office.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text states they are "**crucial for corporate accountability**" (responsabilidad corporativa).
