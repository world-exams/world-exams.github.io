## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-science-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"The patient was gave the wrong medicine."

### Enunciado
What is the error in this passive sentence?

### Options
- [x] A) It should be 'was given'. We must use the past participle of 'give'.
- [ ] B) It should be 'were gave'.
- [ ] C) 'Patient' should be 'Doctor'.
- [ ] D) There is no error.

### Explicación Pedagógica
In Passive Voice, we **always** use the **past participle** (3rd column: give - gave - **given**).
