### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) had my portrait painted

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

They also get their online portfolios managed by web designers. This allows them to focus on creating rather than technical maintenance."

### Enunciado
Why do artists have their portfolios managed by designers?

### Options
- [x] A) To focus on creating rather than maintenance.
- [ ] B) Because they don't like art.
- [ ] C) Because canvas is expensive.
- [ ] D) To stop creating art.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text concludes: "This allows them to **focus on creating rather than technical maintenance**."
