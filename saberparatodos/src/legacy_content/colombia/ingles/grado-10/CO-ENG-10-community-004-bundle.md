## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-community-004-v3`

### Contexto
Mixed Conditional (Past Cause -> Present Result).
"If I **________** (study) harder at school, I **________** (have) a better job now."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct mixed conditional forms.

### Options
- [x] A) had studied / would have
- [ ] B) studied / would have
- [ ] C) had studied / would have had
- [ ] D) study / will have

### Explicación Pedagógica
Past condition (3rd conditional if-clause: had studied) -> Present consequence (2nd conditional result: would have).

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-community-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"richer / have / were / would / I / traveled / if / I / ."

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a mixed conditional.

### Options
- [x] A) If I were richer, I would have traveled.
- [ ] B) I would have traveled if were I richer.
- [ ] C) If I richer were, I traveled would have.
- [ ] D) Were I richer if, I would have traveled.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **If + subject + past simple (state), subject + would have + past participle (past result).**
