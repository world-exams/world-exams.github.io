### Enunciado
What is a **Digital Nomad**?

### Options
- [x] A) A person who works remotely and travels to different locations while using technology.
- [ ] B) A person who only works with ancient books and never uses a computer.
- [ ] C) A special type of robot that cleans the floors of a very large office.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-work-001-v9`

### Contexto
Analyzing a situation of "Upskilling".
"Juan is a traditional graphic designer, but he decides to take a six-month course in User Experience (UX) Design to stay relevant in the digital market."

### Enunciado
What is Juan doing?

### Options
- [x] A) Upskilling (learning new skills to improve his career prospects).
- [ ] B) Downsizing (reducing the size of his company to save money).
- [ ] C) Privatizing (selling a public service to a private company).
- [ ] D) Subsidizing (paying for part of the cost of a product for someone else).
