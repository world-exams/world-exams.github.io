## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-economy-001-v1`

Identifying primary economic sectors.

### Enunciado
Which sector of the economy involves providing services like banking, education, and healthcare?

### Options
- [x] A) Tertiary sector
- [ ] B) Primary sector (agriculture)
- [ ] C) Secondary sector (manufacturing)
- [ ] D) Virtual sector

### Explicación Pedagógica
The **tertiary sector** (sector terciario/servicios) focuses on providing intangible goods and services.

### Enunciado
What is the focus of **Micro-economics**?

### Options
- [x] A) The behavior of individual consumers and businesses in making decisions about resources.
- [ ] B) The study of very small insects that live inside computers and calculators.
