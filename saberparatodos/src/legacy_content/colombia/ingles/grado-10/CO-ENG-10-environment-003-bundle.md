## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-environment-003-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Climate change.
"The increase in the Earth's average temperature due to greenhouse gases is called **________** warming."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct adjective.

### Options
- [x] A) global
- [ ] B) local
- [ ] C) cold
- [ ] D) minor

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Global warming** (calentamiento global) refers to the long-term rise in the average temperature of Earth's climate system.

### Contexto
If only (Present emphasis).
"**________** people cared more about the oceans, they wouldn't throw plastic in them!"

### Enunciado
Choose the expression that emphasizes a strong desire for change.

### Options
- [x] A) If only
- [ ] B) If
- [ ] C) Because

**If only** is used like 'wish' but it is more emphatic. It also takes the **Past Simple** for present desires.

Wish (Ability in the present).
"I wish I **________** (can) stop the polar ice from melting."

### Enunciado

### Options

## Question 6 (Media B - Difficulty 3)

### Contexto
Wish (Behavior of others - would).
"I wish factories **________** (stop) polluting the rivers."

### Enunciado
Choose the form used when we are annoyed with someone else's behavior and want it to change.

### Options
- [x] A) would stop
- [ ] B) stopped
- [ ] C) stop
- [ ] D) will stop

We use **wish + would + verb** to express dissatisfaction with an action or situation that someone else is doing and we want to change.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

Reading comprehension.
"Climate activists argue that if only governments had signed the agreement years ago, we would not be facing such extreme temperatures now. They wish that global corporations would prioritize sustainability over profit. It is a critical moment; if we don't act, future generations will wish we had done more."

### Enunciado
According to the text, what is a current regret of the activists?

### Options
- [x] A) That governments didn't sign the agreement years ago.
- [ ] B) That global corporations are too sustainable.
- [ ] C) That temperatures are not extreme enough.
- [ ] D) That future generations are acting correctly now.

The text says: "**if only governments had signed the agreement years ago**" (expressing a regret about the past).

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-environment-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"I wish it will stop raining soon."

### Enunciado
What is the grammatical error in this wish about the future?

### Options
- [x] A) We use 'would stop' or 'stopped', not 'will stop'. 'Will' is not used after 'wish'.
- [ ] B) 'Raining' should be 'to rain'.
- [ ] C) 'I wish' should be 'I hope'.
- [ ] D) There is no error.

### Explicación Pedagógica
After **wish**, we never use **will**. For future desires/changes, we use **would** or **could**. (Note: 'I hope' is used for likely future events).
