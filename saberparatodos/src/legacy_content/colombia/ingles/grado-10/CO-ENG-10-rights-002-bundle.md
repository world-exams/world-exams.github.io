### Options

- [ ] B) Divided

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-rights-002-v3`

### Contexto
Modals of Deduction (Must).
"The prison is very quiet and empty. The prisoners **________** have been moved to another location."

### Enunciado
Choose the modal that expresses a strong logical conclusion (90-100% certainty).

### Options
- [x] A) must
- [ ] B) might
- [ ] C) can't
- [ ] D) should

### Explicación Pedagógica
"**Must**" is used when we are almost certain that something is true based on evidence.
