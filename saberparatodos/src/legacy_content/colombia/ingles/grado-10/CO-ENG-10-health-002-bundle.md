## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-health-002-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary.
"A lifestyle where you sit a lot and don't exercise is called a **________** lifestyle."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct adjective.

### Options
- [x] A) sedentary
- [ ] B) secondary
- [ ] C) active
- [ ] D) mental

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **sedentary** (sedentario) lifestyle involves little to no physical activity.

First Conditional review.
"If you **________** (eat) too much processed sugar, your risk of diabetes will increase."

### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) eat
- [ ] B) will eat
- [ ] C) ate
- [ ] D) eating

### Explicación Pedagógica
In the First Conditional (real future), we use the **Simple Present** after "if".

Unless (Negative if).
"**________** people change their digital habits, the cases of 'tech neck' will increase."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct linker meaning "if they don't".

### Options
- [x] A) Unless
- [ ] B) If

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-health-002-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Non-communicable diseases (NCDs), such as heart disease and cancer, are the leading cause of death globally. Most NCDs are preventable. If we adopt healthier diets and increase physical activity, a significant proportion of these deaths will be avoided."

### Enunciado
What is the main message regarding NCDs?

### Options
- [x] A) They are largely preventable through lifestyle changes.
- [ ] B) They are contagious and spread rapidly in schools.
- [ ] C) There is no way to avoid them regardless of our diet.
- [ ] D) They are only found in older people in small towns.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text states that "**Most NCDs are preventable**" through diet and activity.
