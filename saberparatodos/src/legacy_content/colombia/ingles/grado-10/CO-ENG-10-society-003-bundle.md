### Enunciado
Choose the correct word.

### Options
- [x] A) equality
- [ ] B) division
- [ ] C) silence

## Question 5 (Media A - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-society-003-v5`

### Contexto
Reported Questions (Wh- questions).
"Where is the meeting?" she asked.
She asked me where the meeting **________**.

### Enunciado
Choose the correct verb form and word order for the reported question.

### Options
- [x] A) was
- [ ] B) is
- [ ] C) were
- [ ] D) will be

### Explicación Pedagógica
Reported questions use **statement word order** (subject before verb) and move one step back in time (is -> **was**).

Juan asked if I **________** (support) the movement.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

### Contexto
Reported Questions (Time and Place changes).
"What did you do here yesterday?" the officer asked.
The officer asked me what I had done **________** the day before.

### Enunciado
Choose the correct transformation for 'here'.

### Options
- [x] A) there
- [ ] B) here
- [ ] C) now
- [ ] D) near

## Question 8 (Difícil B - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-society-003-v8`

### Contexto
Reported Questions (Wh- Questions with 'do').
"How do you protect your rights?" she asked.
She asked me how I **________** my rights.

### Enunciado
Choose the correct reported form.

### Options
- [x] A) protected
- [ ] B) do protected
- [ ] C) did I protect
- [ ] D) have protected

In reported questions, we **remove** the auxiliary 'do/does/did' and change the main verb to the past.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-society-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"He asked me where did I live."

### Enunciado
What is the error in this reported question?

### Options
- [x] A) It uses question word order (did I live). It should be statement word order (I lived).
- [ ] B) 'Where' should be 'Who'.
- [ ] C) 'Asked' should be 'said'.
- [ ] D) There is no error.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Reported questions **do not** use inversion or auxiliaries like 'do/did'. Correct: "He asked me where **I lived**."
