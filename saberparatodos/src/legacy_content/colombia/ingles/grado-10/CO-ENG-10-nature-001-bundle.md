### Enunciado
What is the name of the international treaty designed to limit global warming to well below 2 degrees Celsius?

### Options
- [x] A) The Paris Agreement
- [ ] B) The FIFA World Cup
- [ ] C) The Treaty of London
- [ ] D) The Olympic Charter

### Explicación Pedagógica
The **Paris Agreement** (Acuerdo de París) is the primary global effort to combat climate change.

### Options
- [x] A) Paying for projects that reduce greenhouse gas emissions elsewhere to balance your own emissions.
- [ ] B) Painting all the trees in a forest with a special green glue to make them look brighter.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-nature-001-v10`

### Contexto
The concept of "Ecological Integrity".

### Enunciado
What does it mean for an ecosystem to have **Ecological Integrity**?

### Options
- [x] A) It functions normally, supports its native species, and resists disturbances.
- [ ] B) It contains many different types of plastic and metal objects on the ground.
- [ ] C) It has zero animals and only consists of very tall and dry grass.
- [ ] D) It is managed by robots that fly and clean the air every 15 minutes.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Ecological integrity** (integridad ecológica) is the condition of an ecosystem where structure and function are unimpaired by human stresses.
