## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-society-002-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary.
"The existence of a variety of cultural or ethnic groups within a society is cultural **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct term.

### Options
- [x] A) diversity
- [ ] B) unity
- [ ] C) history
- [ ] D) property

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Cultural diversity** (diversidad cultural) refers to the range of different cultures in a group.

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-society-002-v3`

### Contexto
Used to (Past States).
"People **________** (be) less aware of cultural sensitivity in the past."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct phrase for a past state.

### Options
- [x] A) used to be
- [ ] B) would be
- [ ] C) were used to be
- [ ] D) are used to be

### Explicación Pedagógica
"**Used to**" is used for past states (like being) that have changed or no longer exist.

### Contexto
Would (Past Habits).
"During the festivals, the villagers **________** (dress) in their traditional costumes and dance."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct word for a repeated action in the past.

### Options
- [x] A) would
- [ ] B) should
- [ ] C) used to be

## Question 5 (Media A - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-society-002-v5`

### Contexto
Comparison of Habits.
"In the old days, people **________** (spend) hours listening to stories from the elders, but they **________** (not / have) the internet."

### Enunciado
Choose the best combination for a habit and a state.

### Options
- [x] A) would spend / didn't use to have
- [ ] B) would spend / wouldn't have
- [ ] C) used to spend / wouldn't have
- [ ] D) would spend / didn't owned

### Explicación Pedagógica
"**Would spend**" describes the repeated action; "**didn't use to have**" describes the past state/lack.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-society-002-v7`

### Contexto
State vs. Action Check.
"Which of these sentences is **grammatically incorrect**?"

### Enunciado
Identify the improper use of \'would\' for a state.

### Options
- [x] A) My grandfather would love traditional music when he was young.
- [ ] B) My grandfather used to love traditional music when he was young.
- [ ] C) My grandfather would play traditional music every night.
- [ ] D) My grandfather used to play traditional music every night.

### Explicación Pedagógica
"**Would**" cannot be used to describe past **states** (feelings like "love"); only "used to" works for states.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-society-002-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Cultural appropriation is the adoption of an element or elements of one culture or identity by members of another culture or identity. This can be controversial when members of a dominant culture appropriate from disadvantaged minority cultures without understanding the context or history."

### Enunciado
Why is cultural appropriation often considered controversial?

### Options
- [x] A) Because it involves a dominant culture taking elements without understanding their history.
- [ ] B) Because it promotes deep understanding and mutual respect between all groups.
- [ ] C) Because minority cultures always want people to take their traditions.
- [ ] D) Because it is the only way to save disappearing languages in the world.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text explains that the lack of understanding of "**context or history**" makes it controversial.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-society-002-v10`

### Contexto
History and Change.
"Before globalization, most people **________** (know) very little about the cultures of distant continents."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct form to describe a past state.

### Options
- [x] A) used to know
- [ ] B) would know
- [ ] C) were knowing
- [ ] D) known

### Explicación Pedagógica
"Know" is a stative verb; we must use "**used to**" and not "would" for past states.
