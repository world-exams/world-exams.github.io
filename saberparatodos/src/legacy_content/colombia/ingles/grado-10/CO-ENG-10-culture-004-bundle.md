## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-culture-004-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Globalization has brought cultures closer, but some fear the loss of local traditions. Many young people wish they knew more about their ancestors' customs. If only schools prioritized local history as much as global events, we might preserve our heritage better. It is crucial to find a balance between being a global citizen and maintaining our roots."

### Enunciado
What do many young people wish?

### Options
- [x] A) They wish they knew more about their ancestors' customs.
- [ ] B) They wish they could forget their roots.
- [ ] C) They wish schools stopped teaching history.
- [ ] D) They wish globalization stopped completely.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text states: "Many young people **wish they knew more about their ancestors' customs**."

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-culture-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"listened / only / teacher / if / had / I / the / to / !"

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a regret.

### Options
- [x] A) If only I had listened to the teacher!
- [ ] B) I had listened to the teacher if only!
- [ ] C) If only listened I had to the teacher!
- [ ] D) To the teacher if only I had listened!

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **If only + subject + past perfect + object.**
