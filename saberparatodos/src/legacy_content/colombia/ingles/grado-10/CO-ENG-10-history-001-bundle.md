"Before the peace treaty was finally signed, the two nations **________ ________ ________** over the border for nearly a decade."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct past perfect continuous form for a persistent struggle.

### Options
- [x] A) had been fighting
- [ ] B) was being failed
- [ ] C) is been winning
- [ ] D) did was working

### Explicación Pedagógica
