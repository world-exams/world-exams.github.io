## Question 5 (Media A - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-arts-003-v5`

### Contexto
Defining Relative Clauses (Removing the pronoun).
"In which sentence can we **omit** (remove) the relative pronoun?"

### Enunciado
Select the sentence where the pronoun can be removed.

### Options
- [x] A) The movie (that) I like most is 'Inception'.
- [ ] B) The actor who lives in Hollywood is famous.
- [ ] C) The director who won the award is young.
- [ ] D) The camera which was expensive is broken.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The relative pronoun can be omitted when it is the **object** of the relative clause (I like the movie). If it is the subject (The actor lives...), it cannot be removed.

## Question 8 (Difícil B - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-arts-003-v8`

### Contexto
Relative Clauses (Prepositions).
"The studio **________** she works for is very famous in Europe."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pronoun (formal) or structure. (Formal: for which / Informal: that ... for).

### Options
- [x] A) that
- [ ] B) who
- [ ] C) whose
- [ ] D) where

### Explicación Pedagógica
In "The studio **that** she works for...", 'that' refers to the studio. If it were formal: "The studio **for which** she works...".

Scriptwriters, whose job is often overlooked, create the foundation of these powerful messages. A film that succeeds at the box office is not always the most artistically valuable, but it is the one which the industry celebrates most."

According to the text, what is said about scriptwriters?

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-arts-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"I saw the movie who you recommended."

### Enunciado
What is the error in this relative clause?

### Options
- [x] A) 'Who' is only for people. 'That' or 'Which' should be used for a movie.
- [ ] B) 'Recommended' should be 'recommend'.
- [ ] C) 'Movie' should be 'Cinema'.
- [ ] D) There is no error.

### Explicación Pedagógica
We use **who** for people and **which/that** for things (movies). Correct: "the movie **that** you recommended."
