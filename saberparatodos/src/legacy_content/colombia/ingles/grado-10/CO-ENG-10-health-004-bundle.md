### Options
- [x] A) anxiety
- [ ] B) happiness

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-health-004-v3`

### Contexto
Reported Speech (Wh- Questions).
Direct: "Where do you live?"
Reported: "She asked me where **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct word order.

### Options
- [x] A) I lived
- [ ] B) do I live
- [ ] C) did I live
- [ ] D) I live

### Explicación Pedagógica

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-health-004-v7`

### Contexto
Reported Speech (Tense change - Past to Past Perfect).
Direct: "Why did you call?"
Reported: "He asked me why I **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct backshift.

### Options
- [x] A) had called
- [ ] B) called
- [ ] C) have called
- [ ] D) was calling

### Explicación Pedagógica
Past Simple (did call) usually changes to **Past Perfect** (had called) in reported speech.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-health-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"asked / if / I / help / needed / me / she / ."

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a reported question.

### Options
- [x] A) She asked me if I needed help.
- [ ] B) She asked if me I needed help.
- [ ] C) If I needed help she asked me.
- [ ] D) Me she asked if needed I help.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **Subject + asked + object + if + subject + verb.**
