## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-science-002-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence correction.
"The experiment have been done by the students yesterday."

### Enunciado
What is the error in the sentence?

### Options
- [x] A) \'have\' should be \'was\' because of \'yesterday\' and singular subject.
- [ ] B) \'done\' should be \'do\'.
- [ ] C) \'by\' should be \'for\'.
- [ ] D) \'the students\' should be \'the student\'s\'.

### Explicación Pedagógica
For a specific past time (**yesterday**), we use the **Simple Past Passive** (was done), not the present perfect passive.
