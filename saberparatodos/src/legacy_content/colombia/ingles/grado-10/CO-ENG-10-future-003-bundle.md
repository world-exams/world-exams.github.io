## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-future-003-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Energy sources.
"Energy that comes from natural sources like sunlight, wind, and water is called **________** energy."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct adjective.

### Options
- [x] A) renewable
- [ ] B) fossil
- [ ] C) dark
- [ ] D) static

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Renewable** (renovable) energy is collected from resources which are naturally replenished on a human timescale.

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-future-003-v3`

### Contexto
Future Continuous (Actions in progress at a specific future time).
"This time next year, many cities **________** (use) more wind energy than ever before."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct future continuous form.

### Options
- [x] A) will be using
- [ ] B) will have used
- [ ] C) are using
- [ ] D) will use

### Explicación Pedagógica
The **Future Continuous** (will be + verb-ing) describes actions that will be in progress at a particular moment in the future.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-future-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"She will have been finishing her project by 5 PM."

### Enunciado
What is the structural issue if the speaker wants to emphasize completion?

### Options
- [x] A) 'Will have been finishing' is Future Perfect Continuous (ongoing). It should be 'Will have finished' (Future Perfect) for completion.
- [ ] B) 'Project' should be 'Homework'.
- [ ] C) It should be 'will has finished'.
- [ ] D) There is no error.

### Explicación Pedagógica
To emphasize that an action is **done** by a specific time, use **Future Perfect** (will have + past participle).
