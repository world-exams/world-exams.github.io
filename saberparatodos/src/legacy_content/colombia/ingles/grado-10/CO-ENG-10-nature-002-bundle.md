## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-nature-002-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"The 'Circular Economy' aims to eliminate waste by keeps products and materials in use for as long as possible. Under this model, items are designed to be repaired, reused, or recycled rather than discarded. This approach could significantly lower the human impact on the ecosystem."

### Enunciado
According to the text, what is a key goal of the 'Circular Economy'?

### Options
- [x] A) Designing products to be reused or recycled to eliminate waste.
- [ ] B) Producing as many disposable products as possible for convenience.
- [ ] C) Increasing the extraction of new raw materials from the earth.
- [ ] D) Encouraging consumers to discard items after a single use.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text states the goal is to "**eliminate waste**" by designing items to be "**repaired, reused, or recycled**".
