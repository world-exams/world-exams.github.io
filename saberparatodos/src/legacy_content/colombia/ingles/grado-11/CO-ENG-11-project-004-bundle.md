## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-project-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"not / only / smart / is / she / hardworking / but / also / ."

### Enunciado
Order the words to form an inverted sentence.

### Options
- [x] A) Not only is she smart but also hardworking.
- [ ] B) She is smart not only but also hardworking.
- [ ] C) Not only she is smart but also hardworking.
- [ ] D) Smart she is not only but also hardworking.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **Not only + is + subject + adjective + but + also + adjective.**
