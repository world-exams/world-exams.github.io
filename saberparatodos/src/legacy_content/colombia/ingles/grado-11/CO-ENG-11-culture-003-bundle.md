## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-culture-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar/Coherence check.
"Globalization has benefits. Moreover, it has drawbacks. Therefore, we must find a balance."

### Enunciado
What is the coherence issue?

### Options
- [x] A) 'Moreover' adds supporting info, but 'drawbacks' contrasts. It should be 'However' or 'On the other hand'.
- [ ] B) 'Therefore' is used correctly.
- [ ] C) You cannot discuss benefits and drawbacks together.
- [ ] D) There is no issue.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Moreover** adds similar information. Since 'drawbacks' contrasts with 'benefits', a contrastive marker like **However** or **On the other hand** is needed.
