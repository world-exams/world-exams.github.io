### Enunciado
What is **Ecosystem Restoration**?

### Options
- [x] A) The process of assisting the recovery of an ecosystem that has been degraded, damaged, or destroyed.
- [ ] B) A special prize given to the person who can name 500 different types of flowers in 30 minutes.
- [ ] C) A machine that automatically turns old newspapers into green paint for city buildings.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-nature-001-v10`

### Contexto
The concept of "The Rights of Nature".

### Enunciado
What does it mean for a country (like Ecuador or Bolivia) to recognize **The Rights of Nature**?

### Options
- [x] A) That ecosystems have their own legal rights to exist, persist, maintain, and regenerate their vital cycles.
- [ ] B) That every tree in the country must have its own bank account and pay taxes to the government.
- [ ] C) That animals are allowed to vote in national elections and choose the next president.
- [ ] D) That humans are not allowed to use any type of electricity or technology for 100 years.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Recognizing **legal rights for nature** (derechos de la naturaleza) shifts from human-centered to nature-centered law.
