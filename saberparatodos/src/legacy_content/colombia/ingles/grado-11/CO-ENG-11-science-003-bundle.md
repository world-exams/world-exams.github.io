## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-science-003-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"The race to explore space is intensifying. By 2040, several nations will have sent crewed missions to Mars. While governments are investing heavily, private companies like SpaceX are going to play an even bigger role. Scientists predict that by the end of the century, humanity will have been living in space for decades. However, the ethical questions surrounding space colonization are only just beginning to be debated."

### Enunciado
What will have happened by 2040 according to the text?

### Options
- [x] A) Several nations will have sent crewed missions to Mars.
- [ ] B) Humanity will have been living in space for decades.
- [ ] C) Space colonization debates will be finished.
- [ ] D) Private companies will have disappeared.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text says: "**By 2040**, several nations **will have sent** crewed missions to Mars."

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-science-003-v10`

### Contexto
Error correction.
"By next year, we will have been launched three satellites."

### Enunciado
What is the error?

### Options
- [x] A) It mixes Future Perfect Continuous (will have been) with a past participle (launched). It should be either 'will have launched' (FP) or 'will have been launching' (FPC).
- [ ] B) 'Satellites' should be singular.
- [ ] C) 'Next year' should be 'Last year'.
- [ ] D) There is no error.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Will have launched** (completion) or **will have been launching** (duration). You cannot mix the two forms.
