### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) renewable
- [ ] B) reliable

- [ ] D) removable

### Explicación Pedagógica

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-nature-002-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Sustainable development is defined as meeting the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. This involves balancing economic growth, environmental care, and social well-being. If we continue to deplete resources at the current rate, future generations will suffer severe shortages."

### Enunciado
What is the core definition of 'sustainable development' in the text?

### Options
- [x] A) Meeting current needs without harming the future's ability to meet theirs.
- [ ] B) Stopping all economic growth to protect every tree.
- [ ] C) Using all available resources now to maximize current wealth.
- [ ] D) Developing new technologies to travel to other planets.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text defines it as "meeting the needs of the present **without compromising... future generations**".

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-nature-002-v10`

### Contexto
Double Future.
"By 2050, we **________** (live) in a world where renewable energy **________** (become) the norm."

### Enunciado
Choose the combination of Future Continuous and Future Perfect.

### Options
- [x] A) will be living / will have become
- [ ] B) will live / will become
- [ ] C) are living / has become
- [ ] D) will have lived / becomes

### Explicación Pedagógica
Process in progress (**will be living**) + Completed change (**will have become**).
