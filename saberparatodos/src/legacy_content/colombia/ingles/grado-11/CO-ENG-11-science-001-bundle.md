## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-science-001-v10`

### Contexto
The concept of "Artificial General Intelligence" (AGI).

### Enunciado
How does **AGI** differ from current AI (Narrow AI)?

### Options
- [x] A) AGI would be able to understand, learn, and apply knowledge across a wide range of tasks at a human level.
- [ ] B) AGI only works when it is connected to a very large battery made of potatoes.
- [ ] C) AGI is a type of AI that can only be used by people who are over 100 years old.
- [ ] D) AGI is the official name for a computer that can only play chess and nothing else.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**AGI** (IAG - Inteligencia Artificial General) is the theoretical capacity of an agent to learn any intellectual task a human can.
