### Options

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-history-004-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Historians often disagree on the causes of major events. While some attribute the revolution to economic hardship, others insist on political oppression as the catalyst. One prominent scholar admitted having overlooked the role of social media in modern uprisings. She apologized for underestimating its power."

### Enunciado
What did the prominent scholar admit?

### Options
- [x] A) Having overlooked the role of social media.
- [ ] B) Having started the revolution.
- [ ] C) Being wrong about economics.
- [ ] D) Nothing.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text states: "One prominent scholar **admitted having overlooked the role of social media**."

### Options
