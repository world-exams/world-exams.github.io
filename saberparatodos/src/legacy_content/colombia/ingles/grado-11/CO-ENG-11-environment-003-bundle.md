## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-environment-003-v3`

### Contexto
Mixed Conditional Type 1: Past condition → Present result.
"If the government **had invested** in renewable energy ten years ago, we **________** (not / depend) so much on fossil fuels today."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct present result form for a past hypothetical condition.

### Options
- [x] A) wouldn't depend
- [ ] B) wouldn't have depended
- [ ] C) won't depend
- [ ] D) didn't depend

### Explicación Pedagógica
Mixed Conditional (Past → Present): **If + Past Perfect, ... would + base verb** (present result).

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-environment-003-v7`

### Contexto
Identifying the Mixed Conditional type.
"If I spoke Mandarin, I would have negotiated the trade deal myself."

### Enunciado
What type of mixed conditional is this?

### Options
- [x] A) Present condition → Past result (I don't speak Mandarin now, so I didn't negotiate then).
- [ ] B) Past condition → Present result.
- [ ] C) Third Conditional (pure past).
- [ ] D) Second Conditional (pure present hypothesis).

### Explicación Pedagógica
The 'if' clause uses **Past Simple** (present hypothesis) and the result uses **would have + past participle** (past result).

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-environment-003-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Economists argue that if governments had taxed carbon emissions decades ago, the transition to green energy would be nearly complete now. Conversely, if consumers were truly committed to sustainability, they would have boycotted fast fashion years ago. The reality is that both producers and consumers share responsibility. Some experts believe that if international trade agreements prioritized environmental standards, poorer nations wouldn't face as much ecological exploitation today."

### Enunciado
According to the text, what would have happened if consumers were truly committed?

### Options
- [x] A) They would have boycotted fast fashion years ago.
- [ ] B) They would buy more cheap clothes.
- [ ] C) They would stop caring about the economy.
- [ ] D) They would have increased carbon emissions.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text says: "**if consumers were truly committed...**, they **would have boycotted** fast fashion years ago." (Present → Past mixed conditional).

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-environment-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"If I had studied economics, I would be working in finance now."

### Enunciado
Is this sentence a correct Mixed Conditional?

### Options
- [x] A) Yes. Past condition (had studied) → Present result (would be working). Perfectly correct.
- [ ] B) No, it should be 'would have been working'.
- [ ] C) No, it should be 'If I studied'.
- [ ] D) No, 'would be working' is never used.

### Explicación Pedagógica
This is a correct **Mixed Conditional**: past hypothetical action → present continuous hypothetical result.
