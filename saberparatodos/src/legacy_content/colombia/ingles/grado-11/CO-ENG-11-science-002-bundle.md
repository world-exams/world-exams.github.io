## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-science-002-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary.
"A statement based on evidence that tries to prove a point is an **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct noun.

### Options
- [x] A) argument
- [ ] B) aliment
- [ ] C) apartment
- [ ] D) amusement

### Explicación Pedagógica
In science and logic, an **argument** (argumento) is a reason or set of reasons given with the aim of persuading others.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-science-002-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Peer review is a critical part of the scientific method. Before a study is published, it is evaluated by other experts in the same field to ensure its validity. Despite its importance, the process is often criticized for being slow and prone to bias, as reviewers may reject ideas that challenge established theories."

### Enunciado
What is a criticism of the peer review process mentioned in the text?

### Options
- [x] A) It can be slow and prone to bias against new ideas.
- [ ] B) It allows anyone to publish without any checking.
- [ ] C) It costs too much money for the government.
- [ ] D) It only accepts papers written in Latin.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text states it is "**criticized for being slow and prone to bias**".

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-science-002-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"Despite he studied hard, he failed."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) 'Despite' must be followed by a noun or gerund, not a full clause (subject + verb).
- [ ] B) 'Studied' should be 'studies'.
- [ ] C) 'Failed' is not a verb.
- [ ] D) It is completely correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Correct forms: "Despite investigating hard" OR "Although he studied hard". **Despite** cannot take a full clause directly.
