## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-global-002-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary.
"A person who identifies with being part of an emerging world community is a **________** citizen."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct adjective.

### Options
- [x] A) global
- [ ] B) local
- [ ] C) rural
- [ ] D) private

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Global citizen** refers to someone who feels they belong to the world community.

### Contexto
Third Conditional (Condition).
"If the countries **________** (sign) the treaty, the war would have ended sooner."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct Past Perfect form for the imaginary past condition.

### Options
- [x] A) had signed
- [ ] B) signed
- [ ] C) have signed
- [ ] D) would sign

### Explicación Pedagógica
The Third Conditional uses **Past Perfect** (had + participle) in the "if" clause.

## Question 5 (Media A - Difficulty 3)

Negative Third Conditional.
"If we **________** (not / ignore) the warning signs, the crisis **________** (not / happen)."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct negative forms.

### Options
- [x] A) hadn't ignored / wouldn't have happened
- [ ] B) didn't ignore / won't happen
- [ ] C) haven't ignored / wouldn't happen
- [ ] D) wouldn't ignore / hadn't happened

Condition: **hadn't + participle**; Result: **wouldn't have + participle**.

Inversion (Formal).
"**________** I known about the pollution, I would have acted differently."

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-global-002-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Global citizenship entails not just awareness but action. Critics argue that it undermines national sovereignty, while proponents believe it is essential for tackling transnational issues like climate change. If nations had prioritized global cooperation earlier, many of today's environmental disasters could have been mitigated."

### Enunciado
What is one argument AGAINST global citizenship mentioned in the text?

### Options
- [x] A) It undermines national sovereignty.
- [ ] B) It helps tackle climate change.
- [ ] C) It mitigates environmental disasters.
- [ ] D) It promotes international travel.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text states: "Critics argue that it **undermines national sovereignty**".

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-global-002-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"If he would have asked me, I would have helped him."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect in standard English?

### Options
- [x] A) Use 'had asked' in the condition, not 'would have asked'.
- [ ] B) Use 'will help' in the result.
- [ ] C) 'Asked' should be 'ask'.
- [ ] D) It is completely correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
In the "if" clause of the third conditional, use **Past Perfect** (had asked), never "would have".
