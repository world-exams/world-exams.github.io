## Question 8 (Difícil B - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-history-003-v8`

### Contexto
Third Conditional vs Mixed Conditional.
Sentence A: "If they had signed the treaty, the war would have ended." (3rd)
Sentence B: "If they had signed the treaty, we would be at peace now." (Mixed)

### Enunciado
What is the key difference between these two sentences?

### Options
- [x] A) Sentence A has a past result (would have ended); Sentence B has a present result (would be).
- [ ] B) Both have past results.
- [ ] C) Both have present results.
- [ ] D) They are identical in meaning.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Third Conditional = past result. Mixed Conditional (past → present) = present result.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-history-003-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Historians often speculate about alternative timelines. If the Roman Empire had not fallen, European political structures might have developed very differently. If the printing press had been invented centuries earlier, the spread of knowledge could have accelerated the Scientific Revolution. These counterfactual scenarios, while impossible to prove, help us understand how pivotal historical events have shaped the modern world."

### Enunciado
What does the text suggest about the printing press?

### Options
- [x] A) If it had been invented earlier, the Scientific Revolution could have been accelerated.
- [ ] B) It was invented too early and slowed progress.
- [ ] C) It had no impact on the spread of knowledge.
- [ ] D) It caused the fall of the Roman Empire.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text says: "If the printing press **had been** invented... knowledge **could have accelerated**...".

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-history-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"If they would have known about the crisis, they would have acted differently."

### Enunciado
What is the common error?

### Options
- [x] A) 'Would have' cannot go in the 'if' clause. It should be 'If they had known...'
- [ ] B) 'Acted' should be 'act'.
- [ ] C) 'Differently' should be 'different'.
- [ ] D) There is no error.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The 'if' clause of a Third Conditional **never** takes 'would'. Correct: **If + had + past participle**.
