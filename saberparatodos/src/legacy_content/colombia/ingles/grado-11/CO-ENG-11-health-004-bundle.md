### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) take

### Explicación Pedagógica

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-health-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"demand / truth / the / told / he / be / that / I / ."

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a sentence with the subjunctive.

### Options
- [x] A) I demand that he be told the truth.
- [ ] B) I demand that the truth be told he.
- [ ] C) That he be told the truth I demand.
- [ ] D) He be told the truth that I demand.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **Subject + verb + that + subject + be + past participle (passive).**
