## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-tech-004-v9`

Reading comprehension.
"We often blame the machines for errors. However, it is the human programmers who introduce the bias. What we truly need to address is the data quality. It was only after the audit that the flaws were discovered. All that AI does is mirror our own imperfections."

### Enunciado
Who is responsible for introducing bias?

### Options
- [x] A) It is the human programmers who introduce the bias.
- [ ] B) It is the machines themselves.

- [ ] D) No one is responsible.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-tech-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"happened / that / was / it / yesterday / ."

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a cleft sentence focusing on time.

### Options
- [x] A) It was yesterday that it happened.
- [ ] B) That it happened was it yesterday.
- [ ] C) Yesterday it was that happened it.
- [ ] D) It happened that it was yesterday.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **It was + [time] + that + [event].**
