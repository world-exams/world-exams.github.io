What type of power is Country A exercising?

### Options
- [x] A) Soft Power (persuasion through culture and values).

### Enunciado
What is the primary characteristic of **Realpolitik** in international relations?

### Options
- [x] A) Basing political decisions on practical and material factors (power) rather than on ideological or moral premises.
- [ ] B) Basing every single decision on whether the president had a good dream the night before.
- [ ] C) Always telling the complete truth to all other nations even if it causes a war.
