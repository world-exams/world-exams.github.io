## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-identity-001-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying primary cultural concepts.

### Enunciado
What is **Multiculturalism**?

### Options
- [x] A) The presence of, or support for the presence of, several distinct cultural or ethnic groups within a society.
- [ ] B) A special type of mathematical rule used to calculate the area of a circle in different countries.
- [ ] C) The art of building very large and modern houses using only recycled plastic and paper.
- [ ] D) A historical document that explains how to plant apple trees in the 18th century.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Multiculturalism** (multiculturalismo) promotes coexistence and respect between diverse cultures.

## Question 5 (Media A - Difficulty 3)

Using the present perfect continuous for cultural shifts.
"Societies around the world **________ ________ ________** more diverse due to global migration and digital connectivity."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct present perfect continuous for an ongoing change.

### Options
- [x] A) have been becoming
- [ ] B) was being went
- [ ] C) am been gone
- [ ] D) did was working

### Explicación Pedagógica
The **Present Perfect Continuous** ("have been + -ing") describes a process that started in the past and continues to the present.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-identity-001-v9`

### Contexto
Analyzing a situation of "Ethnocentrism".
"An explorer visits a remote community in the Amazon and concludes that they are 'primitive' and 'unintelligent' because they do not use electricity or speak English."

### Enunciado
What attitude is the explorer demonstrating?

### Options
- [x] A) Ethnocentrism (evaluating other cultures according to the standards of one's own culture).
- [ ] B) Relativism (the idea that a person's beliefs and activities should be understood in their own context).
- [ ] C) Professionalism (doing a job with a high level of skill and respect).
- [ ] D) Optimization (making the best or most effective use of a situation or resource).

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Ethnocentrism** (etnocentrismo) prevents a true understanding of cultural diversity.

### Enunciado
How can a person demonstrate the values of **Global Citizenship**?

### Options
- [x] A) By being aware of and understanding the wider world—and their place in it, taking active roles in their community and work to make our planet more equal and sustainable.
- [ ] B) By buying everything they see on the internet and ignoring the social and environmental cost of production.
