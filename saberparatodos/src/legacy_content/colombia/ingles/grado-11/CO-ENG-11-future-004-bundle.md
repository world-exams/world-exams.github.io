## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-future-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"up / came / solution / with / she / a / brilliant / ."

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a sentence with a 3-part phrasal verb.

### Options
- [x] A) She came up with a brilliant solution.
- [ ] B) She with a brilliant solution came up.
- [ ] C) Came up she with a brilliant solution.
- [ ] D) A brilliant solution she came up with.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **Subject + came up with (invented) + object.**
