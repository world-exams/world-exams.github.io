## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-history-001-v9`

### Contexto
Analyzing a situation of "Hegemony".
"Country Z possesses the strongest economy, the most powerful military, and its culture is dominant across every continent, allowing it to set the global agenda."

### Enunciado
What word best describes Country Z's position?

### Options
- [x] A) Hegemony (political, economic, or military predominance of one state over others).
- [ ] B) Isolationism (a policy of remaining apart from the affairs or interests of other groups).
- [ ] C) Pacifism (the belief that any violence, including war, is unjustifiable under any circumstances).
- [ ] D) Feudalism (a social system that existed in Europe during the Middle Ages).

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Hegemony** (hegemonía) implies both material power and the ability to establish universal norms.

### Enunciado
What did Francis Fukuyama mean by **'The End of History'**?

### Options
- [x] A) The idea that liberal democracy and the market economy might be the final form of human government.
- [ ] B) That everyone in the world would stop writing books about the past starting on January 1st, 2000.
