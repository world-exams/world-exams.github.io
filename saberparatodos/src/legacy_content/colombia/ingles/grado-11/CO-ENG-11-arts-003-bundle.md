## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-arts-003-v3`

### Contexto
Inversion with negative adverbs (Never).
Normal: "I have never read such a powerful book."

### Enunciado
Rewrite with inversion: "**Never** have I **________** such a powerful book."

### Options
- [x] A) read
- [ ] B) reading
- [ ] C) reads
- [ ] D) to read

### Explicación Pedagógica
After **Never** at the start of a sentence, we invert the subject and auxiliary: **Never have I + past participle**.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

Inversion with 'Only when'.
Normal: "I understood the message only when I read the book a second time."

### Enunciado
Complete: "**Only when** I read the book a second time **________** I understand the message."

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-arts-003-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Rarely has a single literary movement influenced global culture as much as Latin American magical realism. Not only did writers like García Márquez redefine storytelling, but they also gave voice to communities that had been historically silenced. Only when readers engage with these texts critically can they appreciate the depth of social commentary embedded within. Never before had fiction served as such a powerful tool for political resistance."

### Enunciado
What does the author claim about magical realism?

### Options
- [x] A) It has rarely been matched in its influence on global culture.
- [ ] B) It has had no influence on fiction.
- [ ] C) Only García Márquez is important.
- [ ] D) Readers should not read these texts critically.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text opens: "**Rarely has** a single literary movement influenced global culture **as much as** Latin American magical realism."

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-arts-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"Never I have seen a performance so moving."

### Enunciado
What is the error?

### Options
- [x] A) Missing inversion. It should be 'Never have I seen...' (auxiliary before subject).
- [ ] B) 'Moving' should be 'moved'.
- [ ] C) 'Performance' should be 'perform'.
- [ ] D) There is no error.

### Explicación Pedagógica
After negative adverbs at the start, the auxiliary verb comes **before** the subject: **Never have I seen**.
