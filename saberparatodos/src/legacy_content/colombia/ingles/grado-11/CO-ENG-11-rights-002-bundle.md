## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-rights-002-v3`

### Contexto
Mixed Conditional (Past Condition -> Present Result).
"If I **________** (study) law, I would be a lawyer now."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct past perfect form for the past condition.

### Options
- [x] A) had studied
- [ ] B) studied
- [ ] C) have studied
- [ ] D) would study

### Explicación Pedagógica
This mixed conditional uses **Past Perfect** in the "if-clause" to refer to a past situation that affects the present.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-rights-002-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Restorative justice focuses on repairing the harm caused by crime rather than just punishing the offender. It involves meetings between the victim, the offender, and the community. If traditional prisons had been effective at rehabilitation, recidivism rates wouldn't be so high today."

### Enunciado
What does the text imply about traditional prisons?

### Options
- [x] A) They have not been effective at rehabilitation, leading to high re-offending rates.
- [ ] B) They are the only way to effectively stop crime permanently.
- [ ] C) They prioritize meetings between victims and offenders.
- [ ] D) They are being replaced entirely by restorative justice in all countries.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The conditional sentence implies that because prisons **weren't effective** (past), rates **are high** (present).

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-rights-002-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"If I had known you were coming, I am baking a cake."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct correction for the result clause.

### Options
- [x] A) would have baked
- [ ] B) will bake
- [ ] C) baked
- [ ] D) have baked

### Explicación Pedagógica
"If I moved known" (Past) requires a past result (**would have baked**) in a standard Third Conditional, or a present result (**would be baking**) in a mixed one. "Am baking" is incorrect. Context suggests the opportunity is past, so **would have baked** fits best.
