## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-community-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"rich / invited / were / would / he / go / to / he / ."

### Enunciado
Order the words to form an inverted conditional.

### Options
- [x] A) Were he invited, he would go.
- [ ] B) He would go were invited he.
- [ ] C) Were invited he to, would go he.
- [ ] D) He were invited, go would he.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **Were + subject + past participle (passive), subject + would + verb.**
