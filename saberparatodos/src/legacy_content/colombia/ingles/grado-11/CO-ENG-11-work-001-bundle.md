## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-work-001-v9`

### Contexto
Analyzing a situation of "Glass Ceiling".
"Despite being the most qualified candidate and having 15 years of experience, a female executive is repeatedly passed over for the CEO position in favor of less qualified male colleagues."

### Enunciado
What social/professional metaphor describes this situation?

### Options
- [x] A) The Glass Ceiling (invisible barriers that prevent marginalized groups from reaching top positions).
- [ ] B) The Open Road (a situation where there are no limits to what you can achieve).
- [ ] C) The Silent Room (a place where no one is allowed to talk or use a phone).
- [ ] D) The Golden Bridge (a way for an enemy to escape so that you don't have to fight them).
