## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-economy-001-v10`

### Contexto
The concept of "The Gig Economy".

### Enunciado
How has globalization and technology impacted the labor market through the **Gig Economy**?

### Options
- [x] A) By creating a market characterized by short-term contracts or freelance work as opposed to permanent jobs.
- [ ] B) By making everyone work for exactly ten minutes and then sleep for twenty hours.
- [ ] C) By forcing every worker to wear a giant green suit made of recycled plastic.
- [ ] D) By deleting the bank accounts of every person who doesn't use a mobile app to work.

### Explicación Pedagógica
