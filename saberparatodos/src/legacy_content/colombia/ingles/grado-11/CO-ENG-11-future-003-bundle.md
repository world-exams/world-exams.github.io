## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-future-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"They must had seen the warning signs."

### Enunciado
What is the error?

### Options
- [x] A) After a modal (must), we use the bare infinitive 'have', not 'had'. Correct: 'must have seen'.
- [ ] B) 'Seen' should be 'saw'.
- [ ] C) 'Warning' should be 'war'.
- [ ] D) There is no error.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Modals are always followed by the **base form**. In perfect modals: **Modal + have + past participle**. Never 'Modal + had'.
