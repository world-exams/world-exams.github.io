## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-issues-001-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying primary global economic divides.

### Enunciado
What does the term **'Global North'** generally refer to?

### Options
- [x] A) Developed countries characterized by high levels of wealth, technology, and economic stability.
- [ ] B) The countries that are located exactly at the North Pole and are covered in ice.
- [ ] C) A type of compass that is used only by pilots when they travel across the ocean.
- [ ] D) A historical document that explains how to build a stone castle in Canada.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The **Global North** (Norte Global) describes socio-economic status rather than just geography.

## Question 2 (Muy Fácil B - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-issues-001-v2`

### Contexto
Defining basic migration terms.

### Enunciado
An **________** is a person who leaves their own country to settle permanently in another.

### Options
- [x] A) emigrant
- [ ] B) architect
- [ ] C) athlete
- [ ] D) editor

### Explicación Pedagógica
An **emigrant** (emigrante) is someone who leaves their country, while an **immigrant** is someone who arrives in a new one.
