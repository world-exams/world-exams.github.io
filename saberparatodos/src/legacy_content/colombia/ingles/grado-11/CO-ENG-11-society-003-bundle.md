### Enunciado
Choose the correct word.

### Options
- [x] A) democracy
- [ ] B) monarchy
- [ ] C) dictatorship
- [ ] D) anarchy

### Explicación Pedagógica

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-society-003-v3`

### Contexto
It-cleft sentence (Basic).
"The mayor proposed the new law." → "**It was** the mayor **who** proposed the new law."

### Enunciado
What part of the original sentence is being emphasized in the cleft sentence?

### Options
- [x] A) The mayor (the person who did it).
- [ ] B) The new law (the object).
- [ ] C) The act of proposing.
- [ ] D) The time it happened.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**It-cleft sentences** (It is/was + focus + who/that...) emphasize a specific element.

## Question 4 (Fácil B - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-society-003-v4`

### Contexto
It-cleft sentence (Object emphasis).
"Citizens demand transparency." → "**It is** transparency **that** citizens demand."

### Enunciado
What element is being emphasized now?

### Options
- [x] A) Transparency (the thing demanded).
- [ ] B) Citizens (the people).
- [ ] C) The act of demanding.
- [ ] D) When they demand it.

### Explicación Pedagógica
By placing 'transparency' after 'It is', we shift the focus to what is demanded.

## Question 5 (Media A - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-society-003-v5`

### Contexto
What-cleft (Pseudo-cleft - Basic).
"People need more political education."

### Enunciado
Complete the what-cleft correctly: "**What** people need **________** more political education."

### Options
- [x] A) is
- [ ] B) are
- [ ] C) were
- [ ] D) has

### Explicación Pedagógica
What-cleft: **What + subject + verb + is/was + focus**. The verb 'is' links the what-clause to the focus.

## Question 6 (Media B - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-society-003-v6`

### Contexto
It-cleft (Time emphasis).
"The constitution was signed in 1991."

### Enunciado
Rewrite emphasizing the time: "It was **________** that the constitution was signed."

### Options
- [x] A) in 1991
- [ ] B) the constitution
- [ ] C) was signed
- [ ] D) it was

### Explicación Pedagógica
The time phrase ('in 1991') goes after 'It was' to emphasize **when** it happened.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-society-003-v7`

### Contexto
What-cleft (Complex).
"The protesters wanted justice for the victims."

### Enunciado
Rewrite as a what-cleft: "What the protesters wanted **________**."

### Options
- [x] A) was justice for the victims
- [ ] B) were justice for the victims
- [ ] C) is justice for the victims
- [ ] D) has justice for the victims

### Explicación Pedagógica
The verb matches the tense of the original sentence: **was** (past).

## Question 8 (Difícil B - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-society-003-v8`

### Contexto
It-cleft (Reason emphasis - why).
"She joined the movement because she believed in equality."

### Enunciado
Complete the it-cleft: "It was because she believed in equality **________** she joined the movement."

### Options

- [ ] B) who

"It was not the politicians who brought about the peace agreement; it was the civil society organizations that pushed for dialogue. What the country needed was not more rhetoric but concrete action. It is precisely this kind of grassroots participation that strengthens democracy in the long run."

According to the text, who pushed for the peace dialogue?

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-society-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"What they need are a new leader."

### Enunciado
What is the error?

### Options
- [x] A) The verb should be 'is' (singular) because 'a new leader' is singular: 'What they need is a new leader.'
- [ ] B) 'What' should be 'It'.
- [ ] C) 'Leader' should be 'Leaders'.
- [ ] D) There is no error.

### Explicación Pedagógica
In a what-cleft, the verb agrees with the complement: '**a new leader**' is singular → **is**.
