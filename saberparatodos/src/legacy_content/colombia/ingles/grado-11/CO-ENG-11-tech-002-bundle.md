## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-tech-002-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Frugal innovation involves reducing the complexity and cost of a good and its production. Usually, this refers to removing non-essential features from a durable good to sell it in developing countries. This concept challenges the idea that innovation must always be expensive and high-tech."

### Enunciado
What is a key characteristic of 'frugal innovation'?

### Options
- [x] A) Making products simpler and cheaper by removing non-essential features.
- [ ] B) Adding as many expensive features as possible to increase profit.
- [ ] C) Only selling products to the richest countries in the world.
- [ ] D) Stopping production of all goods to save the environment.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text states it involves "**reducing the complexity and cost**... removing non-essential features".

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-tech-002-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"The letter must have written by him."

### Enunciado
What is missing in this sentence?

### Options
- [x] A) 'been' (must have **been** written).
- [ ] B) 'be' (must be written).
- [ ] C) 'has' (must has written).
- [ ] D) Nothing, it is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Present/Past Perfect Passive requires **BEEN** (must have been written).
