## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-tech-003-v3`

### Contexto
Passive Causative: Have something done.
"I **had** my digital wallet **________** (set up) by a specialist."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct form.

### Options
- [x] A) set up
- [ ] B) setting up
- [ ] C) to set up
- [ ] D) sets up

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Have + thing + past participle**: someone else does the action for you. 'Set' has the same form in all tenses.

## Question 6 (Media B - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-tech-003-v6`

### Contexto
Active Causative vs Passive Causative.
"The company **had** its engineers **________** (develop) a new algorithm." (Active: the company ordered its engineers.)

### Enunciado
Choose the correct form for the active causative.

### Options
- [x] A) develop
- [ ] B) developed
- [ ] C) to develop
- [ ] D) developing

### Explicación Pedagógica
Active: **have + person + base verb**. Passive: **have + thing + past participle**.

## Question 8 (Difícil B - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-tech-003-v8`

### Contexto
Causative with 'make' (Obligation - Active vs Passive).
Active: "The regulation **made** companies **report** their emissions."
Passive: "Companies **were made ________** (report) their emissions."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct form for the passive of 'make'.

### Options
- [x] A) to report
- [ ] B) report
- [ ] C) reported
- [ ] D) reporting

### Explicación Pedagógica
When 'make' is used in the **passive**, we add **to**: "were made **to report**" (unlike active: "made companies report").

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-tech-003-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"The rise of blockchain technology has had major implications for finance. Many companies have had their payment systems redesigned to accept cryptocurrency. Some governments have gotten their financial regulators to review existing tax codes. However, critics warn that users should have their investments monitored carefully, as the market remains highly volatile. Despite the risks, the industry has had billions of dollars invested in decentralized applications."

### Enunciado
What have many companies had done, according to the text?

### Options
- [x] A) Their payment systems redesigned to accept cryptocurrency.
- [ ] B) Their employees fired for using crypto.
- [ ] C) Their offices relocated to the blockchain.
- [ ] D) Their financial regulators banned.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text says: "Many companies **have had their payment systems redesigned** to accept cryptocurrency."

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-tech-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"I got my phone to repair at the shop."

### Enunciado
What is the error?

### Options
- [x] A) With 'get + thing', use the past participle: 'got my phone repaired'.
- [ ] B) It should be 'I got my phone repairing'.
- [ ] C) 'Shop' should be 'Hospital'.
- [ ] D) There is no error.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Get + thing + past participle**: "I got my phone **repaired**." The infinitive is only used with a person: "I got the technician **to repair** it."
