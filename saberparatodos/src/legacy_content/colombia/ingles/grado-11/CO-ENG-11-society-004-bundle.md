### Options

- [ ] B) expects to implement

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-society-004-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Social movements are often dismissed as disruptive. However, history shows that rights are rarely granted without struggle. The suffragettes were ridiculed before they were celebrated. Civil rights activists were arrested for demanding equality. It is now acknowledged that these disruptions were necessary for progress."

### Enunciado
What is now acknowledged about the disruptions?

### Options
- [x] A) That they were necessary for progress.
- [ ] B) That they were useless.
- [ ] C) That they should be stopped.
- [ ] D) That they were fun.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text states: "It is now acknowledged that these disruptions **were necessary for progress**."

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-society-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"rumored / resigned / have / is / to / minister / the / ."

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a passive reporting sentence.

### Options
- [x] A) The minister is rumored to have resigned.
- [ ] B) Is rumored the minister to have resigned.
- [ ] C) The minister is to have resigned rumored.
- [ ] D) Rumored is the minister to have resigned.

Structure: **Subject + passive verb + perfect infinitive.**
