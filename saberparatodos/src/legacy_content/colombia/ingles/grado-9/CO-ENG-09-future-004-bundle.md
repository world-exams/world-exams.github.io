## Question 5 (Media A - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-future-004-v5`

### Contexto
Future Continuous vs Future Perfect.
"This time next week, we **________** (travel) to the coast, but we **________** (not / arrive) yet."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pair.

### Options
- [x] A) will be traveling / won't have arrived
- [ ] B) will travel / won't arrive
- [ ] C) are traveling / haven't arrived
- [ ] D) travel / don't arrive

### Explicación Pedagógica
Action in progress (will be traveling). Action *not* completed before that time (won't have arrived).

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

"Setting goals is important for success. By the age of 25, many young people hope they will have found a stable job. However, the path is not always straight. Some will be studying for advanced degrees, while others will be traveling the world. Regardless of the path, flexibility is key in the modern world."

What will some young people be doing instead of working?

### Options

- [ ] D) retiring.

The text mentions: "Some **will be studying**... others **will be traveling**."

Sentence Order.

### Options
- [x] A) She will have graduated by 2030.
- [ ] B) By 2030 she graduated will have.
- [ ] C) Will have graduated she by 2030.
