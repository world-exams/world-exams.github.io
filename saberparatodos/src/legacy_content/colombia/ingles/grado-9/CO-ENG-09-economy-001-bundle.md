Identifying sustainable consumer habits.

### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) The three essential actions for sustainable waste management.
- [ ] B) A special recipe for making a fruit salad at home.
- [ ] C) The names of three famous explorers from South America.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The **3 Rs** (Reducir, Reutilizar, Reciclar) are the foundation of waste reduction.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-economy-001-v10`

### Contexto
The concept of "Circular Economy".

### Enunciado
How does a **Circular Economy** differ from a Linear Economy?

### Options
- [x] A) It aims to eliminate waste by designing systems where materials are reused and regenerated indefinitely.
- [ ] B) It only allows people to use coins and paper money that are shaped like circles.
- [ ] C) It requires everyone to walk in circles for 30 minutes before entering a store.
- [ ] D) It is an economy where only children are allowed to buy and sell products.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The **Circular Economy** (economía circular) replaces the "take-make-waste" model with a "reduce-reuse-recycle" one.
