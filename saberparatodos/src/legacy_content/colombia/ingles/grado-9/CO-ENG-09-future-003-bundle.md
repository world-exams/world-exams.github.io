### Contexto
Future Perfect (Structure).
"By the year 2050, scientists **________** (discover) more about life on Mars."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct future perfect form for an action completed at a point in the future.

### Options
- [x] A) will have discovered
- [ ] B) will discover
- [ ] C) will be discovering
- [ ] D) have discovered

### Explicación Pedagógica
The **Future Perfect** (will have + past participle) is used to say that something will be finished by a specific time in the future.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-future-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"I will have went to the store by then."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) Future Perfect needs the past participle (gone), not the past simple (went).
- [ ] B) It should be 'I will have go'.
- [ ] C) 'Will have' cannot be used with 'I'.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The auxiliary **will have** must be followed by the **Past Participle** (3rd column). Correct: "I will have **gone**...".
