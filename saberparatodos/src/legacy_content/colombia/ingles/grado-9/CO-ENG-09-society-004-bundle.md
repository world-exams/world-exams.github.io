## Question 8 (Difícil B - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-society-004-v8`

### Contexto
Active vs Passive.
"The police **________** (arrest) the thief." (Active)
"The thief **________** (arrest) by the police." (Passive)

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pair to complete the sentences.

### Options
- [x] A) arrested / was arrested
- [ ] B) was arrested / arrested
- [ ] C) arrest / is arrest
- [ ] D) arresting / arrested

### Explicación Pedagógica
Active: Past Simple. Passive: **was + past participle**.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

Reading comprehension.
"The fight for human rights has a long history. In the past, many people were denied basic freedoms based on their race or gender. Today, the situation has improved, but discrimination still exists. To ensure a better future, tolerance must be taught in schools and practiced in daily life."

### Enunciado
What must happen to ensure a better future according to the text?

### Options
- [x] A) Tolerance must be taught and practiced.
- [ ] B) Discrimination must increase.
- [ ] C) People should be denied freedoms.
- [ ] D) History should be forgotten.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text states: "...tolerance **must be taught in schools and practiced in daily life**."
