## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-health-001-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying essential lifestyle components.

### Enunciado
What does having a **sedentary lifestyle** mean?

### Options
- [x] A) A person does not do much physical activity and spends a lot of time sitting.
- [ ] B) A person who runs 10 kilometers every morning before breakfast.
- [ ] C) A person who only eats vegetables and fruits for all their meals.
- [ ] D) A person who travels to different countries every month for work.

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **sedentary lifestyle** (estilo de vida sedentario) involves little to no physical movement.

Using the present perfect for lifestyle duration.
"I **________ ________** a vegetarian for three years, and I feel much more energetic."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct present perfect form for an ongoing state.

### Options
- [x] A) have / been
- [ ] B) was / being
- [ ] C) am / be
- [ ] D) did / became

### Explicación Pedagógica
The **Present Perfect** ("have + been") indicates a state that started in the past and continues now.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-health-001-v9`

### Contexto
Analyzing lifestyle impact.
"Marta sleeps only 5 hours a night, drinks 6 cups of coffee daily, and never exercises. However, she says she is 'fine' because she is productive."

### Enunciado
What is the likely long-term consequence of Marta's lifestyle?

### Options
- [x] A) Burnout and serious health issues (exhaustion).
- [ ] B) She will become an immortal superhero with infinite energy.
- [ ] C) Her body will adapt and she will never need to sleep again.
- [ ] D) She will win an award for being the healthiest person in the city.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Neglecting sleep and nutrition leads to **burnout** (agotamiento extremo) and physical decline.
