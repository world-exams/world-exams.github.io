## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-health-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"If he would have more money, he would go to the doctor."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) We do not use 'would' in the 'if' clause of a second conditional. We use past simple (had).
- [ ] B) It should be 'If he will have'.
- [ ] C) 'Doctor' should be 'Teacher'.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
In Second Conditional, the 'if' clause **never** takes 'would'. It uses the Past Simple. Correct: "If he **had** more money...".
