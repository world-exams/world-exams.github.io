## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-history-001-v1`

### Contexto
Defining historical study.

### Enunciado
What is **History**?

### Options
- [x] A) The study of past events, particularly in human affairs.
- [ ] B) Predicting the future using a crystal ball.
- [ ] C) Learning how to cook traditional food from other planets.
- [ ] D) The study of modern music released this morning.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**History** (historia) is the inquiry into past events and their significance for the present.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-history-001-v9`

Analyzing a historical cause-effect chain.
"The **Great Depression** of 1929 led to massive unemployment and eventually contributed to the rise of totalitarian regimes in Europe."

### Enunciado
What does this suggest about history?

### Options
- [x] A) Economic instability can have profound political and social consequences.
- [ ] B) People in 1929 were just very lazy and didn't want to go to work anymore.
- [ ] C) Totalitarian regimes are always the best way to fix a broken economy.
