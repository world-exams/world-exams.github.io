## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-nature-001-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying essential natural protection.

### Enunciado
What is **Conservation**?

### Options
- [x] A) The protection and preservation of the natural environment and wildlife.
- [ ] B) The process of building new shopping malls in the jungle.
- [ ] C) A special type of battery used for electric cars only.
- [ ] D) The study of ancient languages spoken in Europe.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Conservation** (conservación) is the ethical use and protection of natural resources.

### Enunciado
What is the primary goal of **Rewilding**?

### Options

- [ ] B) To create more zoos so people can see animals in cages.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-nature-001-v10`

### Contexto
The concept of "Resource Depletion".

### Enunciado
What happens when a city suffers from **Resource Depletion**?

### Options
- [x] A) It uses up natural resources (like water or minerals) faster than they can be replaced.
- [ ] B) It produces too much clean energy and has to sell it to other planets.
- [ ] C) Every person in the city becomes a professional gardener for one year.
- [ ] D) The trees start growing twice as fast and the water becomes crystal clear.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Resource depletion** (agotamiento de recursos) is the consumption of a resource faster than it can be replenished.
