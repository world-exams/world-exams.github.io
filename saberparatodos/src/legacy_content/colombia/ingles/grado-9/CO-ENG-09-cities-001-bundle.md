## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-cities-001-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying modern urban goals.

### Enunciado
What is a **Smart City**?

### Options
- [x] A) A city that uses digital technology and data to improve the quality of life for citizens.
- [ ] B) A city where every building is made of solid gold and silver.
- [ ] C) A city where animals are the only ones allowed to use cars and buses.
- [ ] D) A city that was built 5,000 years ago using only stones and wood.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Smart Cities** (ciudades inteligentes) leverage ICT to increase operational efficiency and information sharing.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-cities-001-v9`

Analyzing a sustainable urban solution.
"The **'15-Minute City'** concept proposes that all residents should be able to reach most of their daily needs within a 15-minute walk or bike ride from their homes."

### Enunciado
What is the primary goal of this concept?

### Options
- [x] A) To reduce car traffic and improve local community quality of life.
- [ ] B) To force people to run as fast as possible for exactly 15 minutes.
- [ ] C) To close all stores that are further than 15 minutes away from a park.
