## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-identity-001-v1`

### Contexto
Defining basic culture roles.

### Enunciado
**________** refers to the shared beliefs, customs, arts, and way of life of a group of people.

### Options
- [x] A) Culture
- [ ] B) Traffic
- [ ] C) Weather
- [ ] D) Algebra

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Culture** (cultura) encompasses the entire social behavior and norms found in human societies.

Using the present perfect for cultural exposure.

### Enunciado
Choose the correct present perfect form for an ongoing cultural practice.

### Options
- [x] A) have / practiced
- [ ] B) am / practicing

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-identity-001-v9`

### Contexto
Analyzing a cultural scenario.
"Yuki is from Japan but lives in Colombia. She celebrates Christmas with her Colombian friends but also observes the Japanese tea ceremony at home."

### Enunciado
What is Yuki practicing?

### Options
- [x] A) Multiculturalism (or cultural integration).
- [ ] B) Cultural isolation (she only likes her own culture).
- [ ] C) Complete loss of her original identity.
- [ ] D) Forgetting how to speak any language correctly.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Multiculturalism** (multiculturalismo) or integration involves participating in multiple cultures simultaneously.

---

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-identity-001-v10`

### Contexto
Critiquing cultural homogenization.

### Enunciado
Some people worry that globalization leads to **Cultural Homogenization**. What does this mean?

### Options
- [x] A) Local cultures disappear and the whole world becomes very similar (usually Westernized).
- [ ] B) Every person in the world becomes a professional chef and only cooks the same soup.
- [ ] C) Scientists create a machine that makes the whole planet look like it's made of gold.
- [ ] D) The internet stops working and people start using birds to send messages again.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Cultural homogenization** (homogeneización cultural) is the reduction in cultural diversity through the popularization of symbols and values.
