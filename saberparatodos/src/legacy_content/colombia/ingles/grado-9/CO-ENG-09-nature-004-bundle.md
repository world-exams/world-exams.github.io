## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-nature-004-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Climate change.
"The increase in the Earth's average temperature is global **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct word.

### Options
- [x] A) warming
- [ ] B) freezing
- [ ] C) raining
- [ ] D) planting

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Global warming** (calentamiento global) is the rise in temperature.

Connector: Due to (Reason).
"The event was cancelled **________** the bad weather."

### Enunciado
Choose the connector followed by a noun phrase.

### Options
- [x] A) due to
- [ ] B) because
- [ ] C) so
- [ ] D) therefore

### Explicación Pedagógica

## Question 8 (Difícil B - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-nature-004-v8`

### Contexto
Comparison of connectors.
"**________** she is young, she knows a lot about recycling."
"She is young; **________**, she knows a lot about recycling."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pair of connectors.

### Options
- [x] A) Although / however
- [ ] B) However / although
- [ ] C) But / so
- [ ] D) Therefore / because

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Although** starts a dependent clause. **However** connects two independent clauses/sentences.

Reading comprehension.

They produce zero emissions and reduce our dependence on fossil fuels. Therefore, governments should invest more in this infrastructure."

### Enunciado

### Options
