### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) trend
- [ ] B) wall

### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) myself
- [ ] B) my
- [ ] C) me
- [ ] D) mine

### Explicación Pedagógica

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-culture-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"They enjoyed theirselves at the party."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) 'Theirselves' is not a word. The correct plural form is 'themselves'.
- [ ] B) It should be 'They enjoy'.
- [ ] C) 'At the party' should be 'in the party'.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The correct plural reflexive pronoun for 'They' is **themselves**. There is no word 'theirselves' in standard English.
