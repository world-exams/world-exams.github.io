## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

### Enunciado
Which of these is used to have a video call with people in other cities?

### Options
- [x] A) A webcam and an internet connection.
- [ ] B) A paper envelope and a stamp.
- [ ] C) A physical dictionary.
- [ ] D) A box of matches.

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **webcam** (cámara web) enables visual communication over the internet.
