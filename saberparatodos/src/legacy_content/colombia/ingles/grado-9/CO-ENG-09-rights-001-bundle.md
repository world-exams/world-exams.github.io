## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-rights-001-v1`

### Contexto
Defining primary human rights.

### Enunciado
The **Universal Declaration of Human Rights** was created by which international organization?

### Options
- [x] A) The United Nations (UN)
- [ ] B) The FIFA
- [ ] C) The Olympics Committee
- [ ] D) The National Library

### Explicación Pedagógica
The **UN** (ONU) adopted the UDHR in 1948 to promote global rights and peace.
