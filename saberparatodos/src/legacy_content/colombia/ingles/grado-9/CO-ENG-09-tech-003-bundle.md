### Contexto
First vs Second Conditional (Semantic choice).
(Situation: It is possible to buy a smart speaker).
"If I **________** (buy) a smart speaker, I will use it to play music."

### Enunciado
Choose the form that indicates this is a real plan.

### Options
- [x] A) buy
- [ ] B) bought
- [ ] C) will buy
- [ ] D) had bought

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-tech-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"If robots take over the world, we would lose our freedom."

### Enunciado
What is the structural problem in this conditional sentence?

### Options
- [x] A) It mixes First Conditional (take) with Second (would lose). It should be consistent.
- [ ] B) 'Freedom' is not a noun.
- [ ] C) It should be 'If robots takes'.
- [ ] D) There is no error.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Conditional sentences must be consistent: **First** (If + Present, will) or **Second** (If + Past, would). Correct options: "If they **take**... we **will** lose" OR "If they **took**... we **would** lose".
