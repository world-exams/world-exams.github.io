## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-media-001-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying media professional duties.

### Enunciado
What is an **Editor**?

### Options
- [x] A) A person who reviews and corrects stories before they are published.
- [ ] B) A person who only watches movies and never works.
- [ ] C) A special machine used to print paper money at home.
- [ ] D) A type of athlete who runs very fast in the morning.

### Explicación Pedagógica
An **editor** (editor) ensures the quality, accuracy, and clarity of media content.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-media-001-v10`

### Contexto
The concept of "Confirmation Bias".

### Enunciado
How does **Confirmation Bias** affect our information consumption?

### Options
- [x] A) We are more likely to believe information that already agrees with what we believe.
- [ ] B) It helps us learn three languages in one week without any help from a teacher.
- [ ] C) It makes all news stories look like they were written by ancient poets.
- [ ] D) It helps us use our phones for 10 hours without the battery running out.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Confirmation bias** (sesgo de confirmación) leads people to seek and trust info that validates their existing views.
