## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-issues-001-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying primary global environmental issues.

### Enunciado
What is the term for the long-term changes in the Earth's temperatures and weather patterns?

### Options
- [x] A) Climate change
- [ ] B) Daily traffic
- [ ] C) School holidays
- [ ] D) Sports results

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Climate change** (cambio climático) involves significant shifts in planetary climate systems.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

### Contexto
Understanding the Greenhouse Effect.

### Enunciado
What is the primary cause of the **Greenhouse Effect**?

### Options
- [x] A) Certain gases in the atmosphere trap the sun's heat, making the planet warmer.
- [ ] B) Too many people are using umbrellas at the same time in the city.
- [ ] C) The Earth is moving closer to the sun every two years.
- [ ] D) Scientists are using giant flashlights to study the plants at night.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The **Greenhouse Effect** (efecto invernadero) results from gases like CO2 trapping heat.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-issues-001-v9`

### Contexto
Analyzing a carbon footprint.
"John flies to different countries every week and always drives his large car alone. Sarah bikes to work and eats locally grown food."

### Enunciado
In terms of environmental impact, how do their **Carbon Footprints** compare?

### Options
- [x] A) John's carbon footprint is significantly larger than Sarah's.
- [ ] B) Sarah's carbon footprint is much larger because she rides a bike.
- [ ] C) They have exactly the same carbon footprint because they are both humans.
- [ ] D) We cannot know because we don't know their favorite colors.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Carbon Footprint** (huella de carbono) measures the total Greenhouse Gas emissions caused by an individual's lifestyle.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-issues-001-v10`

### Contexto
Critical analysis of policy.

### Enunciado
A **"Zero-Waste Policy"** in a city means that ________.

### Options
- [x] A) The city aims to eliminate trash sent to landfills by recycling and composting everything.
- [ ] B) People are allowed to throw trash in the middle of the street without any rules.
- [ ] C) The city has zero money left to buy any food for the citizens.
- [ ] D) Students are not allowed to use any paper OR computers in school anymore.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Zero-waste** (residuos cero) is a circular economy goal focusing on total waste redirection and reduction.
