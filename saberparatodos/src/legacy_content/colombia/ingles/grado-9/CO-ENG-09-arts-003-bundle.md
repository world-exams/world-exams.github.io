## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-arts-003-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Art is a powerful tool which allows us to preserve our history. Fernando Botero, who was a world-famous Colombian artist, used his unique style to represent the reality of his country. His work, which can be found in many international museums, remains a source of pride for Colombians."

### Enunciado
What does the text say about Botero's work?

### Options
- [x] A) It can be found in many international museums.
- [ ] B) It is only found in his house.
- [ ] C) It is all about the future of space.
- [ ] D) Nobody knows his unique style.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text says: "His work, **which can be found in many international museums**...".

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-arts-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"This is the museum that I visited it last year."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) 'That' already replaces 'the museum', so we don't need 'it'.
- [ ] B) It should be 'who I visited'.
- [ ] C) 'Visited' should be 'visiting'.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
In a relative clause, the pronoun (**that**) represents the object. Adding **it** is redundant (a common error called 'double object'). Correct: "This is the museum **that I visited** last year."
