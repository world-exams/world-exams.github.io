## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-work-001-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying modern career paths.

### Enunciado
What is a **Startup**?

### Options
- [x] A) A new business that is designed to grow quickly using innovation.
- [ ] B) A special button used to turn on a computer monitor.
- [ ] C) A type of exercise where you jump and touch the ceiling.
- [ ] D) A historical document written by ancient kings.

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **startup** (emprendimiento/startup) is a young company founded by entrepreneurs to develop a unique product or service.
