### Contexto
Past Perfect (Structure).
"When the Spanish arrived, the indigenous groups **________** (already / build) many cities."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct past perfect form for an action before another past action.

### Options
- [x] A) had already built
- [ ] B) have already built
- [ ] C) already built
- [ ] D) were already building

### Explicación Pedagógica
The **Past Perfect** (had + past participle) is used to describe an action that happened before another point in the past.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-history-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"She have had a great idea."

### Enunciado
Wait, is this Past Perfect or Present Perfect?
"She had had a great idea before she spoke."

### Options
- [x] A) The first 'had' is the auxiliary; the second 'had' is the past participle of 'have'.
- [ ] B) It is incorrect because 'had' cannot be repeated.
- [ ] C) It should be 'She has had'.
- [ ] D) It is Present Perfect.

### Explicación Pedagógica
In **Past Perfect**, if the main verb is 'have', the structure is **had** (auxiliary) + **had** (past participle). This is correct and common.
