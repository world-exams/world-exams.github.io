"She **________** (tell) the reporter that the story was true."

### Enunciado

### Options

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-media-002-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence correction.
"The reporter told that the fire started at noon."

### Enunciado
What is the mistake?

### Options
- [x] A) \'told\' should be \'said\', or need an object (told us).
- [ ] B) \'noon\' should be \'the noon\'.
- [ ] C) \'that\' should be omitted.
- [ ] D) \'started\' should be \'is start\'.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Told** needs an indirect object (told *someone*). Without an object, we use **said**.
