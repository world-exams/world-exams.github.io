## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-environment-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"Solar panels will been installed soon."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) The correct future passive form is 'will be installed'. 'Been' is for perfect tenses.
- [ ] B) It should be 'will be install'.
- [ ] C) Solar panels are not for the future.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The Future Passive is **will be** + **past participle**. "**Will been**" is incorrect; it sounds like a mix with Present Perfect. Correct: "will **be** installed".
