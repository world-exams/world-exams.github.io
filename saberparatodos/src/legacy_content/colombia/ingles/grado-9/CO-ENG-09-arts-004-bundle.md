## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-arts-004-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Street art.
"Drawings or writing painted on walls in public places is called **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct noun.

### Options
- [x] A) graffiti
- [ ] B) portrait
- [ ] C) sculpture
- [ ] D) museum

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Graffiti** is the specific term for this type of urban art.

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-arts-004-v3`

### Contexto
Past Perfect (Action before another action).
"By the time I arrived, the artist **________** (finish) the mural."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct Past Perfect form.

### Options
- [x] A) had finished
- [ ] B) has finished
- [ ] C) finished
- [ ] D) was finishing

### Explicación Pedagógica
Past Perfect (**had + participle**) describes an action completed before another past action (arrived).

## Question 6 (Media B - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-arts-004-v6`

### Contexto
Past Simple vs Past Perfect.
"When we **________** (get) to the concert, the band **________** (already / start) playing."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct sequence of tenses.

### Options
- [x] A) got / had already started
- [ ] B) got / started
- [ ] C) had got / started
- [ ] D) get / starts

### Explicación Pedagógica
First action (start) -> **Past Perfect**. Second action (get) -> **Past Simple**.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-arts-004-v7`

### Contexto
Past Perfect (Reasons/Explanations).
"She was tired because she **________** (paint) all day."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct form to explain a past state.

### Options
- [x] A) had painted
- [ ] B) has painted
- [ ] C) paints
- [ ] D) is painting

### Explicación Pedagógica
The action of painting happened *before* she was tired. (Note: Had been painting is also correct, but 'had painted' works for completed quantity/task).

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-arts-004-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Graffiti used to be considered vandalism, but in Bogota, it has become a respected art form. By the time the government legalized street art in certain areas, many artists had already gained international fame. The guided graffiti tours usually attract tourists who had never seen such colorful streets before."

### Enunciado
What had happened before the government legalized street art?

### Options
- [x] A) Many artists had already gained international fame.
- [ ] B) Graffiti was considered legal everywhere.
- [ ] C) Tourists had stopped visiting.
- [ ] D) The streets had become gray.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text states: "...many artists **had already gained international fame**."

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-arts-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"finished / arrived / the / had / show / we / when"

### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) The show had finished when we arrived.
- [ ] B) When we arrived had the show finished.
- [ ] C) Had finished the show when we arrived.
- [ ] D) The finished show had when arrived we.

Structure: **Subject + had + finished + time clause.**
