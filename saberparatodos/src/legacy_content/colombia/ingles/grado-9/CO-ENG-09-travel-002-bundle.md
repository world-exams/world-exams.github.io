## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-travel-002-v1`

### Enunciado
Choose the correct term.

### Options
- [x] A) culture
- [ ] B) nature
- [ ] C) science
- [ ] D) machine

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Culture** (cultura) encompasses the shared traditions and arts of a society.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-travel-002-v9`

### Contexto
Reading a short text.
"Ethnocentrism is the belief that one's own culture is superior to all others. However, cultural relativism suggests that no culture is 'better' than another; they are simply different ways of being human."

### Enunciado
What is the main idea of cultural relativism?

### Options
- [x] A) Cultures should be understood as different, not better or worse.
- [ ] B) Every person must believe their culture is the best.
- [ ] C) Learning other languages is not necessary.
- [ ] D) Human beings are exactly the same in every country.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text explains cultural relativism as accepting cultures as "different ways of being human" rather than "better".

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-travel-002-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar correction.
"This is the more interesting book I have ever read about Egypt."

### Enunciado
What is the correct form?

### Options
- [x] A) This is the most interesting book...
- [ ] B) This is the interestingst book...
- [ ] C) This is as interesting book...
- [ ] D) This is the very interesting book...

### Explicación Pedagógica
When comparing one thing to all others in a group ("ever read"), we use the **superlative** (the most interesting).
