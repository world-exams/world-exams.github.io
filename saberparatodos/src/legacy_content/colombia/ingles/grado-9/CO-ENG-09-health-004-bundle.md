## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-health-004-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Medical advice.
"A list of medicines written by a doctor is called a **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct word.

### Options
- [x] A) prescription
- [ ] B) menu
- [ ] C) receipt
- [ ] D) ticket

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Prescription** (receta médica) is the document for medicines.

Reported Speech (Present to Past).
Direct: "I feel sick."
Reported: "He said that he **________** sick."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct backshift for 'feel'.

### Options
- [x] A) felt
- [ ] B) feels
- [ ] C) has felt
- [ ] D) feeling

### Explicación Pedagógica
Present Simple (feel) changes to **Past Simple** (felt) in Reported Speech.

Reported Speech (Reporting verbs).

### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) advised
- [ ] B) said

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-health-004-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"A recent study showed that screen time affects sleep quality. The researchers stated that blue light from phones mimics daylight, confusing the brain. They recommended that people should avoid screens at least one hour before bed. If teenagers followed this advice, their focus in school would improve significantly."

### Enunciado
What did the researchers recommend?

### Options
- [x] A) To avoid screens one hour before bed.
- [ ] B) To use phones in bed.
- [ ] C) To sleep less hours.
- [ ] D) To buy more blue lights.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text reflects: "They recommended that people **should avoid screens at least one hour before bed**."

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-health-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order (Reported Speech).
"said / she / tired / was / that / very / she"

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a reported statement.

### Options
- [x] A) She said that she was very tired.
- [ ] B) She was very tired that she said.
- [ ] C) That she said she was very tired.
- [ ] D) Very tired was she said that she.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **Subject + reporting verb (said) + that + subject + past verb + adjective.**
