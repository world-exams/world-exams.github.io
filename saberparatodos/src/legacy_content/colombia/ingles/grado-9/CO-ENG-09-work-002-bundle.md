## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-work-002-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary.
"A person who starts their own business is an **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct term.

### Options
- [x] A) entrepreneur
- [ ] B) employee
- [ ] C) accountant
- [ ] D) secretary

### Explicación Pedagógica
An **entrepreneur** (emprendedor) is someone who creates and owns a business.

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

### Contexto
Second Conditional structure (Condition).
"If I **________** (have) enough money, I would start my own company."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct past tense form for the \'if\' clause (imaginary situation).

### Options
- [x] A) had
- [ ] B) have
- [ ] C) would have
- [ ] D) having

### Explicación Pedagógica
In the second conditional (imaginary present), we use the **Simple Past** after "if".

Second Conditional with "Be".
"If she **________** (be) the boss, she would allow flexible hours."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct form of \'be\' for the imaginary condition.

### Options
- [x] A) were (or was)

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-work-002-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Digital literacy is no longer an optional skill. If workers didn't adapt to new technologies, they would struggle to find employment in the modern market."

### Enunciado
What does the text imply about technology and jobs?

### Options
- [x] A) Workers must learn technology to stay employed.
- [ ] B) Technology is making jobs easier to find for everyone.
- [ ] C) Traditional skills are more important than digital ones.
- [ ] D) Only scientists need to adapt to new tools.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The conditional "If workers didn't adapt... they would struggle" emphasizes the necessity of learning.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-work-002-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence correction.
"If I would be you, I will apply for that job."

### Enunciado
What is the correct version?

### Options
- [x] A) If I were you, I would apply for that job.
- [ ] B) If I were you, I will apply for that job.
- [ ] C) If I would be you, I would apply for that job.
- [ ] D) If I was you, I will apply for that job.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The advice structure is: **If I were you** (condition) + **I would...** (result).
