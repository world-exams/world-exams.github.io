## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-global-002-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary.
"The lack of food in a large area is called **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct term for food shortage.

### Options
- [x] A) famine
- [ ] B) flood
- [ ] C) forest
- [ ] D) festival

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Famine** (hambruna) describes a severe shortage of food.

Present Perfect Continuous structure.

### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) have been
- [ ] B) has been

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

Reading a graph/description of a trend.
"The world population has been growing rapidly, reaching 8 billion in 2022. This brings challenges like food security and urbanization."

### Enunciado
What has the world population been doing according to the text?

### Options
- [x] A) Growing steadily for a long time.
- [ ] B) Decreasing since 2022.
- [ ] C) Staying at 8 billion forever.
- [ ] D) Solving all food problems instantly.

### Explicación Pedagógica
"Has been growing rapidly" implies a continuous upward trend.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

Correcting a sentence.
"I am been living in this city for ten years."

### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) I have been living in this city for ten years.
- [ ] B) I was been living in this city for ten years.
- [ ] C) I am living in this city for ten years.
- [ ] D) I have being living in this city for ten years.

### Explicación Pedagógica
For duration with "for/since", use the **Present Perfect Continuous** (have been living).
