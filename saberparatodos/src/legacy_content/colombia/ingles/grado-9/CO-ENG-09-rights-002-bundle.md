## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-rights-002-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary.
"Every person has the **________** to education and health."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct term for basic entitlements.

### Options
- [x] A) right
- [ ] B) duty
- [ ] C) law
- [ ] D) fine

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **right** (derecho) is an entitlement to have or do something.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-rights-002-v7`

### Contexto
Obligation vs. Possibility.
"In a democracy, you **________** agree with the president, but you **________** respect the law."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct modals in order.

### Options
- [x] A) might not / must
- [ ] B) must not / might
- [ ] C) shouldn't / shouldn't
- [ ] D) can't / might not

### Explicación Pedagógica
You **might not** (posibilidad negativa) agree, but you **must** (obligación) respect the law.
