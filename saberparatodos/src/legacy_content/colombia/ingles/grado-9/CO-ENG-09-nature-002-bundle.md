### Options
- [x] A) footprint
- [ ] B) handprint

### Explicación Pedagógica

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-nature-002-v3`

### Contexto
Passive Voice (Present).
"Millions of trees **________** (cut down) every year to produce paper."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct passive form for a general fact.

### Options
- [x] A) are cut down
- [ ] B) is cut down
- [ ] C) cut down
- [ ] D) are cutting down

### Explicación Pedagógica
Plural subject (trees) + **are** + Past Participle (cut).

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-nature-002-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"It is estimated that if everyone lived like the average American, five Earths would be needed to sustain the population. Reducing consumption is the only way to avoid ecological bankruptcy."

### Enunciado
What does the text imply about the average American lifestyle?

### Options
- [x] A) It consumes too many resources to be sustainable globally.
- [ ] B) It is a perfect model for everyone to follow.
- [ ] C) It uses exactly one Earth's worth of resources.
- [ ] D) It is better for the environment than other lifestyles.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The mention of "five Earths" indicates excessive consumption.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-nature-002-v10`

### Contexto
Transformation.
Active: "The recycling company collects the trash."
Passive: "**________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct passive transformation.

### Options
- [x] A) The trash is collected by the recycling company.
- [ ] B) The trash are collected by the recycling company.
- [ ] C) The trash was collect by the recycling company.
- [ ] D) The trash collecting the recycling company.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Uncountable subject (trash) + **is** + Past Participle (collected).
