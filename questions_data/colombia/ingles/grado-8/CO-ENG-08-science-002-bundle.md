## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-science-002-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary.
"Alexander Graham Bell invented the **________**. We use it to call people."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct invention.

### Options
- [x] A) telephone
- [ ] B) bicycle
- [ ] C) airplane
- [ ] D) television

### Explicación Pedagógica
Bell is famous for inventing the **telephone**.

Passive Voice structure (Present).
"Spanish **________** (speak) in many countries around the world."

### Enunciado

### Options

- [ ] B) are spoken

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-science-002-v7`

### Contexto
Finding a mistake.
"The radio was invent by Marconi."

### Enunciado
What is wrong in this sentence?

### Options
- [x] A) \'invent\' should be \'invented\'
- [ ] B) \'was\' should be \'is\'
- [ ] C) \'The\' should be \'A\'
- [ ] D) \'by\' should be \'from\'

### Explicación Pedagógica
Passive voice needs the **Past Participle** (invented).

## Question 8 (Difícil B - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-science-002-v8`

### Contexto
Identifying the 'Agent'.
"Penicillin was discovered **________** Alexander Fleming."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct preposition to introduce the person who did the action.

### Options
- [x] A) by
- [ ] B) from
- [ ] C) with
- [ ] D) for

### Explicación Pedagógica
We use "**by**" in the passive voice to introduce the agent (the person who did it).

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-science-002-v9`

### Contexto
Reading a text.
"Many inventions were discovered by accident. For example, the microwave oven was invented when an engineer noticed that a chocolate bar in his pocket had melted while he was working with radar technology."

### Enunciado
How was the microwave oven invented?

### Options
- [x] A) By accident, while working with other technology.
- [ ] B) After many years of specific chocolate research.
- [ ] C) The engineer wanted to melt his chocolate faster.
- [ ] D) It was a planned invention from the beginning.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text states it was discovered "**by accident**" (example of the microwave).

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-science-002-v10`

### Contexto
Transformation.
Active: "The chef prepares the food."
Passive: "**________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct passive transformation.

### Options
- [x] A) The food is prepared by the chef.
- [ ] B) The chef is prepared by the food.
- [ ] C) The food was prepared for the chef.
- [ ] D) The food prepares the chef.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Object (food) becomes Subject + **is** + Past Participle (prepared) + by Agent.
