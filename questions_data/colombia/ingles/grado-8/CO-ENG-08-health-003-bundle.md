What is sugar transformed into if consumed in excess?

- [ ] B) It is used to build muscles.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-health-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"Milk is drink every morning."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) Passive voice needs the past participle (drunk), not the base form (drink).
- [ ] B) It should be 'Milk drinks'.
- [ ] C) 'Is' should be 'are'.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The passive voice requires the **Past Participle** (3rd column). The correct sentence is "Milk **is drunk**...".
