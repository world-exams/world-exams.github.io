## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-nature-004-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Biodiversity.
"The variety of plants and animals in a specific place is called **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct term.

### Options
- [x] A) biodiversity
- [ ] B) pollution
- [ ] C) architecture
- [ ] D) fashion

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Biodiversity** (biodiversidad) refers to the biological variety in an ecosystem.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-nature-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"the / world / in / the / is / animal / elephant / biggest / land"

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a correct superlative sentence.

### Options
- [x] A) The elephant is the biggest land animal in the world.
- [ ] B) Elephant biggest the is land animal the in world.
- [ ] C) In the world biggest land animal is the elephant.
- [ ] D) Land animal the elephant is the biggest in world.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **Subject + verb + the + superlative adjective + noun + group/place.**
