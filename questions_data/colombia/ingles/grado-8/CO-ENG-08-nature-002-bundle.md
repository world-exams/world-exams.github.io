## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-nature-002-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary.
"When you use something again, you **________** it."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct verb for recycling.

### Options
- [x] A) reuse
- [ ] B) destroy
- [ ] C) waste
- [ ] D) throw

### Explicación Pedagógica
To use something again is to **reuse** (reutilizar).

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) You ought to reduce, reuse, and recycle as much as possible.
- [ ] B) You should only recycle paper.
