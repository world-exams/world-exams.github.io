## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-travel-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"I have went to Santa Marta three times."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) Present perfect needs the past participle (gone), not the past simple (went).
- [ ] B) It should be 'I am went'.
- [ ] C) You cannot go to Santa Marta three times.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The Present Perfect requires the **Past Participle**. The past of 'go' is 'went', and the past participle is **gone** (o **been** for experiences). Correct: "I have **been/gone**...".
