First Conditional (Unless).
"We will lose the species **________** we take action now."

### Enunciado
Choose the word that means 'if not'.

### Options
- [x] A) unless
- [ ] B) because
- [ ] C) so

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-nature-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"If I will see him, I will tell him."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) We cannot use 'will' in the 'if' clause of a first conditional.
- [ ] B) Both clauses should use Present Simple.
- [ ] C) It should be 'If I saw'.
- [ ] D) 'Tell' is the wrong verb.

### Explicación Pedagógica
In First Conditional, the 'if' clause **must** use Present Simple, not future: "If I **see** him...".
