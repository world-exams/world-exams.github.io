## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-tech-004-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Artificial Intelligence.
"Machines that can learn and solve problems are part of **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct term.

### Options
- [x] A) Artificial Intelligence
- [ ] B) Manual Labor
- [ ] C) Traditional Math
- [ ] D) Art History

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Artificial Intelligence** (IA) is the technology that allows machines to simulate human intelligence.

Will (Certainty).
"I am sure that AI **________** (change) the way we study."

Choose the correct modal for a certain future prediction.

## Question 4 (Fácil B - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-tech-004-v4`

### Contexto
May / Might (Possibility).
"It **________** (become) cheaper to buy travel robots in 10 years, but it's not and certain."

### Enunciado
Choose the modal that expresses possibility.

### Options
- [x] A) might become
- [ ] B) will definitely become
- [ ] C) became
- [ ] D) have become

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Might** (podría) expresses a possibility when there is less certainty than with 'will'.

## Question 5 (Media A - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-tech-004-v5`

### Contexto
May vs Might (Probability).
"We **________** (use / not) books in the future; everything **________** (be) digital."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pair for possibility and prediction.

### Options
- [x] A) might not use / will be
- [ ] B) will not use / might be
- [ ] C) don't use / is
- [ ] D) not might use / will be

### Explicación Pedagógica
Negative of might: **might not**. Standard prediction: **will be**.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-tech-004-v7`

### Contexto
Probably vs Maybe.
"**________** AI will write all our homework."
"AI will **________** create new jobs we don't know yet."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct position/word for each sentence.

### Options
- [x] A) Maybe / probably
- [ ] B) Probably / maybe
- [ ] C) Maybe / maybe
- [ ] D) Probably / probably

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Maybe** usually goes at the beginning. **Probably** usually goes between will and the verb.
