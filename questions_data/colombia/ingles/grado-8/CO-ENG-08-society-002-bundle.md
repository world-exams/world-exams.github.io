## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-society-002-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary.
"A person who works for free to help others is a **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct term.

### Options
- [x] A) volunteer
- [ ] B) boss
- [ ] C) doctor
- [ ] D) teacher

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **volunteer** (voluntario) helps others without receiving money.

### Enunciado

### Options

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-society-002-v10`

### Contexto
Correcting a sentence.
"He have done a lot of good things."

### Enunciado
What is the mistake?

### Options
- [x] A) \'have\' should be \'has\'
- [ ] B) \'done\' should be \'did\'
- [ ] C) \'lot\' should be \'lots\'
- [ ] D) \'things\' should be \'thing\'

### Explicación Pedagógica
For third person singular (He), the auxiliary in Present Perfect is **has**.
