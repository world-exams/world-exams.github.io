## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-cities-001-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying sustainable transport.

### Enunciado
Which of these is the most eco-friendly way to travel within a city?

### Options
- [x] A) Bicycle
- [ ] B) Airplane
- [ ] C) Large Truck
- [ ] D) Helicopter

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **bicycle** (bicicleta) produces zero emissions and is a key element of sustainable urban transport.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-cities-001-v9`

"The city of Curitiba in Brazil implemented a **Bus Rapid Transit (BRT)** system. This reduced traffic and pollution by making public transport faster and cheaper."

### Enunciado
What does this imply about the BRT system?

### Options
- [x] A) It is an effective sustainable urban mobility solution.
- [ ] B) It is a very expensive way to waste people's time.
- [ ] C) It only works if people stop using bicycles entirely.

### Explicación Pedagógica
