## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-places-002-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying a landmark.
"This is a giant wall in China that is thousands of miles long."

### Enunciado
What place is it?

### Options
- [x] A) The Great Wall
- [ ] B) The Eiffel Tower
- [ ] C) Machu Picchu
- [ ] D) The Sahara Desert

### Explicación Pedagógica
The **Great Wall of China** (la Gran Muralla) is the correct landmark.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-places-002-v9`

### Contexto
Critical analysis.
"If a country protects its natural wonders, it **________** (attract) more tourists in the future."

### Options

- [ ] B) attracts

- [ ] D) is attracting

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-places-002-v10`

### Contexto
Correcting a sentence.
"Russia is the most large country."

### Enunciado
What is the correct form?

### Options
- [x] A) Russia is the largest country.
- [ ] B) Russia is the most largest country.
- [ ] C) Russia is more large than other countries.
- [ ] D) Russia is the the large country.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Short adjectives like 'large' use the **-est** ending for superlatives, not "most".
