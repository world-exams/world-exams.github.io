## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-science-001-v1`

### Contexto
Defining basic science roles.

### Enunciado
A person who works in a laboratory and conducts experiments is a **________**.

### Options
- [x] A) scientist
- [ ] B) baker
- [ ] C) pilot
- [ ] D) artist

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **scientist** (científico/a) is an expert who studies and applies scientific methods.
