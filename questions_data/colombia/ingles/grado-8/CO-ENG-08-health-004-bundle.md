## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-health-004-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Emotions and well-being.
"Stress can make you feel **________** and tired."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct adjective.

### Options
- [x] A) anxious
- [ ] B) excited
- [ ] C) energetic
- [ ] D) talented

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Anxious** (ansioso/a) is a common feeling associated with stress.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-health-004-v7`

### Contexto
Mustn't vs Don't have to.
"You **________** yell in the hospital. (Prohibition)"
"You **________** pay for the entrance. It's free! (No necessity)"

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pair.

### Options
- [x] A) mustn't / don't have to
- [ ] B) don't have to / mustn't
- [ ] C) shouldn't / can't
- [ ] D) must / have to

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Mustn't** = Prohibition (don't do it!). **Don't have to** = No necessity (it's optional).
