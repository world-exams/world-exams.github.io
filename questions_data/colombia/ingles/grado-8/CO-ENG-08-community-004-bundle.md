### Contexto
Vocabulary: Sustainable infrastructure.
"Cities with many parks, bike paths, and solar panels are called **________** cities."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct adjective.

### Options
- [x] A) sustainable
- [ ] B) crowded
- [ ] C) noisy
- [ ] D) expensive

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Sustainable** (sostenible) describes cities that protect the environment for the future.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-community-004-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Urban planners are designing '15-minute cities'. In these cities, if you live there, you will find everything you need (work, school, shops) within a 15-minute walk or bike ride. If we reduce the use of cars in this way, we will create healthier spaces for everyone. However, this transition will be difficult unless governments provide enough budget for new infrastructure."

### Enunciado
What is necessary for the transition to '15-minute cities' to succeed?

### Options
- [x] A) Governments must provide enough budget.
- [ ] B) Every person must buy a new car.
- [ ] C) People should walk for more than one hour.
- [ ] D) Infrastructure should not be changed.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text says: "...this transition will be difficult **unless governments provide enough budget**."

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-community-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"we / if / more / trees / plant / , / will / the / cooler / be / air"

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a correct conditional sentence.

### Options
- [x] A) If we plant more trees, the air will be cooler.
- [ ] B) The air will be cooler if plant more we trees.
- [ ] C) If more trees plant we, will the air be cooler.
- [ ] D) Cooler will the air be if we plant more trees.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **If + subject + present verb + object , subject + will + be + adjective.**
