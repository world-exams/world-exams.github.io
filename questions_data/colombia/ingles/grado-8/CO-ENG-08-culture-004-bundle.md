## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-culture-004-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Global citizenship.
"A person **________** respects different cultures and helps others is a global citizen."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct relative pronoun for a person.

### Options
- [x] A) who
- [ ] B) which
- [ ] C) where
- [ ] D) when

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Who** is used to add information about a person.

## Question 8 (Difícil B - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-culture-004-v8`

### Contexto
Combining Sentences with Relative Clauses.
"I know a girl. Her father is a doctor for the UN."

### Enunciado
Combine these using a relative pronoun.

### Options
- [x] A) I know a girl whose father is a doctor for the UN.
- [ ] B) I know a girl who her father is a doctor for the UN.
- [ ] C) I know a girl which father is a doctor for the UN.
- [ ] D) I know a girl whom father is a doctor for the UN.

### Explicación Pedagógica
We use **whose** to replace the possessive 'her'.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-culture-004-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Global citizenship is more than just traveling. It is an attitude that promotes empathy and justice. Those who are global citizens are aware of world issues like poverty and climate change. They believe that everyone, regardless of the country where they were born, deserves respect and equal rights."

### Enunciado
What does a global citizen believe?

### Options
- [x] A) That everyone deserves respect and equal rights.
- [ ] B) That traveling is the only important thing.
- [ ] C) That climate change doesn't exist.
- [ ] D) That only people from their own country deserve rights.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text reflects: "They believe that **everyone... deserves respect and equal rights**."

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-culture-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"is / someone / global / citizen / a / who / cares / the / about / planet"

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a definition.

### Options
- [x] A) A global citizen is someone who cares about the planet.
- [ ] B) Someone who cares about the planet is global a citizen.
- [ ] C) Cares about the planet someone who is a global citizen.
- [ ] D) Is a global citizen someone who cares the about planet.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **Subject + verb + noun/pronoun + relative clause.**
