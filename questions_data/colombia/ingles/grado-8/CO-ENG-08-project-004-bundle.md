## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-project-004-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Becoming a global citizen is a journey that never ends. It requires curiosity about other cultures and a commitment to justice. Since we are more connected than ever, our local actions have global consequences. If everyone adopts a sustainable lifestyle, we will protect the biodiversity of our planet for the next generations."

### Enunciado
Why do local actions have global consequences now?

### Options
- [x] A) Because we are more connected than ever.
- [ ] B) Because curiosity is decreasing.
- [ ] C) Because biodiversity is protected already.
- [ ] D) Because everyone is a global citizen.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text states: "**Since we are more connected than ever**, our local actions have global consequences."
