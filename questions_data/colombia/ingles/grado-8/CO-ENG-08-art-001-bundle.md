## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

### Contexto
Identifying art forms.

### Enunciado
Which art form involves creating three-dimensional figures using materials like clay, stone, or wood?

### Options
- [x] A) Sculpture
- [ ] B) Painting
- [ ] C) Photography
- [ ] D) Music

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Sculpture** (escultura) is the art of creating 3D forms.

## Question 5 (Media A - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-art-001-v5`

### Contexto
Using the second conditional for hypothetical artistic scenarios.
"If I **________** a famous painter, I **________** paint murals all over the city."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct verb forms for a hypothetical situation.

### Options
- [x] A) were / would
- [ ] B) am / will
- [ ] C) was / am
- [ ] D) did / do

### Explicación Pedagógica
The **Second Conditional** ("If + Past Simple, would + base verb") is used for hypothetical or imaginary situations.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-art-001-v9`

### Contexto
Analyzing a literary metaphor.
"In many stories, a **'White Dove'** (paloma blanca) is a **Symbol** of peace."

### Enunciado
What is a **Symbol** in art or literature?

### Options
- [x] A) An object or image that represents an idea or concept beyond itself.
- [ ] B) A musical instrument used by ancient people in Europe.
- [ ] C) A special type of pen used only by famous writers.
- [ ] D) A mathematical rule for calculating the size of a canvas.

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **symbol** (símbolo) represents an abstract idea through a concrete image or object.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-art-001-v10`

### Contexto
Art appreciation and criticism.

### Enunciado
When people discuss art, they often say **"Beauty is in the eye of the beholder."** What does this proverb mean?

### Options
- [x] A) Different people have different opinions about what is beautiful.
- [ ] B) You must use a telescope to see true beauty in the sky at night.
- [ ] C) Only professional artists are allowed to have an opinion about a painting.
- [ ] D) Every person has a tiny painting inside their eye.

### Explicación Pedagógica
This proverb highlights that **beauty is subjective** (subjetiva) and varies between individuals.
