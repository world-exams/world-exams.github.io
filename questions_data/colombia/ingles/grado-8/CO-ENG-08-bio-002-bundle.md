## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-bio-002-v1`

### Contexto
Past Continuous structure (I).
"At 8:00 PM last night, I **________** my homework."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct verb form for an action in progress.

### Options
- [x] A) was doing
- [ ] B) were doing
- [ ] C) am doing
- [ ] D) did

### Explicación Pedagógica
We use **was** with "I" for the Past Continuous.

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-bio-002-v3`

### Contexto
Interrupted actions (When).
"I was sleeping **________** the phone rang."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct connecting word.

### Options
- [x] A) when
- [ ] B) while
- [ ] C) because
- [ ] D) but

### Explicación Pedagógica
We usually use **when** before a specific event in the Simple Past (the phone rang).

## Question 5 (Media A - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-bio-002-v5`

### Contexto
Simple Past vs. Past Continuous.
"She **________** (walk) in the park when she **________** (see) a famous actor."

### Enunciado
Complete the sentence with the correct forms.

### Options
- [x] A) was walking / saw
- [ ] B) walked / was seeing
- [ ] C) was walking / sees
- [ ] D) is walking / saw

### Explicación Pedagógica
The long action (walking) is Past Continuous; the short interruption (seeing) is Simple Past.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-bio-002-v7`

### Contexto
Question formation.
"**________** you doing at 3 o'clock yesterday?"
"I was waiting for the bus."

### Enunciado
Complete the question.

### Options
- [x] A) What were
- [ ] B) What was
- [ ] C) Where were
- [ ] D) When were

### Explicación Pedagógica
Asking for the activity ("waiting") -> **What**. Auxiliary for "you" -> **were**.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-bio-002-v9`

### Contexto
Biography summary.
"Gabriel García Márquez was born in Aracataca. He **________** (write) *One Hundred Years of Solitude* while he **________** (live) in Mexico."

### Enunciado
Complete the biographical fact.

### Options
- [x] A) wrote / was living
- [ ] B) was writing / lived
- [ ] C) wrote / lived
- [ ] D) writes / was living

### Explicación Pedagógica
The creation (writing) is a completed event (Simple Past), the period (living there) is the background (Past Continuous).

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-bio-002-v10`

### Contexto
Correcting a sentence.
"I was walk home when my friend call me."

### Enunciado
What is the correct version?

### Options
- [x] A) I was walking home when my friend called me.
- [ ] B) I walked home when my friend was calling me.
- [ ] C) I was walking home when my friend calls me.
- [ ] D) I am walking home when my friend called me.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Long action (walking) must be in Past Continuous (**was walking**); interruption must be in Simple Past (**called**).
