## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-history-001-v1`

### Contexto
Defining a revolution.

### Enunciado
A **Revolution** is a major ________ in the way a country is governed or how society works.

### Options
- [x] A) change
- [ ] B) holiday
- [ ] C) song
- [ ] D) sports game

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **revolution** (revolución) is a fundamental and often rapid **change** in social or political structures.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-history-001-v7`

### Contexto
Understanding social consequences.

### Enunciado
What was a major consequence of the **Industrial Revolution** for workers?

### Options
- [x] A) Many people moved from the countryside to live and work in cities (Urbanization).
- [ ] B) Everyone stopped working and started playing video games.
- [ ] C) The world became very cold and snowy for 100 years.
- [ ] D) Horses became the presidents of all major countries.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The rise of factories caused massive **urbanization** (urbanización) as people sought jobs in cities.

What does this statement imply about the Steam Engine?

- [ ] B) It was a small and unimportant toy for children.
