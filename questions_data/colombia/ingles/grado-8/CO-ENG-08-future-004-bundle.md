## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-future-004-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: New careers.
"A person who helps people with their mental health in virtual worlds is a **________** therapist."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct adjective.

### Options
- [x] A) virtual
- [ ] B) physical
- [ ] C) ancient
- [ ] D) manual

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Virtual** refers to environments created by computer technology, a growing area for future jobs.

Choose the correct form for a plan.

### Options

Use **be going to** for plans or intentions already decided.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-future-004-v7`

### Contexto
Mixed Future Forms.
"I believe that robots **________** (replace) some workers, but humans **________** (always / lead) the projects."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pair of future forms.

### Options
- [x] A) will replace / will always lead
- [ ] B) are replacing / are going to lead
- [ ] C) replace / lead
- [ ] D) might replace / were leading

### Explicación Pedagógica
Both are predictions/beliefs -> **will**. Adverbs like 'always' go between will and the verb.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-future-004-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"The jobs of the future will require high levels of critical thinking and social skills. While machines will be taking over repetitive tasks, humans will be focusing on creative and ethical decisions. We might see the emergence of 'Trash Architects' who design buildings using only recycled materials. To prepare, students should focus on lifelong learning."

### Enunciado
What will humans be focusing on in the future according to the text?

### Options
- [x] A) Creative and ethical decisions.
- [ ] B) Repetitive tasks.
- [ ] C) Only building traditional houses.
- [ ] D) Ignoring critical thinking.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text says: "...humans **will be focusing on creative and ethical decisions**."

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-future-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"be / what / your / job / future / think / you / do / will / ?"

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a complex question.

### Options
- [x] A) What do you think your future job will be?
- [ ] B) What you do think will be your future job?
- [ ] C) Your future job will be what do you think?
- [ ] D) Do you think what will be your future job?

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **Wh-word + do + subject + verb (think) + noun phrase + will + be?**
