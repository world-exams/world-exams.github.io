## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-travel-001-v9`

### Contexto
Analyzing a flight itinerary.
"Flight 405 departs from Bogota at **10:00 PM**. It arrives in Madrid at **2:00 PM** the next day. The flight duration is 10 hours."

### Enunciado
If Bogota is **5 hours behind** Madrid, how long was the flight really?

### Options
- [x] A) 11 hours
- [ ] B) 10 hours
- [ ] C) 5 hours
- [ ] D) 15 hours

### Explicación Pedagógica
Departure Bogota 10 PM = Madrid 3 AM (10+5). Arrival Madrid 2 PM. From 3 AM to 2 PM is **11 hours**.
