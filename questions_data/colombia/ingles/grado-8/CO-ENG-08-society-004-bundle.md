### Contexto
Passive Voice (Present - singular).
"English **________** (speak) by millions of people as a second language."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct Present Passive form.

### Options
- [x] A) is spoken
- [ ] B) are spoken
- [ ] C) is speaked
- [ ] D) was spoken

### Explicación Pedagógica
Present Passive: **is/are + past participle**. English is uncountable/singular -> **is spoken**.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-society-004-v7`

### Contexto
Passive Voice vs Active Voice.
"Multinational companies **________** (employ) many people."
"Many people **________** (employ) by multinational companies."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pair (Active / Passive).

### Options
- [x] A) employ / are employed
- [ ] B) are employed / employ
- [ ] C) employs / is employed
- [ ] D) employ / were employed

### Explicación Pedagógica
Active: **Subject + verb + object**. Passive: **Object (Subject) + be + participle + by + agent**.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-society-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"goods / are / globally / thousands / daily / of / shipped"

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a correct passive sentence.

### Options
- [x] A) Thousands of goods are shipped globally daily.
- [ ] B) Globally shipped are thousands of goods daily.
- [ ] C) Daily shipped are of goods thousands globally.
- [ ] D) Thousands globally goods of are shipped daily.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **Subject (Thousands of goods) + be (are) + participle (shipped) + adverb (globally) + time (daily).**
