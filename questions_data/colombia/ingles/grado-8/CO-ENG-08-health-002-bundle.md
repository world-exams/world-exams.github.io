### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) fruits and vegetables
- [ ] B) candies and sodas

### Options

In the first conditional, we use the **Simple Present** after "if".

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-health-002-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence correction.
"If you will eat healthy, you stay thin."

### Enunciado
What is the correct version?

### Options
- [x] A) If you eat healthy, you will stay thin.
- [ ] B) If you eat healthy, you stay thin.
