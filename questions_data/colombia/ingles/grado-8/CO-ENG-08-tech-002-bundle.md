## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-tech-002-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary.
"A small computer that you can carry in your bag is a **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct item.

### Options
- [x] A) laptop
- [ ] B) television
- [ ] C) keyboard
- [ ] D) printer

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **laptop** (portátil) is a portable computer.

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-tech-002-v3`

### Contexto
Predictions (Will).
"I think robots **________** do all the housework in the future."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct word for a future prediction.

### Options
- [x] A) will
- [ ] B) are going to
- [ ] C) do
- [ ] D) were

### Explicación Pedagógica
We use "**will**" for general predictions about the future.

## Question 4 (Fácil B - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-tech-002-v4`

### Contexto
Plans (Going to).
"I **________** buy a new tablet next week. I already saved the money."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct form for an intentional plan.

### Options
- [x] A) am going to
- [ ] B) will
- [ ] C) go to
- [ ] D) buys

### Explicación Pedagógica
We use "**be going to**" for plans and intentions already decided.

## Question 6 (Media B - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-tech-002-v6`

### Contexto
Future in context.
"Technology is changing fast. Maybe we **________** (live) on Mars one day!"

### Enunciado
Choose the correct prediction form.

### Options
- [x] A) will live
- [ ] B) are going to live
- [ ] C) lived
- [ ] D) live

### Explicación Pedagógica
"Maybe" indicates a possibility or prediction, so we use "**will**".

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-tech-002-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"Are you **________** to download that app tonight?"

### Enunciado
Complete the question about a future plan.

### Options
- [x] A) going
- [ ] B) will
- [ ] C) go

### Explicación Pedagógica
