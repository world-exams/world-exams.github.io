## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-food-003-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Sustainability.
"Food that is produced without damaging the environment is called **________** food."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct adjective.

### Options
- [x] A) sustainable
- [ ] B) expensive
- [ ] C) fast
- [ ] D) sweet

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Sustainable** (sostenible) food refers to products that respect ecological balance.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-food-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"I don't have nothing to say."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect in standard English?

### Options
- [x] A) It is a double negative. It should be 'I don't have anything' or 'I have nothing'.
- [ ] B) 'Say' should be 'said'.
- [ ] C) It should be 'I doesn't have'.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
In English, a double negative (don't + nothing) is incorrect in standard grammar. Use **don't have anything** or **have nothing**.
