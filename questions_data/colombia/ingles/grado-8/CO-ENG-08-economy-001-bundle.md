## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-economy-001-v1`

### Contexto
Defining basic economic concepts.

### Enunciado
What is **Currency**?

### Options
- [x] A) The system of money used in a particular country.
- [ ] B) The speed of the wind during a storm.
- [ ] C) A special type of food eaten during festivals.
- [ ] D) The name of a very old and tall mountain.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Currency** (moneda) is the medium of exchange used in a specific nation (e.g., Pesos in Colombia).
