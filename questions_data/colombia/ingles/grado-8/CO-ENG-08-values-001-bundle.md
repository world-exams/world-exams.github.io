## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-values-001-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying citizenship roles.

### Enunciado
A person who belongs to a country and has rights and responsibilities is called a **________**.

### Options
- [x] A) citizen
- [ ] B) tourist
- [ ] C) visitor
- [ ] D) ghost

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **citizen** (ciudadano) is an official member of a political community or country.
