### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) heritage
- [ ] B) school

- [ ] D) bank

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-culture-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"It must to be true."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) Modals (must, can, might) are followed directly by the base verb without 'to'.
- [ ] B) It should be 'must being'.
- [ ] C) True is not an adjective.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Modal verbs like **must** are followed by the base verb without 'to': "It **must be**...".
