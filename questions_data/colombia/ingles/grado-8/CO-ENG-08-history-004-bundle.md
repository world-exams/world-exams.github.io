## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-history-004-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Inventions.
"Before the internet, people used a **________** to send typed documents quickly through phone lines."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct invention.

### Options
- [x] A) fax machine
- [ ] B) microwave
- [ ] C) tractor
- [ ] D) elevator

### Explicación Pedagógica
The **fax machine** was a key communication tool before the digital age.

Past Simple (Specific inventor).
"Tim Berners-Lee **________** (invent) the World Wide Web in 1989."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct Past Simple form.

### Options
- [x] A) invented
- [ ] B) was inventing
- [ ] C) has invented

Use **Past Simple** for a completed action at a specific time (1989).

## Question 4 (Fácil B - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-history-004-v4`

### Contexto
Past Continuous (Background action).
"In 2005, many people **________** (still / use) dial-up internet."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct Past Continuous form for a plural subject.

### Options
- [x] A) were still using
- [ ] B) was still using
- [ ] C) did still use
- [ ] D) have still using

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Past Continuous** (were using) describes a situation that was ongoing at a certain point in the past.

## Question 5 (Media A - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-history-004-v5`

### Contexto
Past Simple vs Past Continuous (Interruption).
"I **________** (chat) with my friend when the WiFi connection **________** (stop) working."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pair of tenses.

### Options
- [x] A) was chatting / stopped
- [ ] B) chatted / was stopping
- [ ] C) was chatting / was stopping
- [ ] D) chat / stop

### Explicación Pedagógica
Ongoing action (was chatting) interrupted by a short action (stopped).

## Question 6 (Media B - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-history-004-v6`

### Contexto
Past Continuous (Simultaneous actions).
"While my brother **________** (play) video games, I **________** (search) for info on Google."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pair for parallel actions.

### Options
- [x] A) was playing / was searching
- [ ] B) was playing / searched
- [ ] C) played / searched
- [ ] D) is playing / is searching

### Explicación Pedagógica
When two actions happen at the same time in the past, we use **Past Continuous** for both.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-history-004-v7`

### Contexto
Past Simple (Irregular verbs).
"The company **________** (send) the first email in 1971."
"We **________** (get) our first computer ten years later."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pair of irregular past forms.

### Options
- [x] A) sent / got
- [ ] B) sended / getted
- [ ] C) sent / gotten
- [ ] D) send / got

### Explicación Pedagógica
Past of send -> **sent**. Past of get -> **got**.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-history-004-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"The Information Age began with the transition from traditional industry to an economy based on information technology. It was during the late 20th century when the first personal computers appeared in homes. While engineers were developing faster processors, the internet was becoming a global phenomenon. Today, we have reached a point where information is the most valuable resource."

### Enunciado
What was happening while engineers were developing faster processors?

### Options
- [x] A) The internet was becoming a global phenomenon.
- [ ] B) personal computers were disappearing.
- [ ] C) traditional industry was growing faster.
- [ ] D) Information was losing its value.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text says: "**While engineers were developing** faster processors, **the internet was becoming** a global phenomenon."

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-history-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"doing / what / when / were / you / the / computer / crashed / ?"

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a correct past question.

### Options
- [x] A) What were you doing when the computer crashed?
- [ ] B) What you were doing when the computer crashed?
- [ ] C) When the computer crashed what were you doing?
- [ ] D) Doing what were you when the computer crashed?

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **Wh-word + were + subject + verb-ing + when + subject + past verb?**
