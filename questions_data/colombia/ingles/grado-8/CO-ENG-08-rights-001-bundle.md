## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-rights-001-v1`

### Contexto
Defining basic human rights.

### Enunciado
What are **Human Rights**?

### Options
- [x] A) Basic rights and freedoms that belong to every person in the world.
- [ ] B) Rules for playing football in the park.
- [ ] C) instructions on how to use a new smartphone.
- [ ] D) The secret codes used for computer games.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Human Rights** (derechos humanos) are universal and inherent to all human beings.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-rights-001-v9`

### Contexto
Analyzing a situation of rights violation.
"In some countries, children are forced to work long hours in factories instead of going to school. This is a violation of the **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the most specific term for this social problem.

### Options
- [x] A) Rights of the Child (Child Labor)
- [ ] B) Freedom of the Press
- [ ] C) Environmental Protection
- [ ] D) Consumer Rights

### Explicación Pedagógica
Forced work for children is categorized as **Child Labor** (trabajo infantil) and is a violation of the rights of the child.
