## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-future-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"I will being working tomorrow."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) The correct auxiliary is 'be', not 'being'. It should be 'will be working'.
- [ ] B) It should be 'I will worked'.
- [ ] C) 'Will' cannot be used with 'tomorrow'.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The future continuous structure is **will be** + **verb-ing**. "**Will being**" is grammatically incorrect.
