> **Level:** A1 (Beginner)

## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-04-feelings-001-v1`

### Contexto
You have a big smile on your face because you got a new toy!

### Enunciado
How do you feel?

### Options
- [x] A) Happy
- [ ] B) Sad
- [ ] C) Angry
- [ ] D) Tired

### Explicación Pedagógica
A smile is a sign of being **happy** (feliz/contento).
