## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-04-numbers-001-v1`

### Contexto
Counting fingers on one hand.

### Enunciado
How many fingers do you have on one hand?

### Options
- [x] A) Five
- [ ] B) Ten
- [ ] C) One
- [ ] D) Seven

### Explicación Pedagógica
One hand has **five** (cinco) fingers.

### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) Four
- [ ] B) Six

**ID:** `CO-ENG-04-numbers-001-v8`

### Contexto
Comparing numbers.

### Enunciado

### Options
