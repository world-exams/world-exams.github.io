## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-04-colors-001-v1`

### Contexto
Look at the fruit: **Apple**.

### Enunciado
What color is a typical ripe apple?

### Options
- [x] A) Red
- [ ] B) Blue
- [ ] C) Black
- [ ] D) Pink

### Explicación Pedagógica
Red (rojo) is the primary color associated with apples.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-04-colors-001-v7`

### Contexto
Reading a short description.
"My dog is **White** with **Black** spots. He has a **Brown** collar."

### Enunciado
What colors is the dog?

### Options
- [x] A) White and Black
- [ ] B) Brown and White
- [ ] C) Black and Brown
- [ ] D) Only White

### Explicación Pedagógica
The dog is "White with Black spots". Brown is the color of the collar, not the dog.

**ID:** `CO-ENG-04-colors-001-v9`

### Contexto
Completing a sequence or pattern.
"Red, Apple; Yellow, Banana; Green, ________; Purple, Grape."

### Enunciado
Choose the best word to complete the sequence.

### Options
- [x] A) Pear
