## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-04-food-001-v1`

### Contexto
A common yellow fruit.

### Enunciado
What fruit is long, yellow, and monkeys love it?

### Options
- [x] A) Banana
- [ ] B) Apple
- [ ] C) Watermelon
- [ ] D) Grapes

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **banana** (banano/plátano) is yellow and often associated with monkeys.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-04-food-001-v9`

### Contexto
Food origins.

### Enunciado
Where does **Bread** come from?

### Options
- [x] A) It is made from flour (wheat) in a bakery.
- [ ] B) It grows on trees in the forest.
- [ ] C) It is found under the ground like carrots.
- [ ] D) Animals like cows give us bread.
