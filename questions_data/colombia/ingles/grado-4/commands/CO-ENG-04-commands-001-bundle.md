## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-04-commands-001-v1`

### Contexto
The teacher wants the students to stop standing.

### Enunciado
What does the teacher say when it's time to work at your desk?

### Options
- [x] A) Sit down, please
- [ ] B) Stand up
- [ ] C) Run fast
- [ ] D) Sleep now

### Explicación Pedagógica
"Sit down" (siéntate/siéntense) is the command used to ask students to take their seats.
