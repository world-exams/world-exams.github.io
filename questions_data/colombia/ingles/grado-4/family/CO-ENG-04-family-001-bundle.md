The person who gave birth to you.

### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) Mother
- [ ] B) Father
- [ ] C) Brother
- [ ] D) Sister

### Explicación Pedagógica

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-04-family-001-v9`

### Contexto
Plurals and family groups.

### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) Parents
- [ ] B) Relatives
- [ ] C) Families
