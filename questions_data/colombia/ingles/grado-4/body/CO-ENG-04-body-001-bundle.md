## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-04-body-001-v1`

### Contexto
The part of the body you use to see.

### Enunciado
Which part of your body do you use to **look** at a beautiful flower?

### Options
- [x] A) Eyes
- [ ] B) Ears
- [ ] C) Nose
- [ ] D) Hands

### Explicación Pedagógica
We use our **eyes** (ojos) for seeing.

**ID:** `CO-ENG-04-body-001-v10`

### Contexto
Logical associations.

### Enunciado
Which of these is NOT on your **Head**?

### Options
- [x] A) Knee
- [ ] B) Hair
- [ ] C) Forehead
- [ ] D) Chin

### Explicación Pedagógica
