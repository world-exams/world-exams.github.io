## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-04-animals-001-v1`

### Contexto
A common pet that barks.

### Enunciado
What animal says "Woof woof"?

### Options
- [x] A) Dog
- [ ] B) Cat
- [ ] C) Cow
- [ ] D) Bird

### Explicación Pedagógica
