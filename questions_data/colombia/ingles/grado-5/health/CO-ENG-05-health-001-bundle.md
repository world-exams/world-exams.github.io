# Base Question: Health, Ailments, and Feelings of Sickness

> **Source:** Colombia Aprende (Suggested Curriculum)
> **Topic:** Health (Period 1)
> **Level:** A1/A2 (Beginner/Elementary)

---

## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-05-health-001-v1`

### Contexto
General question about health.

### Enunciado
When you see a friend who looks sick, what is the most common question you ask?

### Options
- [x] A) What's the matter? (or What's wrong?)
- [ ] B) What time is it?
- [ ] C) Are you playing today?
- [ ] D) Where is the hospital?

### Explicación Pedagógica
"What's the matter?" (¿Qué te pasa? / ¿Qué tienes?) is the standard way to inquire about health issues.

Analyzing cause and effect.
"You should wear a jacket when it is cold, or you will **catch a cold**."

### Enunciado
What does "catch a cold" mean?

### Options
- [x] A) To become sick with a virus.
- [ ] B) To run and get the wind.
- [ ] C) To catch a ball in the air.
- [ ] D) To be very happy.

### Explicación Pedagógica
