## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-05-routine-001-v1`

### Contexto
The first thing you do in the morning.

### Enunciado
What do you do at 6:00 AM when you open your eyes?

### Options
- [x] A) Wake up
- [ ] B) Go to sleep
- [ ] C) Have dinner
- [ ] D) Play football

### Explicación Pedagógica
"Wake up" (despertarse) is the first action of the day.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-05-routine-001-v9`

### Contexto
Reading a short paragraph.
"On Saturdays, Maria doesn't go to school. She gets up at 9:00 AM, has a big breakfast, and plays in the park. She has dinner with her parents at 7:00 PM."

### Enunciado
What does Maria do at 9:00 AM on Saturdays?

### Options
- [x] A) She wakes up later than on school days.
- [ ] B) She goes to school by bus.
- [ ] C) She eats dinner with her parents.
- [ ] D) She does her homework.

### Explicación Pedagógica
If she "gets up at 9:00 AM", and usually school starts earlier, she is waking up later.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-05-routine-001-v10`

### Contexto
Identifying the correct order.
1. Have breakfast
2. Wake up
3. Go to school
4. Get dressed

### Enunciado
What is the logical sequence for these morning activities?

### Options
- [x] A) 2 - 4 - 1 - 3
- [ ] B) 1 - 2 - 3 - 4
- [ ] C) 4 - 3 - 2 - 1
- [ ] D) 3 - 1 - 4 - 2

### Explicación Pedagógica
The logical order is: Wake up -> Get dressed -> Have breakfast -> Go to school.
