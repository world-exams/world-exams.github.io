**ID:** `CO-ENG-05-transport-001-v1`

### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) Car
- [ ] B) Bicycle
- [ ] C) Boat
- [ ] D) Plane

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **car** (carro/auto) is the most common four-wheeled vehicle for personal travel.
