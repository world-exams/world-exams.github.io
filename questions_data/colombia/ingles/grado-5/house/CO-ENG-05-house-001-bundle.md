> **Source:** Colombia Aprende (Suggested Curriculum)
> **Topic:** My House (Period 1)
> **Level:** A1/A2 (Beginner/Elementary)

## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-05-house-001-v1`

### Contexto
The room where you sleep.

### Enunciado
What is the name of the room with a bed?

### Options
- [x] A) Bedroom
- [ ] B) Kitchen
- [ ] C) Bathroom
- [ ] D) Garden

### Explicación Pedagógica
The **bedroom** (habitación/alcoba) is where we sleep.

## Question 5 (Media A - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-05-house-001-v5`

### Contexto
Prepositions of place (in, on, under).
"The cat is sleeping ________ the table."

### Enunciado
Look at the picture (imagine a cat on the floor below the table). Choose the correct word.

### Options
- [x] A) under
- [ ] B) on
- [ ] C) in
- [ ] D) behind

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Under** (debajo de) is used when something is in a lower position than another object.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-05-house-001-v9`

### Contexto
Categorizing objects and rooms.
"Mirror, sink, towel, toothbrush."

### Enunciado
In which room do you find ALL these objects?

### Options
- [x] A) Bathroom
- [ ] B) Garden
- [ ] C) Garage
- [ ] D) Living room

### Explicación Pedagógica
Mirror (espejo), sink (lavamanos), towel (toalla), and toothbrush (cepillo) are all found in a **bathroom**.
