## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-05-weather-001-v1`

### Contexto
The yellow circle in the sky that makes the day bright.

### Enunciado
When the sky is blue and the temperature is hot, we say it is ________.

### Options
- [x] A) sunny
- [ ] B) raining
- [ ] C) snowy
- [ ] D) dark

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Sunny** (soleado) is the descriptor for weather with lots of sunshine.
