> **Level:** A1/A2 (Beginner/Elementary)

## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-05-appearance-001-v1`

### Contexto
Describing height.

### Enunciado
If a person is like a basketball player and very high, we say they are ________.

### Options
- [x] A) tall
- [ ] B) short
- [ ] C) thin
- [ ] D) fat

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Tall** (alto/a) is the adjective for great height.

## Question 6 (Media B - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-05-appearance-001-v6`

### Contexto
Using the verb "to be" vs "to have" for descriptions.
"She ________ blue eyes and she ________ thin."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pair of verbs.

### Options
- [x] A) has / is
- [ ] B) is / has
- [ ] C) have / are
- [ ] D) has / has

### Explicación Pedagógica
We use **has** (tener) for features like eyes/hair and **is** (ser) for general physical traits like thin/tall.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-05-appearance-001-v9`

### Contexto
Analyzing a description.
"My best friend has **freckles** (pecas) on her nose. She wears **glasses** to read."

### Enunciado
Based on the text, which is true?

### Options
- [x] A) She has small spots on her face and uses an accessory for her eyes.
- [ ] B) She has very long hair and big ears.
- [ ] C) She is a very tall basketball player.
- [ ] D) She has a beard and a mustache.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Freckles are spots on the skin, and glasses are accessories for the eyes.
