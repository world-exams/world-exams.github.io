# Base Question: Hobbies, Interests, and Free Time

> **Source:** Colombia Aprende (Suggested Curriculum)
> **Topic:** Hobbies (Period 1)
> **Level:** A1/A2 (Beginner/Elementary)

---

## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-05-hobbies-001-v1`

### Contexto
Activity with a ball and two teams.

### Enunciado
I like to kick a ball into a goal. My favorite hobby is ________.

### Options
- [x] A) playing football
- [ ] B) reading books
- [ ] C) cooking dinner
- [ ] D) sleeping

### Explicación Pedagógica
The description of kicking a ball into a goal refers to **playing football** (jugar fútbol).

Expressing likes and dislikes.

### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) love
- [ ] B) hate
- [ ] C) am

### Explicación Pedagógica

What is Alex's possible hobby?

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-05-hobbies-001-v9`

### Contexto
Understanding negative preferences.
"I like swimming, but I ________ like running because it's too difficult."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct word to complete the contrast.

### Options
- [x] A) don't
