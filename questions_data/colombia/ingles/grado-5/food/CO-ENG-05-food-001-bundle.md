## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-05-food-001-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying common foods.

### Enunciado
I am white, small, and many people in Colombia eat me with every meal. What am I?

### Options
- [x] A) Rice
- [ ] B) Chocolate
- [ ] C) Lettuce
- [ ] D) Pizza

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Rice** (arroz) is a staple food in Colombia, white and small grains.

Using quantifiers (Some/Any).

### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) any
- [ ] B) some
