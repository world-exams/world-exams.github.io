# Bundle: Grammatical Cloze Texts (ICFES Part 7)

This bundle features continuous texts about Environment and Society where students must fill in the blanks. It tests the integration of grammar (articles, prepositions, verb tenses, pronouns, conjunctions) and advanced vocabulary within full narrative contexts.

---

### Contexto: Text 1 - "The Rise of Smart Cities" (Questions 1-5)
By the year 2050, more than 70% of the world's population (1) _________ living in urban areas. To manage this massive demographic shift, urban planners are designing "smart cities". These cities use digital technology to improve the quality of life, reduce waste, and decrease overall resource consumption. For example, smart traffic lights can automatically adapt (2) _________ real-time traffic conditions, preventing long traffic jams.

However, the rapid implementation of these technologies raises serious privacy concerns. (3) _________ every corner of the street is monitored by cameras, citizens might lose their anonymity. Furthermore, if a city becomes entirely dependent on a central computer network, a single cyberattack (4) _________ paralyze all essential services simultaneously.

Despite these risks, the transition to smarter infrastructure is inevitable. We must ensure that the technology serves the people, rather than the people (5) _________ the technology.

## Question 10 (Verb Agreement & Tense in Clauses - Difficulty 5)

**Type:** `multi-correct`
**ICFES:** Gramatical (Parte 7)

Select ALL grammatically correct and logically fitting options to fill blank (10). "before it _________ the water."

### Options
- [x] A) reaches <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [x] B) can enter <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [ ] C) entered
- [x] D) is allowed to reach <!-- weight: 0.8 -->
- [ ] E) reaching

### Scoring
- Correct: A, B, D.
