# Bundle: Literal Reading Comprehension (ICFES Parts 1 & 5)

This bundle tests literal reading skills, starting with public signs and notices (Part 1) and advancing to short informative texts (Part 5). The difficulty scales by increasing the lexical density and syntactical complexity of the literal information presented.

---

## Question 10 (Multi-Detail Literal Verification - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-literalread-001-PRO-v10`
**Type:** `multi-correct`
**ICFES:** Comunicativa (Parte 5)
**Bloom:** Evaluate

### Contexto
"The new city library opens at 9:00 AM on weekdays and at 10:00 AM on weekends. It closes at 8:00 PM every day except Sunday, when it closes early at 4:00 PM. Members can borrow up to 5 books at a time for a maximum period of 14 days."

### Enunciado
Select ALL the statements that are LITERALLY TRUE according to the text.

### Options
- [x] A) The library opens an hour later on Saturdays than on Tuesdays. <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [x] B) You cannot stay in the library until 6:00 PM on a Sunday. <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [ ] C) Members can keep three books for 20 days.
- [x] D) A member is allowed to borrow four books at the same time. <!-- weight: 0.8 -->
- [ ] E) The library is closed on Mondays.

### Scoring
- Correct: A, B, D.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Nivel 5: Extracción literal múltiple. El estudiante debe cruzar datos. A es cierta (9 am vs 10 am). B es cierta (cierra a las 4pm el domingo). D es cierta (máximo 5 libros).

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-literalread-001-PRO-v16`

**ICFES:** Comunicativa (Parte 5)

### Contexto
"The warranty covers all internal mechanical failures for a period of 24 months from the date of purchase, expressly excluding superficial exterior damage, water ingress, or malfunctions resulting from unauthorized third-party repairs."

- [ ] C) A defective internal memory chip reported on the 10th month.

### Scoring

### Explicación Pedagógica
Nivel 6: Lectura literal de lenguaje legal-técnico. A se rechaza por "superficial exterior damage". B se rechaza por "unauthorized third-party repais". D se rechaza porque supera los "24 months".

## Question 17 (Double Negatives and Caveats - Difficulty 7)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-literalread-001-PRO-v17`
**Type:** `single`
**ICFES:** Comunicativa (Parte 5)
**Bloom:** Evaluate

### Contexto
"It is not uncommon for policies designed to reduce poverty to inadvertently exacerbate the very conditions they were intended to alleviate, unless they are implemented without disregarding local cultural paradigms."

### Enunciado
What does the text literally assert about poverty reduction policies?

### Options
- [x] A) They frequently make poverty worse if local culture is ignored.
- [ ] B) It is very rare for them to cause more poverty.
- [ ] C) They must always change the local cultural paradigms to succeed.
- [ ] D) They are intentionally designed to exacerbate conditions.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Nivel 7: Desentrañar sintaxis densa. "not uncommon" = frequently. "inadvertently exacerbate" = make worse by accident. "unless... without disregarding" = if they ignore. Por tanto, frecuentemente empeoran la pobreza si se ignora la cultura local.

## Question 18 (Syntactic Parsing of Complex Information - Difficulty 8)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-literalread-001-PRO-v18`
**Type:** `weighted`
**ICFES:** Comunicativa
**Bloom:** Create

### Contexto
"Notwithstanding the company's prior assertions to the contrary, the CEO admitted Tuesday that the merger would necessitate the redundancy of no fewer than five hundred personnel, albeit strictly from the administrative divisions."

### Enunciado
Which statement captures the exact, literal facts presented?

### Options
- [x] A) The company had previously denied job losses, but at least 500 administrative workers will now be fired. <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [x] B) Exactly 500 people from all departments will lose their jobs, despite earlier promises. <!-- weight: 0.1 -->
- [x] C) The CEO said on Tuesday that a maximum of 500 administrative staff will be made redundant. <!-- weight: 0.0 -->
- [ ] D) The company always planned to fire 500 people to make the merger succeed. <!-- weight: 0.0 -->

### Scoring
- A es perfectamente preciso. B se equivoca en "exactly" ("no fewer than" = at least) y "all departments" ("strictly administrative"). C se equivoca en "maximum" ("no fewer than" = minimum).

### Explicación Pedagógica
Nivel 8: Precisión semántica absoluta. "Notwithstanding" (a pesar de), "assertions to the contrary" (negaciones previas), "no fewer than" (al menos), "redundancy" (despidos).

"Ontological arguments for existence rely not on empirical observation of the physical universe, but strictly on the premise that the very conception of a maximum greatness necessitates its existence in reality as well as in the mind."

- [x] B) The scientific observation of great things existing in the physical universe. <!-- weight: 0.0 -->

### Explicación Pedagógica
Nivel 9: Transponer una definición filosófica extremadamente abstracta a otra frase sin perder la exactitud literal del significado lógico.

## Question 20 (C2 Academic Syntax Parsing - Difficulty 10)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-literalread-001-PRO-v20`
**Type:** `weighted`
**ICFES:** Comunicativa
**Bloom:** Transfer+

### Contexto
"Had the board been apprised of the fiscal anomalies prior to the ratification of the acquisition, it is highly improbable that they would have proceeded without mandating a comprehensive independent audit."

### Enunciado
What is the STRICT LITERAL FACT we can determine about what actually happened?

### Options
- [x] A) The board ratified the acquisition without ordering an independent audit. <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [x] B) The board discovered the fiscal anomalies before ratifying the acquisition. <!-- weight: 0.0 -->
- [x] C) An independent audit uncovered fiscal anomalies after the acquisition. <!-- weight: 0.1 -->
- [ ] D) The board halted the acquisition because of fiscal anomalies. <!-- weight: 0.0 -->

### Scoring
- A es el único HECHO. La frase es un Tercer Condicional con inversión auxiliar ("Had the board been..."). Esto significa que *NO* fueron informados, por lo tanto *SÍ* procedieron, y *NO* ordenaron la auditoría.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Nivel 10: Extraer hechos literales de una estructura condicional irreal del pasado con inversión (C2 Grammar). El estudiante debe deducir la realidad invirtiendo lógicamente la hipótesis irreal.
