# Bundle: The Integrated Language (Cloze)

This bundle mimics Part 7 of the ICFES Saber 11 English exam. It tests the ability to integrate vocabulary and grammar within a continuous text, moving from basic word choice to complex semantic and structural evaluation.

---

## 📝 Text A: The Secret of Sleep (Questions 1-5)

Sleep is (1) ________ for our health. Most teenagers (2) ________ at least eight hours of rest every night. However, many students (3) ________ stay up late studying or playing video games. If you (4) ________ sleep enough, you (5) ________ feel tired and irritable the next day.

---

## Question 1 (Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-cloze-001-PRO-v1`
**Type:** `single`
**ICFES:** Lexical

### Options
- [x] A) essential
- [ ] B) dangerous
- [ ] C) invisible
- [ ] D) expensive

### Explicación Pedagógica
"Essential" (esencial) es el adjetivo lógico que describe la importancia del sueño para la salud en este contexto.

## Question 16 (Semantic Cohesion - Difficulty 6)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-cloze-001-PRO-v16`
**Type:** `multi-correct`
**ICFES:** Comunicativa

### Contexto
"The ubiquity of social media has redefined (16) ________ we perceive 'community'. It is no longer bound by geography, but (17) ________ by shared interests and digital proximity."

### Enunciado
(16) Select the options that correctly complete the meaning (how we perceive):

### Options
- [x] A) the way <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [x] B) how <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [ ] C) which path
- [ ] D) what reason

### Scoring
- Correct: A, B.
- C and D are grammatically incorrect in this slot.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Tanto "the way" como "how" pueden introducir la cláusula que describe el proceso de percepción en este contexto.

"Scientific progress is rarely a linear path of (18) ________. It is more often a messy sequence of (19) ________ that eventually point toward a broader truth."

### Enunciado
(18) Select the word that best describes the idealized version of science mentioned first:

### Options
- [x] A) revelations <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [x] B) discoveries <!-- weight: 0.8 -->
- [x] C) answers <!-- weight: 0.5 -->
- [ ] D) problems <!-- weight: 0.0 -->

### Scoring
- A capturing the "linear path" and "revelation" (1.0).

## Question 20 (Abstract Transfer - Difficulty 10)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-cloze-001-PRO-v20`
**Type:** `weighted`
**ICFES:** Todas

### Contexto
"A text is not a static object; it is a (20) ________ between the author's intention and the reader's perspective."

### Enunciado
(20) Choose the word that best captures the dynamic, interactive nature of reading as a 'transfer' of knowledge:

### Options
- [x] A) dialogue <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [x] B) transaction <!-- weight: 0.8 -->
- [x] C) bridge <!-- weight: 0.6 -->
- [x] D) storage <!-- weight: 0.1 -->

### Scoring
- A (Dialogue) implies two-way interaction (1.0).
- B (Transaction) implies exchange (0.8). C (Bridge) implies connection (0.6).
- D (Storage) is static (0.1).

### Explicación Pedagógica
Ver un texto como un "diálogo" es el nivel más alto de transferencia abstracta, reconociendo que el significado se construye en la interacción Autor-Lector.
