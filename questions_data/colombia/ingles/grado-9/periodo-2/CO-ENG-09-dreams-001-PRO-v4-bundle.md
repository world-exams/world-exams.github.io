# Bundle: Beyond the Horizon (Dreams & Ambitions)

This bundle focuses on the language of aspiration, long-term goals, and the psychological drive behind human ambition. It uses "will", "going to", and "hope/want/wish" to explore personal and collective dreams, mapped to ICFES Parts 1-7.

---

## Question 1 (Career Aspiration - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-dreams-001-PRO-v1`
**Type:** `single`
**ICFES:** Gramática (Parte 4)
**Bloom:** Apply

### Contexto
*Conversation:*
A: What's your biggest dream?
B: I ________ a professional musician and travel the world with my band.

### Options
- [x] A) want to become
- [ ] B) am become
- [ ] C) will becoming
- [ ] D) want becoming

### Explicación Pedagógica
"Want to + verb" es la estructura estándar para expresar un deseo o ambición personal en presente.

## Question 10 (Defining Success - Difficulty 5)

**Bloom:** Evaluate

"True ambition is not about competing with others, but about outperforming your past self to contribute something meaningful to society."

### Enunciado
Select ALL statements that align with this definition of ambition. (Credit: partial)

### Options

- [x] B) Ambition should have a social purpose. <!-- weight: 1.0 -->

Based on this scientific metaphor, what is the ultimate function of human ambition?
