# Bundle: Pragmatic Exchanges (ICFES Part 3)

This bundle focuses on short conversational interactions where the student must select the most appropriate pragmatic response. It covers future plans, professional advice, and hypothetical dreams, scaling from direct responses to complex socio-linguistic nuances.

---

## Question 1 (Planning Response - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-conversations-001-PRO-v1`
**Type:** `single`
**ICFES:** Pragmática (Parte 3)
**Bloom:** Apply

### Enunciado
"What are you going to do with your first salary?"

### Options
- [x] A) I'm going to save most of it for college.
- [ ] B) I like working in the city center.
- [ ] C) Salaries are very low these days.

### Explicación Pedagógica
La pregunta requiere una respuesta sobre un plan futuro específico ("going to"). La opción A es la única que responde a la intención de uso del dinero.

## Question 10 (Socio-Linguistic Nuance - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-conversations-001-PRO-v10`
**Type:** `multi-correct`
**ICFES:** Pragmática (Parte 3)
**Bloom:** Evaluate

### Enunciado
Friend: "I'm going to start my own business next month."
You: (Select ALL appropriate pragmatic responses)

### Options
- [x] A) That's a bold move! What's your business plan? <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [x] B) I wish you the best of luck with your new venture. <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [ ] C) Why don't you just get a normal job like everyone else?
- [x] D) Let me know if you need any help with the marketing. <!-- weight: 0.8 -->
- [ ] E) Business is a very difficult word to spell in English.

### Scoring
- Correct: A, B, D.

### Explicación Pedagógica
La opción C es pragmáticamente grosera. E es irrelevante al contexto comunicativo. A, B y D muestran interés, apoyo u ofrecimiento de ayuda.

## Question 19 (Meta-Pragmatics: Power Dynamics - Difficulty 9)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-conversations-001-PRO-v19`
**Type:** `weighted`
**ICFES:** Pragmática (Parte 3)
**Bloom:** Meta-cognitive

### Enunciado
Boss to intern: "It would be great if you could finish this by tonight."

### Enunciado de Tarea
Select the option that correctly identifies the HIDDEN meaning of the boss's statement.

### Options
- [x] A) It is a polite but firm command that must be followed for job security. <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [x] B) It is a friendly suggestion and the intern can say no if they want. <!-- weight: 0.0 -->
- [x] C) The boss is just wondering out loud and doesn't expect anything. <!-- weight: 0.1 -->
- [ ] D) The boss is testing if the intern understands English grammar. <!-- weight: 0.0 -->

### Scoring
- A identifies the "Mitigated Speech" (lenguaje mitigado) donde un deseo se traduce en una orden en una jerarquía de poder.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Nivel 9: Analizar la pragmática del poder donde una estructura de cortesía ("It would be great") es en realidad un imperativo laboral.

## Question 20 (Transfer & Paradoxical Response - Difficulty 10)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-conversations-001-PRO-v20`
**Type:** `weighted`
**ICFES:** Pragmática (Parte 3)
**Bloom:** Transfer+

### Enunciado
An AI asks a human: "If I am going to become your superior in every task, will you still find value in your own intentions?"

### Options
- [x] A) Our value will shift from the efficiency of the result to the sincerity of the struggle. <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [x] B) I am going to turn you off if you ask that again. <!-- weight: 0.2 -->
- [x] C) I don't know, I haven't studied AI grammar yet. <!-- weight: 0.1 -->
- [ ] D) Yes, because humans are better than robots. <!-- weight: 0.3 -->

### Scoring
- A is a philosophical transfer of the concept of "value" in a post-scarcity/AI world.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Pregunta final: Responder a un dilema existencial planteado por una inteligencia artificial, analizando la naturaleza humana frente a la eficiencia.
