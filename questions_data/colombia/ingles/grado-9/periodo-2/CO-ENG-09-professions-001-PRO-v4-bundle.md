# Bundle: Future Careers & Skills

This bundle explores the world of work, from traditional professions to emerging digital careers. It focuses on the linguistic skills needed to describe duties, requirements, and the socio-economic evolution of the labor market, mapped to ICFES Parts 1-7.

## Question 1 (Career Definition - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-professions-001-PRO-v1`
**Type:** `single`
**ICFES:** Léxico (Parte 1)
**Bloom:** Apply

### Contexto
A person who designs buildings and ensures they are safe and functional is called an _______.

### Options
- [x] A) architect
- [ ] B) archaeology
- [ ] C) accountant
- [ ] D) artist

### Explicación Pedagógica
"Architect" es el sustantivo correcto para la profesión descrita. Opción B es la disciplina, C y D son profesiones no relacionadas con el diseño de edificios.

## Question 10 (Career Evolution - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-professions-001-PRO-v10`
**Type:** `multi-correct`
**ICFES:** Comprensión Inferencial (Parte 6)
**Bloom:** Evaluate

### Contexto
"The 'gig economy' refers to a labor market characterized by short-term contracts or freelance work. While it offers flexibility, it often lacks the stability and benefits of traditional employment."

### Enunciado
What can be concluded about the gig economy? (Select all that apply)

### Options
- [x] A) It allows workers to choose their own schedule. <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [x] B) Workers might face financial insecurity. <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [ ] C) It is only for people who are lazy.
- [x] D) It represents a shift away from 9-to-5 office jobs. <!-- weight: 0.8 -->
- [ ] E) Health insurance is always included in these contracts.

### Scoring
- Correct: A, B, D.

### Explicación Pedagógica
"Flexibility" implica control de horario (A). "Lacks stability" sugiere inseguridad financiera (B). Es un modelo opuesto al tradicional (D).

## Question 12 (Professional Ethics - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-professions-001-PRO-v12`
**Type:** `single`
**ICFES:** Léxico-Gramatical (Parte 7)
**Bloom:** Evaluate

### Contexto
(1) _______ professional, you have the duty (2) _______ confidentiality regarding your clients' data, even if (3) _______ difficult.

### Options
- [x] A) As a / to maintain / it is
- [ ] B) Being / maintaining / is
- [ ] C) For an / to keeps / its
- [ ] D) Like / for maintain / they are

### Explicación Pedagógica
"As a" introduce el rol. "Duty to maintain" es la forma verbal correcta. "It is" completa el sujeto inexistente en español pero necesario en inglés.

## Question 16 (Interdisciplinary Analysis - Difficulty 6)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-professions-001-PRO-v16`
**Type:** `multi-correct`
**ICFES:** Análisis Crítico (Parte 6)
**Bloom:** Analyze+

### Contexto
In the late 19th century, 90% of workers were in agriculture. Today, it's less than 2% in the US. This "Great Transition" didn't create mass unemployment; it created the service sector.

### Enunciado
Select ALL valid conclusions based on this historical data. (Credit: partial)

### Options
- [x] A) Job loss in one sector can lead to the birth of entirely new industries. <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [x] B) The definition of a "traditional job" changes over time. <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [ ] C) Food production has stopped because there are fewer farmers.
- [x] D) Productivity increased, allowing people to work in different areas. <!-- weight: 0.8 -->
- [ ] E) Agriculture is the only important job for the future.

### Scoring
- Correct: A, B, D.

### Explicación Pedagógica
El texto muestra que el fin de un sector laboral no es el fin del trabajo (A/B) y que la sociedad evoluciona hacia servicios (D).

## Question 18 (Causal Logic in Careers - Difficulty 8)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-professions-001-PRO-v18`
**Type:** `weighted`
**ICFES:** Todas (Integración)
**Bloom:** Create

### Contexto
"The automation of cognitive tasks (LLMs) is reducing the demand for entry-level white-collar roles, forcing graduates to demonstrate 'high-level oversight' abilities immediately."

### Enunciado
What is the primary challenge for new graduates according to this prediction?

### Options
- [x] A) The 'entry-level' step is disappearing; they must manage AI instead of doing manual data tasks. <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [x] B) Technology is making jobs easier for everyone without any effort. <!-- weight: 0.2 -->
- [x] C) Graduates need to become experts in oversight (supervision) faster than before. <!-- weight: 0.8 -->
- [ ] D) They should stop going to university entirely. <!-- weight: 0.0 -->

### Scoring
- A is the most accurate transformation (The 'missing rung' of the ladder).

### Explicación Pedagógica
Nivel 8: Sintetizar que la desaparición de tareas básicas fuerza una maduración profesional acelerada.

## Question 19 (Meta-Cognitive Professionalism - Difficulty 9)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-professions-001-PRO-v19`
**Type:** `weighted`
**ICFES:** Análisis de Retórica (Parte 6)
**Bloom:** Meta-cognitive

### Contexto
"Employment is a contract of time for money. Entrepreneurship is a contract of risk for freedom."

### Enunciado
What underlying assumption is the author making about the nature of choice in the labor market?

### Options
- [x] A) That all professional paths require a sacrifice (time or security) to obtain a specific benefit. <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [x] B) That everyone should be an entrepreneur to be truly happy. <!-- weight: 0.4 -->
- [x] C) That employees are bored and entrepreneurs are rich. <!-- weight: 0.1 -->
- [ ] D) That choosing a job is a random event without any trade-offs. <!-- weight: 0.0 -->

### Scoring

Nivel 9: Analizar la estructura interna de una comparación filosófica sobre el trabajo.

## Question 20 (Transfer & Synthesis - Difficulty 10)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-professions-001-PRO-v20`
**Type:** `weighted`
**ICFES:** Todas (Integración)
**Bloom:** Transfer+

### Contexto
"In a world where AI can replicate every technical skill, 'Professionalism' will eventually be defined not by what you can do, but by the level of responsibility you are willing to take for the outcome."

### Enunciado
How does this change the traditional educational focus on 'Learning Skills'?

### Options
- [x] A) It shifts the goal from acquiring 'how-to' knowledge to developing ethical accountability and judgment. <!-- weight: 1.0 -->
- [x] B) It means we don't need to study anything anymore because AI does it all. <!-- weight: 0.1 -->
- [x] C) It proves that teachers are no longer part of the professional world. <!-- weight: 0.2 -->
- [ ] D) Responsibility is a word that cannot be measured in a professional context. <!-- weight: 0.0 -->

### Scoring
- A is a high-level transfer from skill-acquisition to accountability-development.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Pregunta final: Transfiere la resolución técnica a la ontología de la responsabilidad en la era de la IA.
