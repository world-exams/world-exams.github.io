# Bundle: Colombian Identity (ICFES Part 1 - Vocabulary Matching A-H)

This bundle focuses on Part 1 of the ICFES exam: vocabulary matching. A master set of 8 definitions (A-H) is provided. Students must match each definition to one of the 20 terms presented in individual questions. Topic: Colombian and Latin American cultural identity at B1+ level.

---

## Master Context: Term Definitions

- **A.** The culture, language, and traditions inherited from indigenous peoples.
- **B.** A person of mixed European (usually Spanish) and indigenous ancestry.
- **C.** The set of beliefs, values, arts, and social habits that define a community.
- **D.** A deep connection to one's place of origin, community, or cultural roots.
- **E.** A festival or celebration that expresses a community's cultural identity.
- **F.** A form of art, music, or storytelling passed down through generations without formal documentation.
- **G.** The process by which people or ideas from different cultural backgrounds blend together.
- **H.** Official recognition or protection given to a cultural practice, site, or artifact.
