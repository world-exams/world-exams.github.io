# Bundle: Afro-Colombian Heritage (ICFES Part 1 - Vocabulary Matching A-H)

This bundle focuses on Part 1: vocabulary matching. The topic is Afro-Colombian heritage, music, cuisine, and history. A master set of 8 definitions (A-H) is provided for matching. Level: B1+/B2.

---

## Master Context: Term Definitions

- **A.** The forced transportation of enslaved Africans to the Americas, one of history's greatest human tragedies.
- **B.** A cultural fusion originating in the Colombian Caribbean blending indigenous, African, and European elements.
- **C.** A traditional Afro-Colombian musical genre from the Pacific region, often associated with spiritual events.
- **D.** An ingredient, dish, or culinary tradition that reflects African influence in Colombian cooking.
- **E.** The legal or social status of freedom from enslavement, often fought for and self-determined.
- **F.** A concept describing spaces or communities where enslaved people escaped to form free settlements.
- **G.** Colombia's primary legal provision recognizing Afro-Colombian territorial and cultural rights.
- **H.** The ongoing social, economic, and cultural effects of historical racial discrimination experienced today.
