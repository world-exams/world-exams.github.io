Most of these low-cost items are made (6) _______ synthetic fibers like polyester, which take hundreds of years to decompose. (7) _______ we continue to buy clothes at this rate, the textile industry will account for 25% of the world's carbon budget by 2050. It is crucial that we (8) _______ our relationship with fashion. Instead (9) _______ following every trend, we should (10) _______ quality over quantity.
