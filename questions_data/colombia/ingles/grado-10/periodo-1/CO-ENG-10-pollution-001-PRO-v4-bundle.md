# Bundle: Pollution & Waste Management (ICFES Part 7 - Cloze)

This bundle focuses on grammatical accuracy and the use of connectors in the context of urban pollution and waste solutions. It challenges students to differentiate between similar grammatical structures at a B1+ level.
