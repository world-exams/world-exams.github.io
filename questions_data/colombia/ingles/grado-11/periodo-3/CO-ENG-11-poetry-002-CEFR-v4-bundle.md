## Question 19 — **CEFR: C1** | Difficulty: 10
**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-poetry-002-CEFR-v19`
**Skill:** C1 — critique of weak analytical writing

**Enunciado:** Identify ALL weaknesses in this sentence: "The poem is very emotional and makes you feel sad about immigration because the writer chose words carefully."

**Options:**
- [x] A) Multiple weaknesses: (1) "very emotional" is vague — no specific device or technique named; (2) "makes you feel sad" conflates personal response with textual analysis; (3) "about immigration" doesn't capture the poem's specific argument; (4) "the writer chose words carefully" is trivially true of all poets and explains nothing; (5) no quotation or evidence; (6) no analytical vocabulary.
- [ ] B) The sentence is acceptable for informal literary response but too casual for formal analysis.
- [ ] C) The only weakness is the use of "very" which is informal; the rest is analytically sound.
- [ ] D) There is one main weakness: "makes you feel" is subjective and should be avoided.
