## Question 1 — **CEFR: B1+** | Difficulty: 5
**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-health-002-CEFR-v1`
**Skill:** Grammar — basic reported statement

**Enunciado:** A doctor said: "Exercise reduces the risk of cardiovascular disease." Report this statement correctly:

**Options:**
- [x] A) "The doctor said that exercise reduced the risk of cardiovascular disease."
- [ ] B) "The doctor said that exercise reduces the risk of cardiovascular disease." *(No backshift — technically possible but less standard with 'said')*
- [ ] C) "The doctor said that exercise had been reducing the risk of cardiovascular disease."
- [ ] D) "The doctor told that exercise reduced the risk of cardiovascular disease."
