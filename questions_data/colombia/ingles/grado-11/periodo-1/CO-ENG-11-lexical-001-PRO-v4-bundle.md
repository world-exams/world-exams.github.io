**Bloom:** Apply

### Contexto
During a high-level corporate meeting, the CEO mentioned they need to **cut to the chase** to reach a decision before the deadline.

### Enunciado
What is the meaning of the idiom "cut to the chase" in this context?

### Options
- [x] A) To get directly to the point without wasting time
- [ ] B) To physically run after someone in the office
- [ ] C) To end the meeting immediately without a vote
- [ ] D) To reduce the budget for the chasing department

### Explicación Pedagógica
"Cut to the chase" is a common idiom meaning to skip unnecessary details and focus on the main issue.
