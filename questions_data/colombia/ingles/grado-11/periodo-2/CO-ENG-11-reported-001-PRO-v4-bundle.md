## Question 1 (Standard Application - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-reported-001-PRO-v1`
**Type:** `single`
**ICFES:** Gramatical
**Bloom:** Apply

### Contexto
The professor told the class, "The final exam will cover all the material we have discussed this semester."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct reported speech transformation:

### Options
- [ ] A) The professor told the class that the final exam will cover all the material we have discussed this semester.
- [ ] B) The professor told the class that the final exam would covered all the material they had discussed that semester.
- [x] C) The professor told the class that the final exam would cover all the material they had discussed that semester.
- [ ] D) The professor told the class the final exam would cover all the material we had discussed this semester.

### Explicación Pedagógica
When changing from direct to reported speech after a past tense reporting verb ("told"), tense backshifting is required: "will cover" becomes "would cover", and "have discussed" becomes "had discussed". Pronouns change ("we" to "they"), and time/place words change ("this" to "that"). Option C correctly applies all these rules. Option A fails to backshift. Option B uses "covered" after the modal "would." Option D fails to change the pronoun and the demonstrative adjective.
