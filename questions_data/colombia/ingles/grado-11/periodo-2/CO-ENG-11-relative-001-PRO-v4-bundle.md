## Question 1 (Standard Application - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-relative-001-PRO-v1`
**Type:** `single`
**ICFES:** Gramatical
**Bloom:** Apply

### Contexto
The professor just published a groundbreaking study on climate change. She has won three international awards.

### Enunciado
Select the correct combination using a non-defining relative clause:

### Options
- [x] A) The professor, who has won three international awards, just published a groundbreaking study on climate change.
- [ ] B) The professor that has won three international awards just published a groundbreaking study on climate change.
- [ ] C) The professor, whom has won three international awards, just published a groundbreaking study on climate change.
- [ ] D) The professor whose has won three international awards, just published a groundbreaking study on climate change.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The relative clause adds extra information about a specific noun ("The professor"), making it a non-defining relative clause requiring commas. For a person acting as the subject, "who" is used. Option A correctly uses commas and "who". Option B incorrectly uses "that" without commas (incorrect for non-defining clauses referencing specific people). Option C incorrectly uses "whom" (object pronoun) instead of the subject "who". Option D incorrectly uses the possessive pronoun "whose".
