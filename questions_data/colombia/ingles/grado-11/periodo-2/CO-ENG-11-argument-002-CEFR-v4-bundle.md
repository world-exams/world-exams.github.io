## Question 19 — **CEFR: C1** | Difficulty: 10
**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-argument-002-CEFR-v19`
**Skill:** C1 — argument coherence assessment

**Enunciado:** Evaluate this argumentative paragraph's coherence: "Social media harms democracy. Furthermore, social media has many benefits. However, democracy is important. Consequently, social media should be regulated."

**Options:**
- [x] A) It is incoherent — the connectors (Furthermore, However, Consequently) do not logically connect the sentences; the sequence mixes contrast and addition without establishing a clear causal or logical thread; the conclusion does not follow from the preceding reasoning.
- [ ] B) It is coherent — the connectors are grammatically correct, which is sufficient for academic writing.
- [ ] C) It is coherent because it presents multiple perspectives on social media.
- [ ] D) It would be coherent if longer; the problem is its brevity, not its logic.
