# Bundle: Formal vs. Informal Register — Reading Two Texts on the Same Topic
**CEFR Level:** B2 (Questions range B1+ → C1)
**Focus:** Identifying and analyzing formal/informal features, vocabulary, tone, purpose, and audience differences between register-matched texts

---

## 📖 Texts on the Same Topic: The Benefits of a Four-Day Work Week

**Text A (Formal — Corporate Report Extract):**
*"The implementation of a condensed four-day work schedule across a cohort of 61 UK-based companies between 2022 and 2023 yielded substantive improvements in measured productivity metrics, employee wellbeing indices, and staff retention rates. Participants reported a 57% reduction in turnover intention and a statistically significant improvement (p < 0.01) in self-assessed work-life balance scores. Crucially, revenue performance over the trial period demonstrated no statistically significant decline relative to pre-trial benchmarks, challenging the prevailing assumption that temporal work reduction necessarily corresponds with diminished organizational output."*

**Text B (Informal — Blog Post Extract):**
*"Okay, so imagine working Monday through Thursday and then having THREE WHOLE DAYS off. Sounds amazing, right? Well, it turns out it's not just a dream — thousands of workers in a big UK study actually did it, and guess what? Their bosses were totally fine with the results! People were happier, they stopped quitting so much, and the companies didn't even really lose money. So basically, working less might actually make you work BETTER. Mind = blown."*
