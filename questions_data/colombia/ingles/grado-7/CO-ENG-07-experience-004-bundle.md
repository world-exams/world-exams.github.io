## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-experience-004-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Travel experiences.
"I want to see the world! I love **________** to different countries."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct word.

### Options
- [x] A) traveling
- [ ] B) sleeping
- [ ] C) cooking
- [ ] D) studying

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Traveling** (viajar) is the activity of visiting different countries.

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-experience-004-v3`

### Contexto
Present Perfect (Affirmative - regular).
"I **________** (visit) Bogota many times."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct Present Perfect form.

### Options
- [x] A) have visited
- [ ] B) has visited
- [ ] C) visited
- [ ] D) am visiting

### Explicación Pedagógica
Present Perfect: **have/has + past participle**. 'I' takes 'have'. 'Visit' is regular -> 'visited'.

Present Perfect (Affirmative - irregular).

Present Perfect (Negative).
"We **________** (not / eat) sushi yet."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct negative form.

### Options
- [x] A) haven't eaten
- [ ] B) hasn't eaten
- [ ] C) didn't eat

Negative: **haven't/hasn't + past participle**. 'We' takes 'haven't'. Participle of eat is **eaten**.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-experience-004-v7`

### Contexto
Present Perfect vs Past Simple.
A: "Have you ever been to San Andres?"
B: "Yes, I **________** (go) there last year."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct verb form for the specific past time 'last year'.

### Options
- [x] A) went
- [ ] B) have gone
- [ ] C) have been
- [ ] D) go

### Explicación Pedagógica
Use **Past Simple** (went) for finished actions in a specific past time (last year). Use Present Perfect for general experiences.

## Question 8 (Difícil B - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-experience-004-v8`

### Contexto
Irregular Past Participles.
"I have **________** (write) a letter to my penpal."
"She has **________** (do) her homework."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pair of past participles.

### Options
- [x] A) written / done
- [ ] B) wrote / did
- [ ] C) written / did
- [ ] D) wrote / done

### Explicación Pedagógica
Participle of write -> **written**. Participle of do -> **done**.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-experience-004-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"My name is Carlos. I am 13 years old and I have had many adventures. I have climbed a mountain in Boyacá and I have swum in the Atlantic Ocean. I haven't traveled outside Colombia yet, but I want to visit Spain next year. My sister has lived in Madrid for two years."

### Enunciado
Has Carlos ever traveled outside his country?

### Options
- [x] A) No, he hasn't.
- [ ] B) Yes, he has.
- [ ] C) He lives in Spain now.
- [ ] D) He doesn't want to travel.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text says: "I **haven't traveled outside Colombia yet**."

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-experience-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"never / have / food / eaten / I / Mexican"

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a correct sentence.

### Options
- [x] A) I have never eaten Mexican food.
- [ ] B) I never have eaten Mexican food.
- [ ] C) I have eaten never Mexican food.
- [ ] D) Mexican food have I never eaten.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Position of 'never': **Subject + have/has + never + past participle.**
