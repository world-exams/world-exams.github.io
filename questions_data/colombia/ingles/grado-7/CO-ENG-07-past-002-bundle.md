# Base Question: What Did You Do Yesterday?

> **Source:** Nigeria Curriculum (Adapted for Colombia)
> **Topic:** Simple Past (Verbs) (Period 2)
> **Level:** A2 (Elementary)

---

## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-past-002-v1`

### Contexto
Regular verb (ED).
"Yesterday, I **________** football."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct verb form.

### Options
- [x] A) played
- [ ] B) play
- [ ] C) playing
- [ ] D) plays

### Explicación Pedagógica
Regular verbs in past tense end in **-ed** (played).

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-past-002-v10`

### Contexto
Correcting a sentence.
"He didn't went to school."

### Enunciado
What is the correct form?

### Options
- [x] A) He didn't go to school.
- [ ] B) He didn't goed to school.
- [ ] C) He not went to school.
- [ ] D) He don't went to school.

### Explicación Pedagógica
After "didn't", the verb must be in the **base form** (go), not the past form (went).
