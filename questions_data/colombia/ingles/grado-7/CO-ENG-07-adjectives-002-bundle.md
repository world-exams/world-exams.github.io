## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-adjectives-002-v1`

### Contexto
Opposites.
"This box is not big. It is **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct adjective.

### Options
- [x] A) small
- [ ] B) tall
- [ ] C) hot
- [ ] D) fat

### Explicación Pedagógica
The opposite of big is **small** (pequeño).

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-adjectives-002-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence correction.
"This exam is more easy than the last one."

### Enunciado
What is the correct form?

### Options
- [x] A) This exam is easier than the last one.
- [ ] B) This exam is easyer than the last one.
- [ ] C) This exam is more easier than the last one.
- [ ] D) This exam is as easy than the last one.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Adjectives ending in 'y' change to **-ier** (easier), never "more easy".
