## Question 8 (Difícil B - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-sports-004-v8`

### Contexto
'Stop' + Gerund vs Infinitive.
A: "I stopped **________** (run) because I was tired." (Gave up the activity)
B: "I stopped **________** (look) at the map." (Stopped for a purpose)

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pair for these two different meanings.

### Options
- [x] A) running / to look
- [ ] B) to run / looking
- [ ] C) running / looking
- [ ] D) to run / to look

### Explicación Pedagógica
Stop + -ing = quit the action. Stop + to = interrupt one action to do another.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-sports-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"finished / training / he / has / not / yet"

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a correct Present Perfect negative sentence.

### Options
- [x] A) He has not finished training yet.
- [ ] B) He not has finished training yet.
- [ ] C) He has finished not training yet.
- [ ] D) Training has not he finished yet.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **Subject + has not + past participle + (additional info) + yet.**
