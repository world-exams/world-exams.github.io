## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-travel-004-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Travel items.
"You need this document to travel to another country. It is a **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct word.

### Options
- [x] A) passport
- [ ] B) ticket
- [ ] C) suitcase
- [ ] D) map

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **passport** (pasaporte) is the official identification document for international travel.

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-travel-004-v3`

### Contexto
Be going to (Future plans).
"I **________** (visit) my cousins in Medellin next month."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct form for a planned future action.

### Options
- [x] A) am going to visit
- [ ] B) am and visiting
- [ ] C) go to visit
- [ ] D) will visit to

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Be going to** is used for plans and intentions. 'I' takes 'am going to'.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-travel-004-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Dear Maria, Next week I'm going to travel to the Coffee Region. I'm going to stay in a traditional coffee farm. I'm taking my camera because I want to take photos of the wax palms in Salento. I'm not going to buy many souvenirs, but I will definitely drink a lot of coffee! I will send you a postcard. Best, Julian."

### Enunciado
What is Julian going to do in Salento?

### Options
- [x] A) Take photos of the wax palms.
- [ ] B) Buy many souvenirs.
- [ ] C) Send a postcard before he leaves.
- [ ] D) Stay in a modern hotel.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text says: "I want to **take photos of the wax palms in Salento**."
