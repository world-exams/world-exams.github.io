## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-tech-004-v1`

### Contexto

"When you like a photo on Instagram, you **________** it."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct verb.

### Options
- [x] A) like
- [ ] B) delete
- [ ] C) print
- [ ] D) close

### Explicación Pedagógica
In social media, people **like** (dan me gusta) content they enjoy.

Adverbs of Manner (-ly).
"You should type your password **________** (quiet / careful)."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct adverb form.

### Options
- [x] A) carefully
- [ ] B) careful
- [ ] C) carefuly
- [ ] D) carefulness

### Explicación Pedagógica
Most adverbs are formed by adding **-ly** to the adjective: careful -> **carefully**.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-tech-004-v7`

### Contexto
Adverbs vs Adjectives.
"The video is **________** (slow), it is loading **________** (slow)."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pair (Adjective / Adverb).

### Options
- [x] A) slow / slowly
- [ ] B) slowly / slow
- [ ] C) slow / slow
- [ ] D) slowly / slowly

### Explicación Pedagógica
Adjective describes a noun (The video is **slow**). Adverb describes a verb (loading **slowly**).

## Question 8 (Difícil B - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-tech-004-v8`

### Contexto
Position of Adverbs.
"They followed the online safety rules **________**."

### Enunciado
Where does the adverb usually go in this sentence?

### Options
- [x] A) at the end (strictly).
- [ ] B) before the subject (Strictly they).
- [ ] C) after the subject (They strictly).
- [ ] D) after 'the' (the strictly rules).

### Explicación Pedagógica
Adverbs of manner often go **after the object** or the verb: "...followed the rules **strictly**."

### Enunciado
According to the text, how should you communicate online?

### Options

The text says: "...you should wait and **think clearly before posting**."

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-tech-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"should / software / you / update / regularly / your"

### Enunciado
Order the words to form an advice sentence.

### Options
- [x] A) You should update your software regularly.
- [ ] B) You should update regularly your software.
