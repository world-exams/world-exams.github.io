## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-food-004-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"In some countries, people eat insects that are rich in protein. For example, in Mexico, you can find grasshoppers which are sold as a salty snack. While many westerners find it unusual, it is a tradition whose history goes back centuries. Entomophagy is the name of the practice where people eat bugs."

### Enunciado
What is "entomophagy"?

### Options
- [x] A) The practice where people eat bugs.
- [ ] B) A type of spicy grasshopper.
- [ ] C) A tradition from Western countries.
- [ ] D) A salty snack sold in supermarkets.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text defines it: "Entomophagy is the name of the practice **where people eat bugs**."
