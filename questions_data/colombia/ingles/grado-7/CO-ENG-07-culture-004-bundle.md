## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-culture-004-v3`

### Contexto
Passive Voice Intro (Process).
"Coffee **________** (produce) in many regions of Colombia."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct Present Passive form.

### Options
- [x] A) is produced
- [ ] B) are produced
- [ ] C) is produce
- [ ] D) producing

### Explicación Pedagógica
Present Passive: **is/are + past participle**. Coffee is uncountable/singular -> **is produced**.

How are the flower arrangements moved during the parade?

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-culture-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"in / Barranquilla / Carnival / January / held / is / not"

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a correct fact about a festival. (Note: It's in February).

### Options
- [x] A) Carnival is not held in January in Barranquilla.
- [ ] B) In January is not held Carnival in Barranquilla.
- [ ] C) Barranquilla Carnival is not in January held.
- [ ] D) Not held is Carnival in January in Barranquilla.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **Subject + is + not + past participle (held) + place/time.**
