## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-world-001-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying continents.

### Enunciado
In which continent is **Colombia** located?

### Options
- [x] A) South America
- [ ] B) North America
- [ ] C) Europe
- [ ] D) Asia

### Explicación Pedagógica
Colombia is part of the **South American** continent (América del Sur).

Identifying hot and dry places.

### Enunciado
Choose the correct geographical feature.

### Options
- [x] A) desert
- [ ] B) island

Understanding time zones.

### Enunciado
When it is **12:00 PM** in Bogota, it is already night in London. This happens because of ________.

### Options
- [x] A) time zones
- [ ] B) the clouds
- [ ] C) the wind
- [ ] D) the moon's gravity

### Explicación Pedagógica
