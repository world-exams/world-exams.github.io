## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-media-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"I am boring with this movie."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect (if you mean you don't like it)?

### Options
- [x] A) Usually we say 'I am bored with/by' to express feeling. 'I am boring' means I am a boring person.
- [ ] B) It should be 'I am bore'.
- [ ] C) Movie is spelled wrong.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
"I am **bored**" means I feel boredom. "I am boring" means my personality causes boredom in others.
