## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-history-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"Who did broke the window?"

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) In subject questions (Who), we do not use 'did'. It should be 'Who broke the window?'.
- [ ] B) It should be 'Who does break'.
- [ ] C) Window is correct.
- [ ] D) 'Did' is always necessary.

### Explicación Pedagógica
When **Who** refers to the subject of the action, we do not use the auxiliary **did**. We use the past verb directly: "**Who broke** the window?".
