## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-tech-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"Did she went to the museum?"

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) After 'Did', we use the base verb (go), not the past form (went).
- [ ] B) It should be 'Does she went'.
- [ ] C) 'Museum' is spelled wrong.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
In questions with **Did**, the main verb stays in the base form: "Did she **go**...?".
