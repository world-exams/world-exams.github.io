# Base Question: Future Plans, Dreams, and Intentions

> **Source:** Colombia Aprende (Suggested Curriculum)
> **Topic:** Future Plans (Period 1)
> **Level:** A2/B1 (Intermediate)

---

## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-future-001-v1`

### Contexto
Expressing intent for the weekend.

### Enunciado
"I ________ visit my grandparents this Saturday." Choose the most common expression for planned future.

### Options
- [x] A) am going to
- [ ] B) going
- [ ] C) will to
- [ ] D) visited

### Explicación Pedagógica
We use **be going to** (voy a) for plans and intentions already decided.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-future-001-v9`

### Contexto
Analyzing a dialogue about plans.
A: "What are your plans for the summer vacation?"
B: "I'm not sure yet, but I think I ________ stay with my cousins in Cali."

### Enunciado
Why did person B use "will / think I will"?

### Options
- [x] A) Because it is not a 100% certain plan yet.
- [ ] B) Because it is a plan they already paid for.
- [ ] C) Because they are in Cali right now.
- [ ] D) Because they hate their cousins.

### Explicación Pedagógica
"I'm not sure" and "I think" indicate a **prediction or possibility**, which favors **will**.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

Interpreting a bucket list.
1. Visit Paris
2. Write a book

### Enunciado
Which of these is **NOT** a physical activity goal?

### Options
- [x] A) Write a book
- [ ] B) Run a marathon

Writing a book is an intellectual activity, while the others involve travel or physical movement.
