## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-future-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"I will to travel to space."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) After 'will', we use the base verb without 'to'.
- [ ] B) It should be 'I will traveling'.
- [ ] C) Travel is not a verb.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Modal verbs like **will** are followed directly by the base verb: "I will **travel**" (never 'to travel').
