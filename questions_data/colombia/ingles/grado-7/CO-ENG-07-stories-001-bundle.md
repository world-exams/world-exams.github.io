## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-stories-001-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying personal history.

### Enunciado
What is the word for the story of a person's life?

### Options
- [x] A) Biography
- [ ] B) Dictionary
- [ ] C) Newspaper
- [ ] D) Calendar

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **biography** (biografía) is a detailed account of a person's life.

## Question 2 (Muy Fácil B - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-stories-001-v2`

### Contexto
Past tense of "to be".

### Enunciado
"I ________ born in 2013." Choose the correct form of the verb "to be" in the past.

### Options
- [x] A) was
- [ ] B) were
- [ ] C) am
- [ ] D) is

### Explicación Pedagógica
For "I", the past form of "to be" is **was** (nací).

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-stories-001-v3`

### Contexto
Regular past tense verbs.

### Enunciado
"Yesterday, I ________ my grandmother and we talked for hours."

### Options
- [x] A) visited
- [ ] B) visit
- [ ] C) visiting
- [ ] D) visits

### Explicación Pedagógica
For regular verbs in the past tense, we usually add **-ed**: **visited** (visité).

## Question 4 (Fácil B - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-stories-001-v4`

### Contexto
Describing childhood activities.

### Enunciado
"When I was six years old, I ________ playing with dolls."

### Options
- [x] A) loved
- [ ] B) go
- [ ] C) am
- [ ] D) will

### Explicación Pedagógica
The context "When I was six" indicates a past state, so we use **loved** (amaba/encantaba).

## Question 6 (Media B - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-stories-001-v6`

### Contexto
Using time expressions.
"We moved to this house **three years ________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the word used to indicate how much time has passed since a past event.

### Options
- [x] A) ago
- [ ] B) before
- [ ] C) since
- [ ] D) for

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Ago** (hace) is placed after a duration of time to indicate a point in the past.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-stories-001-v7`

### Contexto
Analyzing an experience.
"Maria went to London last summer. She visited the Big Ben and ate fish and chips. It was an **unforgettable** trip."

### Enunciado
What does **unforgettable** mean?

### Options
- [x] A) Something you will always remember.
- [ ] B) Something that happens every day.
- [ ] C) Something very boring.
- [ ] D) Something you cannot see.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Unforgettable** (inolvidable) means so good or intense that it is always remembered.

### Contexto
Identifying major life events.

### Enunciado
Which of these is a typical **Life Milestone** (hito de vida)?

### Options
- [x] A) Graduating from school
- [ ] B) Eating a sandwich for lunch

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-stories-001-v9`

### Contexto
Understanding negative past sentences.
"I wanted to play football, but it ________ raining, so I stayed home."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct expression for a past continuous action.

### Options
- [x] A) started
- [ ] B) starts
- [ ] C) will start
- [ ] D) is starting

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Started** (empezó) fits the past context set by "wanted" and "stayed".

How much time passed between starting school and traveling to the USA?

From 2015 to 2020 is a duration of **five years**.
