## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-future-004-v7`

### Contexto
Will vs Be going to (Review).
"I **________** (have) a meeting with the manager at 3 PM." (Arrangement)
"I think I **________** (be) a famous manager one day." (Prediction)

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pair.

### Options
- [x] A) am having / will be
- [ ] B) will have / will be
- [ ] C) have / am being
- [ ] D) am going to have / am being

### Explicación Pedagógica
Arrangement/Plan: **Present Continuous/Going to**. Prediction: **Will**.
