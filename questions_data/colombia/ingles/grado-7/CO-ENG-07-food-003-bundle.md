## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-food-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"I have too many homework."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) 'Homework' is uncountable, so it should be 'too much homework'.
- [ ] B) It should be 'too many homeworks'.
- [ ] C) Homework is fun, not too much.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Homework** is uncountable in English. We say **too much homework**, never "too many" or "homeworks".
