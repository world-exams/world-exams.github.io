## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-intro-002-v1`

### Contexto

"Hello! My name **________** Sara."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct verb.

### Options
- [x] A) is
- [ ] B) are
- [ ] C) am
- [ ] D) be

### Explicación Pedagógica
"My name" is singular (it) -> **is**.

## Question 8 (Difícil B - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-intro-002-v8`

### Contexto
Possessive adjectives.
"We have a dog. **________** name is Max."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct adjective.

### Options
- [x] A) Its
- [ ] B) His
- [ ] C) Our
- [ ] D) Their

### Explicación Pedagógica
Refers to the dog's name -> **Its**. (Note: "Our" could refer to the dog belonging to us, but usually "Its name" refers to the dog itself in this context, though "Our" is grammatically possible if referring to the dog. Standard answer "Its" for animal names unless gendered). *Self-correction*: If the sentence is "We have a dog. ___ name is Max.", "Its" is the most standard possessive for an animal.
