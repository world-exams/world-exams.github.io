## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-sports-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"You mustn't to smoke here."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) After 'mustn't', we use the base verb without 'to'.
- [ ] B) It should be 'mustn't smoking'.
- [ ] C) Smoke is allowed everywhere.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Modal verbs (must/mustn't) are followed by the base verb without 'to': "You mustn't **smoke**" (never 'to smoke').
