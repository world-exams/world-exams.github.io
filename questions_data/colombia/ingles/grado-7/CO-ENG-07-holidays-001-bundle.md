## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-holidays-001-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying common holiday greetings.

### Enunciado
What do people usually say to wish others a happy Christmas?

### Options
- [x] A) Merry Christmas!
- [ ] B) Happy Birthday!
- [ ] C) Good Luck!
- [ ] D) Nice to meet you!

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Merry Christmas** (Feliz Navidad) is the standard greeting for the holiday.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-holidays-001-v9`

### Contexto
Analyzing the meaning of a festival.
"The **Flower Festival** (Feria de las Flores) in Medellín honors the work of 'Silleteros'."

### Enunciado
What does this festival celebrate besides flowers?

### Options
- [x] A) Regional identity and agricultural work.
- [ ] B) The invention of the car.
- [ ] C) Modern technology and apps.
- [ ] D) Snow sports and winter.

### Explicación Pedagógica
