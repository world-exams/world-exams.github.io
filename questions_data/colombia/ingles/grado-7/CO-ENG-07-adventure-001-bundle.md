**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-adventure-001-v1`

### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) A tent
- [ ] B) A bicycle
- [ ] C) A surfboard
- [ ] D) A guitar

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **tent** (carpa) is the portable shelter used for camping.
