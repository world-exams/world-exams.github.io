## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-project-004-v9`

### Contexto
Review: Reading Comprehension.
"I have always enjoyed adventures. Last summer, I went to the Tayrona Park where I spent three days. I am going to return next year because I haven't seen everything yet. I should invite my friends to come with me next time."

### Enunciado
Why is the author going back next year?

### Options
- [x] A) Because they haven't seen everything yet.
- [ ] B) because they enjoyed the winter.
- [ ] C) because they want to invite friends now.
- [ ] D) because it is a tradition whose name is adventure.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text says: "I am going to return next year **because I haven't seen everything yet**."
