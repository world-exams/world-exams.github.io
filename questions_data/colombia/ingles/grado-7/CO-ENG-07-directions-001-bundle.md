## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-directions-001-v1`

### Contexto
Basic directional vocabulary.

### Enunciado
What does **"Go straight ahead"** mean when giving directions?

### Options
- [x] A) Continue walking in the same direction without turning.
- [ ] B) Turn left at the next corner.
- [ ] C) Go back to the beginning.
- [ ] D) Stop and wait for the bus.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Go straight ahead** (sigue derecho) means to maintain the current path.
