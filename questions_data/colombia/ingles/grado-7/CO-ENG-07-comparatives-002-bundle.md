> **Level:** A2 (Elementary)

## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-comparatives-002-v1`

### Contexto
Comparative (Short adjectives).
"An elephant is **________** than a dog."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct adjective form.

### Options
- [x] A) bigger
- [ ] B) big
- [ ] C) more big
- [ ] D) biggest

### Explicación Pedagógica
Short adjective + vowel-consonant -> double consonant + **er** (bigger).

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-comparatives-002-v10`

### Contexto
Correcting a sentence.
"He is the most happier person I know."

### Enunciado
What is the correct form?

### Options
- [x] A) He is the happiest person I know.
- [ ] B) He is the most happy person I know.
- [ ] C) He is the happyest person I know.
- [ ] D) He is more happy person I know.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Adjectives ending in 'y' change to **-iest** (happiest). Don't use "most".
