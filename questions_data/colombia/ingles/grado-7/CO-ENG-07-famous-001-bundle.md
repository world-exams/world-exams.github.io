## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-famous-001-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying iconic figures.

### Enunciado
Gabriel García Márquez was a famous Colombian ________ who won the Nobel Prize.

### Options
- [x] A) writer
- [ ] B) singer
- [ ] C) pilot
- [ ] D) chef

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Gabriel García Márquez** is the most famous Colombian **writer** (escritor), known for magical realism.
