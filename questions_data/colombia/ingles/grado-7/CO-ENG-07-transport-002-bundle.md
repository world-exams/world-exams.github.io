## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-transport-002-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying vehicles.
"It has two wheels and no engine. You ride it."

### Enunciado
What is it?

### Options
- [x] A) bicycle
- [ ] B) car
- [ ] C) bus
- [ ] D) train

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Bicycle** (bicicleta) has two wheels and no engine.

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-transport-002-v3`

### Contexto
Prepositions of transport.
"I go to school **________** bus."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct preposition.

### Options
- [x] A) by
- [ ] B) in
- [ ] C) on
- [ ] D) with

### Explicación Pedagógica
We use "**by**" with general modes of transport (by bus, by car, by plane).

## Question 6 (Media B - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-transport-002-v6`

### Contexto
Verb usage (Drive vs Ride).
"I can **________** a motorcycle."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct verb.

### Options
- [x] A) ride
- [ ] B) drive
- [ ] C) go
- [ ] D) conduct

### Explicación Pedagógica
We "**ride**" open vehicles like bikes/motorcycles. We "drive" enclosed ones like cars.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-transport-002-v10`

### Contexto
Correcting a sentence.
"I drive my bicycle every day."

### Enunciado
What is the correct form?

### Options
- [x] A) I ride my bicycle every day.
- [ ] B) I conduct my bicycle every day.
- [ ] C) I go my bicycle every day.
- [ ] D) I travel my bicycle every day.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Collocation error: You **ride** a bicycle, not drive.
