## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-nature-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"She is the most happiest girl."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) You cannot use 'most' and '-est' together. It should be 'happiest'.
- [ ] B) 'Happy' is a long adjective.
- [ ] C) It should be 'most happy'.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
We do not use double superlatives. "Happy" becomes **happiest**. "Most happiest" is incorrect.
