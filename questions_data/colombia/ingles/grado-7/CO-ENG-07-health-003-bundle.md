### Options

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-health-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"You should to see a doctor."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) After 'should', we use the base verb without 'to'.
- [ ] B) It should be 'should seeing'.
- [ ] C) Doctor is not a verb.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Modal verbs like **should** are followed directly by the base verb: "You should **see**" (never 'to see').
