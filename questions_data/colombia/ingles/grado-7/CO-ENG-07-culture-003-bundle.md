### Options

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-culture-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"Did she enjoyed the party?"

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) After 'Did', we use the base verb (enjoy), not the past (enjoyed).
- [ ] B) It should be 'Does she enjoyed'.
- [ ] C) Party is wrong spelling.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
In Past Simple questions with **Did**, the main verb must be in the base form: "Did she **enjoy**...?".
