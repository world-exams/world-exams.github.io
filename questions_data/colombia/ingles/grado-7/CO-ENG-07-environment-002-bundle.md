## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-environment-002-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary.
"We put trash in the **________** can."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct object.

### Options
- [x] A) garbage (or trash)
- [ ] B) water
- [ ] C) tree
- [ ] D) car

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Garbage can** (basurero) is for trash.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-environment-002-v9`

### Contexto
Reading a sign.
"Sign: 'Keep off the grass'."
What does this mean?

### Enunciado
Choose the correct meaning.

### Options
- [x] A) You shouldn't walk on the grass.
- [ ] B) You should cut the grass.
- [ ] C) You must plant more grass.
- [ ] D) The grass is dangerous.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-environment-002-v10`

### Contexto
Correcting a sentence.
"You should to plant a tree."

### Enunciado
What is the correct form?

### Options
- [x] A) You should plant a tree.
- [ ] B) You should planting a tree.
- [ ] C) You should planted a tree.
- [ ] D) You should plants a tree.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Modals like **should** are followed by the base verb without "to" (should plant).
