### Enunciado

### Options

- [ ] B) soap

### Explicación Pedagógica

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-body-004-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Dealing with a nosebleed:
1. Sit the person down.
2. Tell them to lean forward, not backward.
3. Pinch the soft part of the nose for 10 minutes.
4. Don't let them blow their nose for a few hours."

### Enunciado
Should the person lean backward during a nosebleed?

### Options
- [x] A) No, they should lean forward.
- [ ] B) Yes, it stops the blood.
- [ ] C) Yes, for 10 minutes.
- [ ] D) No, they should blow their nose.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text says: "Tell them to **lean forward, not backward**."
