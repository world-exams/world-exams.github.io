## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-nature-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"She is happyer than me."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) 'Happy' ends in 'y', so it changes to 'happier'.
- [ ] B) 'Happy' is a long adjective, so it is 'more happy'.
- [ ] C) It should be 'happiest'.
- [ ] D) She cannot be happy.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Adjectives ending in consonant + 'y' change the 'y' to 'i' and add '-er': **happier**. Speling "happyer" is wrong.
