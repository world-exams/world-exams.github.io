# Base Question: What's the weather like?

> **Source:** DBA Inglés (Adapted for Colombia)
> **Topic:** Seasons & Preferences (Period 4)
> **Level:** A1.2 (Beginner)

---

## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-weather-004-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Weather.
"When the sun is shining, it is **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct adjective.

### Options
- [x] A) sunny
- [ ] B) rainy
- [ ] C) snowy
- [ ] D) cloudy

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Sunny** (soleado) describes weather with sunshine.

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-weather-004-v3`

### Contexto
Like + -ing (Affirmative).
"I **________** (like / swim) in summer."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct form.

### Options
- [x] A) like swimming
- [ ] B) likes swimming
- [ ] C) like swim
- [ ] D) liking swim

### Explicación Pedagógica
After verbs of preference (like, love, hate), use the **-ing** form.

### Contexto
Like + -ing (3rd Person Singular).
"She **________** (love / walk) in the rain."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct form for 'She'.

### Options
- [x] A) loves walking
- [ ] B) love walking

## Question 5 (Media A - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-weather-004-v5`

### Contexto
Like + -ing (Negative).
"We **________** (not / like / feel) cold."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct negative form.

### Options
- [x] A) don't like feeling
- [ ] B) doesn't like feeling
- [ ] C) not like feeling
- [ ] D) aren't like feeling

### Explicación Pedagógica
Negative present simple: **don't/doesn't like + verb-ing**. 'We' takes 'don't'.

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-weather-004-v7`

### Contexto
Weather Nouns vs Adjectives.
"There is a lot of **________** (wind) today. It is very **________** (wind)."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pair (Noun, Adjective).

### Options
- [x] A) wind / windy
- [ ] B) windy / wind
- [ ] C) winds / winding
- [ ] D) winded / windy

### Explicación Pedagógica
Noun: **wind** (viento). Adjective: **windy** (ventoso).

## Question 8 (Difícil B - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-weather-004-v8`

### Contexto
Spelling rules (-ing with 'e').
"They hate **________** (drive) in the fog."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct spelling of 'drive' + -ing.

### Options
- [x] A) driving
- [ ] B) driveing
- [ ] C) drivin
- [ ] D) drivving

### Explicación Pedagógica
Verbs ending in 'e': drop the 'e' and add -ing (drive -> **driving**).

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-weather-004-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"In Colombia, we don't have four seasons like winter or summer. We have rainy seasons and dry seasons. I love visiting Cartagena when it is hot and dry because I like going to the beach. I don't like it when it rains because I hate staying inside."

### Enunciado
Why does the author like visiting Cartagena when it is hot?

### Options
- [x] A) Because they like going to the beach.
- [ ] B) Because they hate the beach.
- [ ] C) Because they have four seasons.
- [ ] D) Because they like rain.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text says: "I love visiting Cartagena when it is hot... **because I like going to the beach**."

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-weather-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"listening / likes / music / she / to"

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a sentence.

### Options
- [x] A) She likes listening to music.
- [ ] B) She listening likes to music.
- [ ] C) Likes she listening to music.
- [ ] D) She likes to listening music.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Word order: **Subject + verb (likes) + verb-ing (listening) + complement.**
