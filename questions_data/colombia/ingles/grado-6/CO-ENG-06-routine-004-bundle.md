## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-routine-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"homework / do / often / they / their / evening / the / in"

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a sentence.

### Options
- [x] A) They often do their homework in the evening.
- [ ] B) Often they do their homework in the evening.
- [ ] C) They do often their homework in the evening.
- [ ] D) In the evening they often do homework their.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **Subject + adverb + verb + object + time phrase.**
