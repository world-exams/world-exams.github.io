## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-sports-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"I can to speak English."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) After 'can', we use the base verb without 'to'.
- [ ] B) 'Speak' is not a verb.
- [ ] C) It should be 'I cans'.
- [ ] D) English is a difficult language.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The modal verb **Can** is followed directly by the base verb: "I can **speak**" (never "can to speak").
