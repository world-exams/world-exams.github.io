## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-neighborhood-001-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying where you live.

### Enunciado
What word describes the area around your house and the people who live near you?

### Options
- [x] A) Neighborhood
- [ ] B) Country
- [ ] C) Classroom
- [ ] D) Office

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **neighborhood** (barrio/vecindario) is the local area surrounding your home.

Inference from a description.
"I live in a quiet neighborhood. My house is **behind** the library and **opposite** the bakery."

### Enunciado
If you are standing at the bakery and looking at the street, what is in front of you?

### Options
- [x] A) The narrator's house
- [ ] B) The library
- [ ] C) A noisy factory
- [ ] D) The school bus

### Explicación Pedagógica
"Opposite" means facing. So the house is facing the bakery.
