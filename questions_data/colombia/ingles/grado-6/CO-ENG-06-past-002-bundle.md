# Base Question: Where Were You?

> **Source:** Nigeria Curriculum (Adapted for Colombia)
> **Topic:** Simple Past (Verb To Be) (Period 2)
> **Level:** A1 (Beginner)

---

## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-past-002-v1`

### Contexto
Describing past location.
"Yesterday I **________** at school."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct verb.

### Options
- [x] A) was
- [ ] B) were
- [ ] C) am
- [ ] D) is

### Explicación Pedagógica
For "I", the past of "am" is "**was**".

- [ ] B) You is late yesterday.
