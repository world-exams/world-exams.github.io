## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-descriptions-001-v1`

### Contexto
Describing general physical size.

### Enunciado
If a house has 10 bedrooms and a very large garden, we say the house is ________.

### Options
- [x] A) big
- [ ] B) small
- [ ] C) thin
- [ ] D) short

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Big** (grande) describes objects with significant size or volume.
