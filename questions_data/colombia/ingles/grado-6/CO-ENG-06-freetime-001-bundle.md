## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-freetime-001-v1`

### Contexto
General term for things you like doing.

### Enunciado
What word do we use for activities that people do for pleasure in their free time?

### Options
- [x] A) Hobbies
- [ ] B) Homework
- [ ] C) Exams
- [ ] D) Jobs

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Hobbies** (pasatiempos) are activities done for enjoyment during leisure time.

## Question 5 (Media A - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-freetime-001-v5`

### Contexto
Using "go" vs "do" with activities.

### Enunciado
"On weekends, I ________ yoga and my brother ________ swimming."

### Options
- [x] A) do / goes
- [ ] B) go / does
- [ ] C) am / is
- [ ] D) play / plays

### Explicación Pedagógica
We use **do** (hacer) for individual non-ball sports like yoga/karate, and **go** (ir) for activities ending in -ing like swimming.
