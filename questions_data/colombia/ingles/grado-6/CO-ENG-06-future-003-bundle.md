## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-future-003-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Planning.
"On Saturday and Sunday, we don't go to school. It is the **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct word for Saturday and Sunday.

### Options
- [x] A) weekend
- [ ] B) weekday
- [ ] C) workday
- [ ] D) birthday

### Explicación Pedagógica
The **weekend** (fin de semana) is Saturday and Sunday.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-future-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"I going to play soccer."

### Enunciado
What is missing in this sentence?

### Options
- [x] A) The verb 'am' (I **am** going to...).
- [ ] B) The verb 'will' (I **will** going to...).
- [ ] C) The verb 'is' (I **is** going to...).
- [ ] D) Nothing, it is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The structure is **Subject + Be + going to**. We cannot omit the verb 'be' (am).
