## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-community-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"The cat is inside of the box."

### Enunciado
Which word is usually unnecessary here?

### Options
- [x] A) 'Of' (The cat is inside the box).
- [ ] B) 'The' (Cat is inside of box).
- [ ] C) 'Is' (The cat inside of the box).
- [ ] D) 'Box' is not a place.

### Explicación Pedagógica
We usually say "**inside** the box" (without 'of'), although 'inside of' is possible but less common/more informal depending on context. For A1 level, "inside the box" is standard. A better answer might be that "in" is simpler: "The cat is **in** the box". But focusing on the options, removing "of" makes it standard.
