## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-animals-002-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying farm animals.
"The **________** gives us milk and says 'Moo'."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct animal.

### Options
- [x] A) cow
- [ ] B) chicken
- [ ] C) horse
- [ ] D) pig

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Cow** (vaca) is the animal associated with milk and "Moo".

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-animals-002-v10`

### Contexto
Correcting a sentence about ability.
"She cans play the guitar very well."

### Enunciado
What is the correct form?

### Options
- [x] A) She can play the guitar very well.
- [ ] B) She can plays the guitar very well.
- [ ] C) She cans plays the guitar very well.
- [ ] D) She is can play the guitar very well.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The modal verb "**can**" never takes an **-s**, and the following verb is always in the base form.
