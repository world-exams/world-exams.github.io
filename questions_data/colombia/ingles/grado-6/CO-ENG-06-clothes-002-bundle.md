## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-clothes-002-v10`

### Contexto
Constructing a sentence about clothing.
"wearing / are / blue / They / jeans."

### Enunciado
Organize the sentence.

### Options
- [x] A) They are wearing blue jeans.
- [ ] B) They wearing are blue jeans.
- [ ] C) Blue jeans are they wearing.
- [ ] D) They blue jeans wearing are.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Sentence structure: Subject + Be + Verb-ing + Object (They + are + wearing + blue jeans).
