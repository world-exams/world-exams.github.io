## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-town-002-v1`

### Contexto
Buying food.
"I want to buy bread. I go to the **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct place.

### Options
- [x] A) bakery
- [ ] B) library
- [ ] C) hospital
- [ ] D) park

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Bakery** (panadería) is where bread is sold.

### Enunciado
What is missing?

### Options
- [x] A) of (in front **of**)
- [ ] B) to (in front **to**)
- [ ] C) at (in front **at**)
- [ ] D) on (in front **on**)

### Explicación Pedagógica
The correct prepositional phrase is "**in front of**".
