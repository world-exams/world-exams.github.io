## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-school-004-v7`

### Contexto
Differences: Must vs Should.
"It's raining. You **________** take an umbrella. (Advice)"
"The exam starts at 8:00. You **________** be late. (Prohibition)"

### Enunciado
Choose the correct pair.

### Options
- [x] A) should / mustn't
- [ ] B) must / should
- [ ] C) can / do
- [ ] D) should / must

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Should** for advice. **Mustn't** for prohibition.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-school-004-v9`

Reading comprehension.

2. Students must not use cellphones in class.
3. Students must listen to the teacher.
4. Students must bring their books every day."

### Enunciado
What is forbidden in class?

### Options
- [x] A) Using cellphones.
- [ ] B) Listening to the teacher.

- [ ] D) Bringing books.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The rule says: "Students **must not use cellphones**."

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-school-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"in / must / garbage / the / put / you / bin / the"

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a rule.

### Options
- [x] A) You must put the garbage in the bin.
- [ ] B) Must you put the garbage in the bin.
- [ ] C) In the bin you must put garbage.
- [ ] D) Garbage you must put in the bin.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **Subject + modal + verb + object + place.**
