# Base Question: Making Questions with Wh- Words

> **Source:** Nigeria Curriculum (Adapted for Colombia)
> **Topic:** Wh- Questions (Period 2)
> **Level:** A1 (Beginner)

---

## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-questions-002-v1`

### Contexto
Asking about a thing.
"**________** is your name?"

### Enunciado
Choose the correct question word.

### Options
- [x] A) What
- [ ] B) Who
- [ ] C) When
- [ ] D) Where

### Explicación Pedagógica
"**What**" (qué/cuál) asks about things or names.
