# Base Question: Telling the Time and School Schedules

> **Source:** Colombia Aprende (Suggested Curriculum)
> **Topic:** Time and Schedules (Period 1)
> **Level:** A2 (Elementary)

---

## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-time-001-v1`

### Contexto
Basic clock reading.

### Enunciado
If the long hand is on 12 and the short hand is on 3, what time is it?

### Options
- [x] A) It's three o'clock.
- [ ] B) It's twelve o'clock.
- [ ] C) It's three thirty.
- [ ] D) It's fifteen minutes past twelve.

### Explicación Pedagógica
When the long hand is on 12, it is the top of the hour (**o'clock**).

## Question 2 (Muy Fácil B - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-time-001-v2`

### Contexto
Identifying AM/PM.

### Enunciado
We use **AM** for ________ and **PM** for ________.

### Options
- [x] A) morning / afternoon and night
- [ ] B) night / morning
- [ ] C) lunch / dinner
- [ ] D) weekend / weekdays

### Explicación Pedagógica
**AM** (Ante Meridiem) is for the morning, and **PM** (Post Meridiem) is for afternoon and evening.

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-time-001-v3`

### Contexto
Half past the hour.

### Enunciado
"It's **seven thirty**." What is another way to say this time?

### Options
- [x] A) It's half past seven.
- [ ] B) It's quarter past seven.
- [ ] C) It's seven o'clock.
- [ ] D) It's seven fifteen.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Half past** (media pasada) is the standard way to express 30 minutes past the hour.

## Question 4 (Fácil B - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-time-001-v4`

### Contexto
Quarter past.

### Enunciado
"It's **ten fifteen**." Choose the synonymous expression.

### Options
- [x] A) It's a quarter past ten.
- [ ] B) It's a quarter to ten.
- [ ] C) It's ten forty-five.
- [ ] D) It's ten o'clock.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**A quarter past** (un cuarto después de) is 15 minutes past the hour.

## Question 6 (Media B - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-time-001-v6`

### Contexto
Quarter to the hour.
"It's **1:45 PM**."

### Enunciado
How do you say 1:45 using "quarter to"?

### Options
- [x] A) It's a quarter to two.
- [ ] B) It's a quarter past one.
- [ ] C) It's a quarter to one.
- [ ] D) It's half past two.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**A quarter to two** (un cuarto para las dos) means 15 minutes remain until 2:00.

Frequency and scheduling.

If a bus comes at 6:45, when is the next bus?

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-time-001-v8`

### Contexto
Understanding duration.
"The movie starts at 8:00 and ends at 9:45."

### Enunciado
How long is the movie?

### Options
- [x] A) One hour and forty-five minutes.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-time-001-v9`

### Contexto
Analyzing a complicated schedule.
"I work from **9:00 to 12:00**. I have a **one-hour break**. Then I work from **1:00 to 5:00**."

### Enunciado
How many total hours do I work in a day?

### Options
- [x] A) Seven hours
- [ ] B) Eight hours
- [ ] C) Nine hours
- [ ] D) Six hours

### Explicación Pedagógica
(9:00 to 12:00 = 3 hours) + (1:00 to 5:00 = 4 hours) = **7 hours**. The break hour is not working time.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-time-001-v10`

### Contexto
Digital vs Analog time.

### Enunciado
On a digital clock, **"Midnight"** is represented as ________.

### Options
- [x] A) 00:00 (or 12:00 AM)
- [ ] B) 12:00 PM
- [ ] C) 24:00 AM
- [ ] D) 06:00

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Midnight** (medianoche) is 00:00 or 12:00 AM.
