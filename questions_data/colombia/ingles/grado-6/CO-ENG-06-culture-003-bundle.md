## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-culture-003-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Celebrations.
"This is a special day. You have cake and balloons. It is your **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct celebration.

### Options
- [x] A) birthday
- [ ] B) school day
- [ ] C) doctor visit
- [ ] D) exam

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **birthday** (cumpleaños) is celebrated with cake and balloons.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-culture-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"She is danceing very well."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) 'Dance' ends in 'e', so we drop the 'e' before adding '-ing' (dancing).
- [ ] B) It should be 'dances'.
- [ ] C) Dance is not a verb.
- [ ] D) It is correct.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Verbs ending in 'e' drop the 'e': **dancing** (not danceing).
