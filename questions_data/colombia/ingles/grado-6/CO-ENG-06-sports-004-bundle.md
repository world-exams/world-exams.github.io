## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-sports-004-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Sports equipment.
"To play soccer, you need a ball and a big field with two **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct word.

### Options
- [x] A) goals
- [ ] B) kitchens
- [ ] C) cars
- [ ] D) pools

### Explicación Pedagógica
In soccer, you score by putting the ball into the **goals** (porterías/arcos).

### Options
- [x] A) him

### Enunciado

### Options
- [x] A) She / me

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

Reading comprehension.
"Our school basketball team is playing today. I am going to watch them. My brother plays in the team. I bought a new jersey for him. He loves it! We hope they win."

### Enunciado
Who did the author buy a jersey for?

### Options
- [x] A) For his/her brother.
- [ ] B) For the whole team.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

Sentence Order.

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a request.

### Options
- [x] A) Can you help me?
- [ ] B) Can me help you?
- [ ] C) You can help me?
- [ ] D) Me can help you?

### Explicación Pedagógica
