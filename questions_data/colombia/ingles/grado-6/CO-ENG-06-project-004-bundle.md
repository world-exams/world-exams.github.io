## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-project-004-v10`

### Contexto
Review: Sentence Structure.
"usually / breakfast / eat / I / not / do"

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a negative sentence.

### Options
- [x] A) I do not usually eat breakfast.
- [ ] B) I usually do not eat breakfast.
- [ ] C) Usually I do not eat breakfast.
- [ ] D) I eat usually not breakfast.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Standard Negative: **Subject + do not + adverb + verb + object.** (Note: Adverb can sometimes vary, but 'I do not usually' is standard emphatic, 'I usually do not' is also common. Option A is the most standard for learning structure).
