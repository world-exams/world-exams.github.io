## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-health-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"No smoke here, please."

### Enunciado
What is the correct way to say this command?

### Options
- [x] A) Don't smoke here, please.
- [ ] B) Not smoke here.
- [ ] C) No smoking is correct.
- [ ] D) Smoke not here.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The imperative form is "**Don't** + verb" (Don't smoke). "No smoking" is a noun phrase sign, but as a command to a person, "Don't smoke" is better grammar for this level.
