> **Topic:** Food and Likes (Period 2)
> **Level:** A1 (Beginner)

## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-food-002-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying fruits.
"The **________** is a red fruit. It is sweet and crunchy."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct fruit.

### Options
- [x] A) apple
- [ ] B) potato
- [ ] C) chicken
- [ ] D) bread

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Apple** (manzana) fits the description of a red, sweet fruit.

Identifying drinks.
"I am thirsty. I want to drink a glass of **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct drink.

### Options
- [x] A) water (or milk)
- [ ] B) bread
- [ ] C) pizza

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-food-002-v3`

Expressing likes.
"I **________** pizza. It is delicious!"

### Enunciado
Choose the correct verb.

### Options
- [x] A) like (or love)
- [ ] B) hate
- [ ] C) am

### Explicación Pedagógica
"**Like**" (gustar) expresses a positive preference.

## Question 4 (Fácil B - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-food-002-v4`

### Contexto
Expressing dislikes.
"My brother **________** broccoli. He says it's yucky."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct negative form.

### Options
- [x] A) doesn't like
- [ ] B) don't like
- [ ] C) not like
- [ ] D) isn't like

### Explicación Pedagógica
For he/she/it, we use "**doesn't like**" (no le gusta).

## Question 6 (Media B - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-food-002-v6`

### Contexto
Countable vs. Uncountable.
"I want **________** apple, please."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct article.

### Options
- [x] A) an
- [ ] B) a
- [ ] C) some
- [ ] D) two

### Explicación Pedagógica
We use "**an**" before singular nouns starting with a vowel sound (apple).

Using "Any" in questions.

### Enunciado

### Options

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-food-002-v10`

### Contexto
Constructing a sentence with frequency and preference.
"She / eat / never / sushi."

### Enunciado
Organize the sentence.

### Options
- [x] A) She never eats sushi.
- [ ] B) She eats never sushi.
- [ ] C) Never she eats sushi.
- [ ] D) She sushi eats never.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Adverbs of frequency (never) go **before** the main verb (eats).
