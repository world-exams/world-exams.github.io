## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-family-002-v1`

### Contexto
Identifying family members.
"My mother's mother is my **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct family member.

### Options
- [x] A) grandmother
- [ ] B) aunt
- [ ] C) sister
- [ ] D) daughter

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Grandmother** (abuela) is the mother of your mother.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

Reading a description of a family photo.
"In this photo, the man with the beard stands behind the boy with glasses. The woman is sitting on a chair."

### Enunciado
Where is the man?

### Options

- [ ] B) Sitting on a chair.

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-family-002-v10`

### Contexto
Correcting a sentence description.
"She is long hair and blue eyes."

### Enunciado
What is the correct form?

### Options
- [x] A) She has long hair and blue eyes.
- [ ] B) She is long hair and has blue eyes.
- [ ] C) She has long hair and is blue eyes.
- [ ] D) She contains long hair and blue eyes.

### Explicación Pedagógica
We use "**has**" for body parts/features (hair, eyes), not "is".
