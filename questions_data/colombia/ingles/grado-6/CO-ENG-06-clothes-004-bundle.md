# Base Question: What are they wearing?

> **Source:** DBA Inglés (Adapted for Colombia)
> **Topic:** Fashion & Style (Period 4)
> **Level:** A1.2 (Beginner)

---

## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-clothes-004-v1`

### Contexto
Vocabulary: Clothing.
"You wear this on your head to protect yourself from the sun. It is a **________**."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct word.

### Options
- [x] A) hat
- [ ] B) shoe
- [ ] C) sock
- [ ] D) belt

### Explicación Pedagógica
A **hat** (sombrero/gorra) is worn on the head.

## Question 3 (Fácil A - Difficulty 2)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-clothes-004-v3`

### Contexto
Present Continuous (Affirmative).
"She **________** (wear) a beautiful red dress today."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct form of the verb.

### Options
- [x] A) is wearing
- [ ] B) wearing
- [ ] C) are wearing
- [ ] D) wears

### Explicación Pedagógica
Present Continuous: Subject + **am/is/are** + verb-ing. 'She' takes 'is'.

Present Continuous (Negative).
"I **________** (not / buy) new pants right now."

### Enunciado

### Options

## Question 5 (Media A - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-clothes-004-v5`

### Contexto
Present Continuous (Question).
"**________** (they / shop) for winter clothes?"

### Enunciado
Choose the correct question form.

### Options
- [x] A) Are they shopping
- [ ] B) Is they shopping
- [ ] C) Do they shopping
- [ ] D) Are they shop

### Explicación Pedagógica
Question: **Am/Is/Are** + Subject + verb-ing? 'They' takes 'Are'. Note: 'shop' doubles the 'p' -> 'shopping'.

## Question 6 (Media B - Difficulty 3)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-clothes-004-v6`

### Contexto
Present Continuous vs Present Simple.
"Normally I wear jeans, but today I **________** (wear) a skirt."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct form for a temporary action happening today.

### Options
- [x] A) am wearing
- [ ] B) wear
- [ ] C) wearing
- [ ] D) wears

### Explicación Pedagógica
Use **Present Continuous** (am wearing) for actions happening now/today, contrasting with habits (wear).

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-clothes-004-v7`

### Contexto
Spelling rules (-ing).
"He is **________** (put) on his jacket."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct spelling.

### Options
- [x] A) putting
- [ ] B) puting
- [ ] C) puttin
- [ ] D) puts

### Explicación Pedagógica
For short verbs ending in CVC (Consonant-Vowel-Consonant), double the last consonant: put -> **putting**.

## Question 9 (Muy Difícil A - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-clothes-004-v9`

### Contexto
Reading comprehension.
"Look at the photo. Sarah is wearing a yellow raincoat and boots because it is raining. Her brother, Tom, isn't wearing a coat; he is just wearing a T-shirt. He looks cold!"

### Enunciado
Why is Sarah wearing a raincoat?

### Options
- [x] A) Because it is raining.
- [ ] B) Because she likes yellow.
- [ ] C) Because Tom is cold.
- [ ] D) Because she is swimming.

### Explicación Pedagógica
The text states: "Sarah is wearing a yellow raincoat... **because it is raining**."

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-clothes-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"wearing / today / ? / what / you / are"

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a correct question.

### Options
- [x] A) What are you wearing today?
- [ ] B) What you are wearing today?
- [ ] C) Are you wearing what today?
- [ ] D) Today you are wearing what?

### Explicación Pedagógica
Word order: **Wh-word + am/is/are + subject + verb-ing + complement?**
