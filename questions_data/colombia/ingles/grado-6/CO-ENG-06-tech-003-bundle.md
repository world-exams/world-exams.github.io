## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-tech-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"She watchs TV every day."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) The spelling involves adding '-es' (watches), not just 's'.
- [ ] B) It should be 'watching'.
- [ ] C) She don't watch TV.
- [ ] D) TV is not a verb.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Verbs ending in -ch add **-es** for the third person singular: **watches**.
