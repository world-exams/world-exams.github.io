## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-environment-001-v1`

### Contexto
The three R's.

### Enunciado
What are the three R's for taking care of the planet?

### Options
- [x] A) Reduce, Reuse, Recycle
- [ ] B) Read, Run, Rest
- [ ] C) Rice, Red, River
- [ ] D) Rain, Road, Roof

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle** (Reducir, Reutilizar, Reciclar) are the fundamental actions for environmental care.
