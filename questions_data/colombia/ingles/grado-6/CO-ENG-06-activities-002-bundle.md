# Base Question: What Are They Doing?

> **Source:** Nigeria Curriculum (Adapted for Colombia)
> **Topic:** Present Continuous (Period 2)
> **Level:** A1 (Beginner)

---

## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-activities-002-v1`

### Contexto
Describing an action.
"I am **________** a book right now."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct verb form.

### Options
- [x] A) reading
- [ ] B) read
- [ ] C) reads
- [ ] D) reader

### Explicación Pedagógica
We use **-ing** (reading) after "am" for present continuous.

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-activities-002-v7`

Contrast with Simple Present.

### Enunciado
Choose the correct form.

### Options
- [x] A) am taking
- [ ] B) take

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-activities-002-v10`

### Contexto
Correcting a sentence.
"Why you are crying?"

### Enunciado
What is the correct question structure?

### Options
- [x] A) Why are you crying?
- [ ] B) Why do you crying?
- [ ] C) Why you crying?
- [ ] D) Why is you crying?

### Explicación Pedagógica
In Wh- questions, verb 'be' comes before the subject: **Why are you...?**
