## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-food-004-v7`

### Contexto
Do you like vs Would you like.
A: "Do you like fish?" (General preference)
B: "Yes, I do."
A: "Would you like some fish?" (Offer now)
B: "No, thank you. I'm not hungry."

### Enunciado
Select the sentence that asks for a general preference.

### Options
- [x] A) Do you like fish?
- [ ] B) Would you like some fish?
- [ ] C) I would like fish.
- [ ] D) I am liking fish.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Do you like...?** asks about general tastes. **Would you like...?** is an offer for right now.
