## Question 1 (Muy Fácil A - Difficulty 1)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-health-001-v1`

### Contexto
Healthy food choices.

### Enunciado
Which of these foods is the healthiest for a snack?

### Options
- [x] A) An apple
- [ ] B) A bag of chips
- [ ] C) A chocolate bar
- [ ] D) A soda

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Fruits** like apples are the healthiest snack choice among the options.
