Superlatives (Short adjectives).
"The cheetah is the **________** (fast) land animal."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct superlative form.

### Options
- [x] A) fastest
- [ ] B) faster

- [ ] D) most fast

## Question 7 (Difícil A - Difficulty 4)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-animals-004-v7`

### Contexto
Superlative vs Comparative.
"The elephant is **________** (heavy) than the hippo, but the blue whale is the **________** (heavy)."

### Enunciado
Choose the correct forms.

### Options
- [x] A) heavier / heaviest
- [ ] B) heaviest / heavier
- [ ] C) heavyer / heavyest
- [ ] D) more heavy / most heavy

### Explicación Pedagógica
Comparative: **heavier** (two things). Superlative: **heaviest** (one thing above all).

## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-animals-004-v10`

### Contexto
Sentence Order.
"animal / is / dangerous / the / most / hippo / the"

### Enunciado
Order the words to form a sentence.

### Options
- [x] A) The hippo is the most dangerous animal.
- [ ] B) The animal is the most dangerous hippo.
- [ ] C) Dangerous is the most animal hippo.
- [ ] D) The most dangerous is the animal hippo.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Structure: **Subject (The hippo) + verb (is) + superlative (the most dangerous) + noun (animal).**
