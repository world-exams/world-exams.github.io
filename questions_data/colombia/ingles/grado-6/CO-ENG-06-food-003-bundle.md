## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-food-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"I eat a rice every day."

### Enunciado
Why is this sentence incorrect?

### Options
- [x] A) Rice is uncountable, so we cannot use 'a'.
- [ ] B) Rice is a vegetable.
- [ ] C) You cannot eat rice every day.
- [ ] D) It should be 'an rice'.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Rice** is uncountable. We say "some rice" or just "rice", never "a rice".
