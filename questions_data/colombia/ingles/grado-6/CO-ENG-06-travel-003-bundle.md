## Question 10 (Muy Difícil B - Difficulty 5)

**ID:** `CO-ENG-06-travel-003-v10`

### Contexto
Grammar check.
"Where you go yesterday?"

### Enunciado
What is missing in this question?

### Options
- [x] A) The auxiliary 'did' (Where **did** you go...?).
- [ ] B) The verb 'to be' (Where **are** you go...?).
- [ ] C) The subject (Where go yesterday?).
- [ ] D) Nothing, it is informal English.

### Explicación Pedagógica
In standard English questions (Past Simple), we usually need the auxiliary **did**: "Where **did** you go yesterday?".
