## Question 8 (Grammar - Frequency Adverbs)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-home-family-107-v8`


**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-home-family-107-v8`

### Enunciado
**We (1)_______ have lunch together on weekdays because my parents (2)_______ at work.**
### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) rarely, (2) are
- [ ] B) (1) always, (2) is
