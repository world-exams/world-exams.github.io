## Question 1 (Part 1 - Vocabulary)

### Enunciado
**Your father's mother is your:** *La mamá de tu papá es tu:*

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Aunt *(Tía)*
- [x] B) Grandmother *(Abuela)*
- [ ] C) Cousin *(Prima)*
- [ ] D) Sister *(Hermana)*

### Explicación Pedagógica
*Grand-* = one generation back. Grandmother = parent's mother. Grandfather = parent's father.
*Grand- = una generación atrás. Abuela = madre del padre/madre.*

## Question 7 (Part 7 - Grammar)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-home-family-004-v7`

ID: CO-ENG-07-home-family-004-v7

### Contexto
"There (1)______ a living room, two bedrooms, and a kitchen in my house. There (2)______ also a small garden."

### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) is, (2) is
- [ ] B) (1) are, (2) are
- [ ] C) (1) is, (2) are
- [ ] D) (1) are, (2) is

### Explicación Pedagógica
After "There is/are," use the first noun: "a living room" (singular) = is. "A garden" (singular) = is.
*Después de "There is/are," usa el primer sustantivo: "a living room" (singular) = is.*
