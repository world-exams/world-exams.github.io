**The first meal of the day is:** *La primera comida del día es:*

### Opciones
- [x] A) Breakfast *(Desayuno)*
- [ ] B) Lunch *(Almuerzo)*
- [ ] C) Dinner *(Cena)*
- [ ] D) Snack *(Merienda)*

### Explicación Pedagógica
*Break* + *fast* = break the "fast" (not eating while sleeping). First meal!
*Break + fast = romper el "ayuno" (no comer mientras duermes). ¡Primera comida!*

ID: CO-ENG-07-food-006-v8

### Enunciado
**A "vegetarian" is someone who:** *Un "vegetariano" es alguien que:*

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Only eats meat
- [x] B) Doesn't eat meat but may eat eggs/dairy
- [ ] C) Eats everything
- [ ] D) Only eats vegetables from gardens

### Explicación Pedagógica
Vegetarian = no meat. Vegan = no animal products at all (no eggs, dairy, honey).
*Vegetariano = sin carne. Vegano = sin productos animales (sin huevos, lácteos, miel).*

## Question 10 (Challenge - Critical Thinking)

ID: CO-ENG-07-food-006-v10

### Contexto
**Quote:** "Tell me what you eat, and I will tell you who you are."

### Enunciado
**What does this quote suggest?**

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Food has no meaning.
- [x] B) Food choices reflect culture, values, and identity.
- [ ] C) Everyone eats the same things.

### Explicación Pedagógica

*Comida = identidad cultural, valores de salud, tradiciones, estatus económico. Somos lo que comemos.*
