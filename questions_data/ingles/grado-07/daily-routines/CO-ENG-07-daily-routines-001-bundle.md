## Question 1 (Part 1 - Vocabulary)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-daily-routines-001-v1`

ID: CO-ENG-07-daily-routines-001-v1

### Enunciado
**What do you do when you open your eyes in the morning?**
*¿Qué haces cuando abres los ojos en la mañana?*

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Go to sleep *(Ir a dormir)*
- [x] B) Wake up *(Despertarse)*
- [ ] C) Have dinner *(Cenar)*
- [ ] D) Do homework *(Hacer tarea)*

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Wake up** = open your eyes, stop sleeping. Different from "get up" (leave bed).
*Wake up = abrir los ojos, dejar de dormir. Diferente de "get up" (levantarse de la cama).*

**Morning Routine Vocabulary / Vocabulario de Rutina Matutina:**
| English | Español |
|---------|---------|
| Wake up | Despertarse |
| Get up | Levantarse |
| Brush teeth | Cepillarse los dientes |
| Take a shower | Ducharse |
| Get dressed | Vestirse |
| Have breakfast | Desayunar |

## Question 4 (Part 4 - Grammar)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-daily-routines-001-v4`

ID: CO-ENG-07-daily-routines-001-v4

### Contexto
"I (1)______ breakfast every morning. Yesterday, I (2)______ eggs."

### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) have, (2) had
- [ ] B) (1) had, (2) have
- [ ] C) (1) having, (2) had
- [ ] D) (1) has, (2) had

### Explicación Pedagógica
"Every morning" = habit = present simple (have). "Yesterday" = past simple (had).
*"Every morning" = hábito = presente simple. "Yesterday" = pasado simple.*
