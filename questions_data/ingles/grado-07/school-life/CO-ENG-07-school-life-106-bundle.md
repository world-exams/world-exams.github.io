## Question 8 (Grammar - Present Simple)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-school-life-106-v8`


**ID:** `CO-ENG-07-school-life-106-v8`

### Enunciado
**The teacher (1)_______ the students to (2)_______ their homework every day.**
### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) tells, (2) do
- [ ] B) (1) tell, (2) does
- [ ] C) (1) telling, (2) doing
- [ ] D) (1) told, (2) done

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** Singular third person (The teacher) takes "tells." The infinitive "to" is followed by the base form "do."
*Español: La tercera persona del singular (The teacher) lleva "tells". El infinitivo "to" va seguido de la forma base "do".*
