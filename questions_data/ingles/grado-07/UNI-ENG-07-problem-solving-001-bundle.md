## Question 2 (Part 2 - Time Management - Difficulty 5)
**ID:** `UNI-ENG-07-problem-solving-001-v2`

ID: UNI-ENG-07-problem-solving-001-v2

### Contexto
**Schedule:**
- Movie starts: 4:15 PM
- Movie ends: 6:30 PM
- Dinner reservation: 7:00 PM
- Travel time schedule to restaurant: 45 minutes

### Enunciado
Do they have enough time to get to dinner?

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Yes, they have 1 hour.
- [ ] B) Yes, they have exactly 45 minutes.
- [x] C) No, they will be 15 minutes late.
- [ ] D) No, they are 2 hours late.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** Calculation: 6:30 (End) + 45 min (Travel) = 7:15 arrival. Reservation is 7:00. 7:15 is **15 minutes late**.
*Español: Cálculo matemático-temporal. 6:30 + 45m = 7:15. Llegan tarde.*
