**Words:**
A. Reuse
B. Recycle
C. Reduce
D. Eliminate
E. Compost

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Because it's shaped like lungs.

"Plastic bags (1)______ banned in many countries. Reusable bags (2)______ become more popular as a result."

### Enunciado
**Choose the correct words for (1) and (2).**

### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) have been, (2) have
- [ ] B) (1) was, (2) has
- [ ] C) (1) are being, (2) is

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Correct Answer A:** Present perfect passive ("have been banned") and present perfect ("have become") for recent changes with current relevance.
