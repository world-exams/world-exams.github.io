### Contexto
**Definitions:**
1. To share content originally posted by someone else.
2. A short message or update on social media.
3. The number of people who follow you online.
4. To remove someone's ability to see your content.

**Words:**
A. Repost/Share
B. Post
C. Followers
D. Block
E. Like

### Enunciado
Match definition **"4. To remove someone's ability to see your content"** with the correct word.

### Opciones
- [ ] A
- [ ] B
- [ ] C
- [x] D

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Correct Answer D (Block):** To block someone prevents them from seeing your posts or contacting you. A basic safety feature.

## Question 4 (Part 4 - Grammar Cloze I)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-09-social-media-001-v4`

ID: CO-ENG-09-social-media-001-v4

### Contexto
**Text:**
"I (1)______ Instagram since I was 12. My friends (2)______ me to try TikTok next year."

### Enunciado
**Choose the correct words for (1) and (2).**

### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) have used, (2) want
- [ ] B) (1) used, (2) wanted
- [ ] C) (1) use, (2) are wanting
- [ ] D) (1) am using, (2) wants

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Correct Answer A:** "Have used since" = present perfect (action continuing). "Want" = simple present for general desires.
