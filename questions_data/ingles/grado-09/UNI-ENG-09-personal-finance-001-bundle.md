## Question 1 (Part 1 - Needs vs Wants)
**ID:** `UNI-ENG-09-personal-finance-001-v1`

ID: UNI-ENG-09-personal-finance-001-v1

### Enunciado
Which of these is a **Need** (essential for survival)?

### Opciones
- [ ] A) A new video game
- [x] B) Food / Water
- [ ] C) Designer clothes
- [ ] D) Netflix subscription

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** **Needs** are essential (housing, food). **Wants** are optional.
*Español: Necesidades vs Deseos.*

**Why incorrect?**
*   Other options are entertainment or luxury items (Wants).
