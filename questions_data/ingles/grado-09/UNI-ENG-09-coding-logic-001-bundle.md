## Question 3 (Part 3 - Variable Assignment - Difficulty 4)
**ID:** `UNI-ENG-09-coding-logic-001-v3`

ID: UNI-ENG-09-coding-logic-001-v3

### Contexto
**Steps:**
1. X = 5
2. Y = X + 2
3. X = 10

### Enunciado
What is the value of Y at the end?

### Opciones
- [ ] A) 12
- [ ] B) 5
- [x] C) 7
- [ ] D) 10

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** **Sequence matters**. Y is calculated in step 2 (5+2=7). Step 3 changing X does not retroactively change Y (in standard logic).
*Español: Asignación secuencial de variables.*

**Recipe Algorithm:**

2. Wait 30 minutes.
3. Turn on oven.

### Enunciado
Why will this fail?

### Opciones
- [ ] A) You forgot the cake.
- [x] B) You put it in before turning the oven on (Sequence Error).
- [ ] C) 30 minutes is too long.
