Match definition: **"The ability to understand and share the feelings of another"**

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Sympathy
- [x] B) Empathy
- [ ] C) Apathy
- [ ] D) Antipathy

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Correct Answer B (Empathy):** Feeling WITH someone (understanding their perspective). Different from sympathy (feeling FOR someone).
