"She (1)______ exercise regularly since January. As a result, she (2)______ much healthier now."

### Enunciado
**Choose the correct words for (1) and (2).**

### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) has been exercising, (2) feels
- [ ] B) (1) exercised, (2) felt
- [ ] C) (1) exercises, (2) will feel
- [ ] D) (1) was exercising, (2) is feeling

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Correct Answer A:** Present perfect continuous for ongoing action since the past. Simple present for current state.
