### Enunciado

### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) should, (2) skip
- [ ] B) (1) must, (2) skipped
- [ ] C) (1) can, (2) will skip

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Correct Answer A:** "Should" for health advice. First conditional for consequence: if + present, may + base verb.
