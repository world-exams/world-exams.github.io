"The movie (1)______ already started when we arrived. We (2)______ the first 10 minutes."

### Enunciado
**Choose the correct words for (1) and (2).**

### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) had, (2) missed
- [ ] B) (1) has, (2) miss
- [ ] C) (1) was, (2) are missing
- [ ] D) (1) is, (2) will miss

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Correct Answer A:** Past perfect ("had started" = earlier past) + simple past ("missed" = later past).
