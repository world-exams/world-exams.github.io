### Enunciado
**I (1)_______ that movie three times, and I (2)_______ it is a masterpiece.**
### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) have seen, (2) think
- [ ] B) (1) see, (2) thought
- [ ] C) (1) saw, (2) am thinking
- [ ] D) (1) seen, (2) thinks

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** Present perfect ("have seen") for an action repeated in the past. Present simple ("think") for an opinion.
*Español: Presente perfecto para una acción repetida en el pasado. Presente simple para una opinión.*
