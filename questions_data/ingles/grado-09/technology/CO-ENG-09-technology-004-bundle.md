"The first smartphone (1)______ invented in 1992. Since then, technology (2)______ dramatically."

### Enunciado
**Choose the correct words for (1) and (2).**

### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) was, (2) has changed
- [ ] B) (1) is, (2) changed
- [ ] C) (1) has been, (2) changes
- [ ] D) (1) were, (2) had changed

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Correct Answer A:** "Was invented in 1992" = past passive (specific past). "Has changed since then" = present perfect (past to now).
