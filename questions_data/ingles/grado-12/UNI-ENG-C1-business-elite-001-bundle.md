## Question 5 (Part 5 - Economic Trends - Difficulty 5)
**ID:** `UNI-ENG-C1-business-elite-001-v5`

ID: UNI-ENG-C1-business-elite-001-v5

### Contexto
**Report:**
"The market has saturated; consequently, profit margins are _______."

### Enunciado
Choose the verb that logically follows logical market saturation.

### Opciones
- [ ] A) skyrocketing
- [ ] B) expanding
- [ ] C) soaring
- [x] D) dwindling

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** If a market is **saturated** (full), growth is hard, so margins typically **dwindle** (diminish/shrink). The other options imply growth.
*Español: Si el mercado está saturado, los márgenes **disminuyen (dwindle)**.*
