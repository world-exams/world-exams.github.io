## Question 1 (Part 1 - Logic Matching - Difficulty 4)
**ID:** `UNI-ENG-06-logic-puzzles-001-v1`

ID: UNI-ENG-06-logic-puzzles-001-v1

### Contexto
**Clues:**
1. It has four legs but no tail.
2. You sit on it.
3. It is not an animal.

### Enunciado
What is it?

### Opciones
- [ ] A) A dog
- [x] B) A chair
- [ ] C) A sofa
- [ ] D) A table

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** You must combine three simple facts. A dog matches #1 but fails #2 and #3. A table matches #1 and #3 but you don't usually sit *on* it. A **chair** fits all clues perfectly.
*Español: Debes combinar tres pistas simples. Una silla es la única que cumple todas.*

### Enunciado

### Opciones

### Enunciado

### Opciones

## Question 7 (Part 7 - Mathematical Logic - Difficulty 4)
**ID:** `UNI-ENG-06-logic-puzzles-001-v7`

ID: UNI-ENG-06-logic-puzzles-001-v7

### Contexto
**Math Word Problem:**
"I have 3 red pencils and 2 blue pencils. I give 1 red pencil to my friend."

### Enunciado
How many pencils do I have now?

### Opciones
- [ ] A) 5
- [x] B) 4
- [ ] C) 3
- [ ] D) 2

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** Total start: 3 + 2 = 5. Give away 1. Result: 5 - 1 = 4. Tests **numbers** and **verbs** like "give".
*Español: Total inicial 5, menos 1 que regalé, quedan 4.*
