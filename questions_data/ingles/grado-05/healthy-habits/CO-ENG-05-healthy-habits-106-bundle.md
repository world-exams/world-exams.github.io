**ID:** `CO-ENG-05-healthy-habits-106-v2`

### Enunciado
**I (1)_______ fruits and vegetables every day because they (2)_______ healthy.**
### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) eat, (2) are
- [ ] B) (1) eats, (2) is
- [ ] C) (1) eat, (2) is
- [ ] D) (1) drinks, (2) are

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** "I" takes the base form "eat." Plural subjects (fruits and vegetables) take "are."
*Español: "I" (yo) lleva la forma base "eat". Los sujetos en plural llevan "are".*
