**ID:** `CO-ENG-05-descriptions-110-v2`

### Enunciado
**My sister (1)_______ long hair and (2)_______ very smart.**
### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) has, (2) is
- [ ] B) (1) is, (2) has
- [ ] C) (1) have, (2) am
- [ ] D) (1) am, (2) is

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** Use "has" for possessions (hair) and "is" for characteristics (smart).
*Español: Usa "has" para posesiones (cabello) e "is" para características (inteligente).*
