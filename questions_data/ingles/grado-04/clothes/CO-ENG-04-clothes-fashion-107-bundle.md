## Question 8 (Grammar - Possessives)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-04-clothes-fashion-107-v8`


**ID:** `CO-ENG-04-clothes-fashion-107-v8`

### Enunciado
**This is (1)_______ jacket and those (2)_______ your shoes.**
### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) my, (2) are
- [ ] B) (1) mine, (2) is
- [ ] C) (1) I, (2) are
- [ ] D) (1) me, (2) is

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** Use the possessive adjective "my" before a noun. Use "are" for plural "shoes."
*Español: Usa el adjetivo posesivo "my" antes de un sustantivo. Usa "are" para el plural "shoes".*
