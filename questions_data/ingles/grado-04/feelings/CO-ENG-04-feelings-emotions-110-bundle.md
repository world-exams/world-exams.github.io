## Question 2 (Grammar)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-04-feelings-emotions-110-v2`


**ID:** `CO-ENG-04-feelings-emotions-110-v2`

### Enunciado
**She (1)_______ sad because she (2)_______ find her toy.**
### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) is, (2) can't
- [ ] B) (1) are, (2) doesn't
- [ ] C) (1) am, (2) can't
- [ ] D) (1) is, (2) don't

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** Use "is" for state of being (feelings) and "can't" for inability (finding the toy).
*Español: Usa "is" para el estado de ser (sentimientos) y "can't" para la incapacidad (encontrar el juguete).*
