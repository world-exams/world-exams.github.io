"She goes to work ______ bus every day."

### Opciones
- [x] A) by
- [ ] B) on
- [ ] C) in
- [ ] D) with

### Explicación Pedagógica
- **A) by:** Correct. We use "by" + transport mode (by bus, by car, by train).
- **B) on:** Incorrect. We say "on the bus" (specific), but for general mode, "by" is better. However, "on bus" is incorrect grammar without "the".
- **C) in:** Incorrect. Used for "in a car" or "in a taxi".
- **D) with:** Incorrect. Means accompanied by.

- [ ] C) was / started
- [ ] D) have / started

### Explicación Pedagógica
- **A) had / started:** Correct. Past Perfect (had + participle) is used for an action that happened before another action in the past.
