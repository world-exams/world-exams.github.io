2. The practice of dividing power among government branches.
3. A government controlled by a small elite group.
4. The principle that all are equal under the law.

**Words:**
A. Rule of Law
B. Direct Democracy

"Social media has transformed political engagement, enabling rapid information sharing and grassroots organization. ### Opciones
- [ ] A) It was completely unnecessary.
- [x] B) It provided a filtering function that is now missing.

- [ ] D) It never influenced political opinions.
