## Question 10 (Bonus - C1 Challenge)
**ID:** `CO-ING-11-advertising-001-v10`

ID: CO-ING-11-advertising-001-v10

### Enunciado
**Sentence Transformation:**
"The company launched a new campaign to increase sales."
**Rewrite using:** "AIMED AT"

### Opciones
- [ ] A) The company launched a new campaign aimed at increased sales.
- [x] B) The company launched a new campaign aimed at increasing sales.
- [ ] C) The company aimed at a new campaign to increase sales.
- [ ] D) Increasing sales was the aim at the company.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Grammar:** 'Aimed at' is followed by a gerund (-ing form) or noun phrase. 'Aimed at increasing sales' is the correct structure. Option A uses past/adjective 'increased' which is grammatically awkward here (aimed at [doing something]). Option B is standard English.
