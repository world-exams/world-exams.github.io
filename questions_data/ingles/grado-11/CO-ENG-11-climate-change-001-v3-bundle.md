### Contexto
**Reading passage:**
"The Greenhouse Effect is a natural process that warms the Earth's surface. When the Sun's energy reaches the Earth's atmosphere, some of it is reflected back to space and the rest is absorbed and re-radiated by greenhouse gases. These gases include water vapor, carbon dioxide, methane, and nitrous oxide. Without this effect, Earth would be too cold to support life."

### Enunciado
According to the text, what happens to the Sun's energy when it reaches Earth?

### Opciones
- [ ] A) It is all reflected back to space.
- [ ] B) It is all absorbed by the oceans.
- [x] C) Part of it is reflected and part is absorbed by gases.
- [ ] D) It disappears before reaching the surface.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** The text states: "...some of it is reflected back... and the rest is absorbed and re-radiated."
*Español: El texto afirma: "...parte se refleja... y el resto es absorbido y re-irradiado".*

ID: CO-ENG-11-climate-change-001-v8

### Contexto
**Reading passage (Same as above):**
"Without this effect, Earth would be too cold to support life."

### Enunciado
What can be inferred about the Greenhouse Effect from the passage?

### Opciones
- [ ] A) It is entirely harmful to the planet.

ID: CO-ENG-11-climate-change-001-v9

### Contexto
**Text:**
(9) ______ the global consensus on climate change, some groups still question its (10) ______.

### Enunciado
Choose the correct word for (9).

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Although
- [x] B) Despite
- [ ] C) Even though
- [ ] D) However

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** **Despite** is followed by a noun phrase ("the global consensus"). **Although/Even though** would require a full clause (subject + verb).
*Español: **Despite (A pesar de)** es seguido por una frase nominal. **Although** requeriría una oración completa.*
