## Texto de Lectura (Maria's Decision)

"Maria had always dreamed of traveling the world. After graduating from university, she found a job that paid well but demanded long hours and offered little vacation time. One day, while browsing online, she saw an advertisement for a volunteer program in South America that involved teaching English and protecting endangered species. The program was unpaid, but it covered accommodation and food. She spent weeks deliberating, weighing the **financial stability** of her current job against the **adventure and purpose** offered by the volunteer program. Eventually, she made a decision and handed in her resignation."

## Pregunta 1 (Original - Dificultad 3)
**ID:** `CO-ING-11-part6-inference-001-v1`


**ID:** `CO-ING-11-part6-inference-001-v1`

### Enunciado

**What is the main reason why Maria considered leaving her job?**

### Opciones

- [x] A) She was looking for an experience aligned with her personal dreams and values.
- [ ] B) She wanted to make more money volunteering.
- [ ] C) She was fired by her boss for being lazy.
- [ ] D) She hated South America and wanted to leave.

### Explicación Pedagógica

**¿Por qué A es correcta?**
El texto dice que soñaba viajar ("dreamed of traveling") y contrastó su estabilidad financiera con "adventure and purpose". Dejar un trabajo bien pagado por uno no pagado (unpaid) implica que busca **significado** (values/dreams) sobre dinero.

**Competencia evaluada:** Lectura inferencial (motivaciones implícitas).

**What is the most likely outcome for Maria immediately after the text ends?**

### Opciones

- [x] A) She will pack her bags and travel to South America.
- [ ] B) She will look for another office job in her city.
- [ ] C) She will ask her boss for a promotion.
- [ ] D) She will buy a new house.
