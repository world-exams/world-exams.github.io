## Question 2 (Grammar)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-globalization-107-v2`

### Enunciado
**If globalization (1)_______ so rapidly, our local markets (2)_______ very different today.**
### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) hadn't expanded, (2) would be
- [ ] B) (1) didn't expand, (2) will be
- [ ] C) (1) wasn't expand, (2) would was
- [ ] D) (1) had expanded, (2) is

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** Mixed conditional. We are talking about a past condition (hadn't expanded) and its present result (would be).
*Español: Condicional mixto. Estamos hablando de una condición pasada (hadn't expanded) y su resultado presente (would be).*

## Question 4 (Vocabulary)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-globalization-107-v4`

### Enunciado
**The policy of protecting domestic industries against foreign competition by means of tariffs or quotas is called _______.**
### Opciones
- [x] A) Protectionism
- [ ] B) Free trade
- [ ] C) Capitalism
- [ ] D) Socialism

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** Protectionism is the opposite of free trade and focuses on supporting local business by taxing imports.
*Español: El proteccionismo es lo opuesto al libre comercio y se enfoca en apoyar el negocio local mediante impuestos a las importaciones.*

## Question 6 (Inferential)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-globalization-107-v6`

### Contexto
**Passage:** "While English remains the dominant language of global business and science, the rise of regional powers is creating a more 'multipolar' world where multiple cultures influence each other simultaneously."
### Enunciado
**What does the passage suggest about the future?**
### Opciones
- [ ] A) Only English will be spoken.
- [x] B) The world will be influenced by many different powerful regions, not just one.
- [ ] C) Cultures will stop interacting.
- [ ] D) Science will disappear.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** "Multipolar" and "multiple cultures influence each other" implies a diversity of power and influence.
*Español: "Multipolar" y "múltiples culturas se influencian entre sí" implica una diversidad de poder e influencia.*
