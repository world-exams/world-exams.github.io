A. Tariffs
B. Outsourcing

## Question 10 (Challenge - Critical Thinking)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-globalization-005-v10`

ID: CO-ENG-11-globalization-005-v10

### Contexto
**Statement:**
"True globalization would mean not just free movement of goods and capital, but also free movement of people across all borders."

### Enunciado
**What critique of current globalization is implied?**

### Opciones
- [ ] A) There is too much migration in the world.
- [x] B) Current globalization favors capital mobility over labor mobility.
- [ ] C) All borders should be immediately abolished.
- [ ] D) Goods and capital should be restricted.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Correct Answer B:** The statement implies an asymmetry: goods and money move freely, but people face restrictions. This critiques selective/incomplete globalization.
