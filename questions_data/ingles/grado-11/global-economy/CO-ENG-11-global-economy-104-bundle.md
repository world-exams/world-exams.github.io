## Question 1 (Part 1 - Vocabulary)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-global-economy-104-v1`

### Enunciado
**A general increase in prices over time is called:**
### Opciones
- [x] A) Inflation
- [ ] B) Deflation
- [ ] C) Recession
- [ ] D) Depression

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** *Inflation* = prices go up, purchasing power goes down. Tracked by DANE in Colombia.
*Español: *Inflación* = los precios suben, el poder adquisitivo baja. Medida por el DANE en Colombia.*

| English | Español | Effect |
|---------|---------|--------|
| Inflation | Inflación | ⬆️ Prices |
| Deflation | Deflación | ⬇️ Prices |
| Recession | Recesión | Economic decline |

## Question 9 (Challenge - Use of English)

### Contexto
**Original:** "Many economists predict a recession."
**Rewrite:** *A RECESSION...*
### Enunciado
**Choose correct transformation.**
### Opciones
- [x] A) A recession is predicted by many economists.
- [ ] B) A recession predict by many economists.
- [ ] C) A recession has predicted.
- [ ] D) A recession being predicted.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** Passive: Object becomes subject + is/are + past participle + by agent.
*Español: Pasiva: Objeto se convierte en sujeto + is/are + participio pasado + by agente.*
