## Question 7 (Part 7 - Advanced Grammar)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-social-justice-105-v7`

### Contexto
"(1)______ the protests, the government (2)______ to address systemic inequality."
### Enunciado
**Choose correct options.**
### Opciones
- [ ] A) (1) Despite, (2) has failed
- [x] B) (1) Following, (2) has begun
- [ ] C) (1) Although, (2) failed
- [ ] D) (1) Because, (2) begun

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** "Following" = after (causal link). "Has begun" = present perfect (recent action).
*Español: "Following" = después (enlace causal). "Has begun" = presente perfecto (acción reciente).*
