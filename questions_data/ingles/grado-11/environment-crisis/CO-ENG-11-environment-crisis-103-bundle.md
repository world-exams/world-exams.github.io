**The variety of plant and animal life in a particular habitat is called:**

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Ecosystem
- [x] B) Biodiversity
- [ ] C) Pollution
- [ ] D) Deforestation

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** "Biodiversity" = bio (life) + diversity (variety). Colombia is the 2nd most biodiverse country in the world!

*Español: "Biodiversity" (biodiversidad) = bio (vida) + diversidad. ¡Colombia es el 2do país más biodiverso del mundo!*

| English | Español | Root |
|---------|---------|------|
| Biodiversity | Biodiversidad | Bio = life |
| Ecosystem | Ecosistema | Eco = environment |
| Deforestation | Deforestación | De = remove |

**Passage:** "Companies now market 'sustainable' products with green packaging. Yet many merely swap one material for another without addressing production practices. True sustainability requires systemic change, not cosmetic rebranding."

**What does the author suggest about 'green' marketing?**

### Opciones
- [ ] A) All green products are sustainable.
- [x] B) Some companies use sustainability as marketing without real changes.

*Español: "Cosmetic rebranding" (cambio superficial) y "merely swap" (simplemente intercambiar) sugieren cambios superficiales. El autor critica el "greenwashing."*

**Greenwashing** = falsely marketing products as eco-friendly.
*Greenwashing = falsamente promocionar productos como ecológicos.*

**Original:** "Scientists have warned us about climate change for decades."
**Rewrite using:** *WE HAVE BEEN...*

### Enunciado
**Choose the correct transformation.**

### Opciones
