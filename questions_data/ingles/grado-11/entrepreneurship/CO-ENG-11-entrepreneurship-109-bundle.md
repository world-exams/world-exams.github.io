## Question 2 (Grammar)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-entrepreneurship-109-v2`

### Enunciado
**The CEO (1)_______ the company for ten years by the time she (2)_______ to retire next winter.**
### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) will have been leading, (2) decides
- [ ] B) (1) leads, (2) decided
- [ ] C) (1) is leading, (2) will decide
- [ ] D) (1) led, (2) decide

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** Future perfect continuous ("will have been leading") for an action that will be happening up to a point in the future.
*Español: Futuro perfecto continuo ("will have been leading") para una acción que estará ocurriendo hasta un punto en el futuro.*

## Question 8 (Grammar - Conditional)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-entrepreneurship-109-v8`

### Enunciado
**Unless the team (1)_______ effectively, the project (2)_______ its deadline.**
### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) cooperates, (2) will miss
- [ ] B) (1) don't cooperate, (2) is missing
- [ ] C) (1) cooperated, (2) would miss
- [ ] D) (1) will cooperate, (2) misses

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** "Unless" means "if not." So: "If the team does NOT cooperate, it will miss the deadline."
*Español: "Unless" significa "si no". Por lo tanto: "Si el equipo NO coopera, perderá la fecha límite".*
