## Question 1 (Part 1 - Vocabulary)

### Enunciado
**A general increase in prices over time is called:**

### Opciones
- [x] A) Inflation
- [ ] B) Deflation
- [ ] C) Recession
- [ ] D) Depression

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** *Inflation* = prices go up, purchasing power goes down. *Deflation* = opposite. In Colombia, inflation is tracked by DANE.

*Español: *Inflación* = los precios suben, el poder adquisitivo baja. *Deflación* = lo contrario. En Colombia, la inflación es medida por el DANE.*

| Term | Effect on Prices | Término |
|------|-----------------|---------|
| Inflation | ⬆️ Prices increase | Inflación |
| Deflation | ⬇️ Prices decrease | Deflación |
| Stagflation | High inflation + slow growth | Estanflación |

### Contexto
**Bank advertisement:** "Open a savings account today! Earn 5% APY. Terms and conditions apply."

### Enunciado
**What does "APY" stand for?**

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Annual Payment Yearly
- [x] B) Annual Percentage Yield
- [ ] C) Automatic Payment Year
- [ ] D) Additional Profit Yearly

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** APY = Annual Percentage Yield, the total interest earned on a deposit over a year, including compound interest.

*Español: APY = Rendimiento Porcentual Anual, el interés total ganado en un depósito durante un año, incluyendo interés compuesto.*
