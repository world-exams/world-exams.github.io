### Explicación Pedagógica
- **A) take:** Correct. First Conditional with "Unless" (If not). "Unless + Present Simple, ... Future".
- **B) will take:** Incorrect. Future not used in the conditional clause.

- **D) would take:** Incorrect. Conditional form not used in the if/unless clause.
