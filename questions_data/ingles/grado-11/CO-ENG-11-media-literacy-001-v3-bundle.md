## Question 8 (Part 6 - Inferential Reading - Difficulty 4)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-media-literacy-001-v8`

ID: CO-ENG-11-media-literacy-001-v8

### Contexto
**Reading passage (Same as above):**
"In an echo chamber, people are rarely exposed to differing viewpoints, which can lead to increased political polarization."

### Enunciado
What can be inferred about political polarization?

### Opciones
- [ ] A) It is caused exclusively by the internet.
- [x] B) It can be worsened by the lack of exposure to diverse opinions.
- [ ] C) It is a positive development for democracy.
- [ ] D) It helps people understand each other better.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** If the lack of exposure to differing viewpoints "can lead to" polarization, it means it can **worsen** it.
*Español: Si la falta de exposición a puntos de vista diferentes "puede llevar a" la polarización, significa que puede **empeorarla**.*
