### Enunciado

- [x] B) Artificial Intelligence

## Question 2 (Grammar)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-11-artificial-intelligence-106-v2`

### Enunciado
**AI (1)_______ rapidly in recent years, and it (2)_______ to change our lives significantly.**
### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) has developed, (2) is expected
- [ ] B) (1) developed, (2) expected
- [ ] C) (1) is developing, (2) expects
- [ ] D) (1) had developed, (2) was expected

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** We use the present perfect ("has developed") for actions that started in the past and continue to the present. The passive voice ("is expected") is used for general predictions.
*Español: Usamos el presente perfecto ("has developed") para acciones que comenzaron en el pasado y continúan en el presente. La voz pasiva ("is expected") se usa para predicciones generales.*
