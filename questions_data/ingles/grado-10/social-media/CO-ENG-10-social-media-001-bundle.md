## Question 9 (Challenge - Analysis)

ID: CO-ENG-10-social-media-001-v9

### Contexto
**Statistic:**
"The average teenager spends 7+ hours daily on screens, with 3+ hours specifically on social media."

### Enunciado
**What question does this statistic NOT answer?**

### Opciones
- [ ] A) How much time do teens spend on screens?
- [ ] B) How much of screen time is social media?
- [x] C) What is the impact of this usage on mental health?
- [ ] D) Is social media part of teen screen time?

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Correct Answer C:** The statistic provides TIME data but no OUTCOME data. Critical thinking: statistics show correlation, not causation.

- [ ] A) Teenagers should eat healthier food.
