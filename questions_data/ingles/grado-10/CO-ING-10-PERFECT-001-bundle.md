### Enunciado

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Since 2 years.
- [x] B) For 2 years / Since 2021.
- [ ] C) Ago 2 years.
- [ ] D) From 2 years.

### Explicación Pedagógica

> **Análisis**: For + Period (2 years). Since + Start point (2020). Never use "Since 2 years".
