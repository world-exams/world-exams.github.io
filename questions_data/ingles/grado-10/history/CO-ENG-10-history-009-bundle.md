4. The period of industrial development in the late 18th century.

**Words:**

B. Imperialism
C. Revolution

"The Berlin Wall, (1)______ divided East and West Germany, stood for 28 years. It (2)______ finally demolished in 1989."

### Enunciado
**Choose the correct words for (1) and (2).**

### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) which, (2) was
- [ ] B) (1) that, (2) is
- [ ] C) (1) who, (2) has been
- [ ] D) (1) whose, (2) were

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Correct Answer A:** Non-defining clause (comma + which). "Was demolished in 1989" = past passive for completed historical event.
