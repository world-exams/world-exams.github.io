### Enunciado
Contraction: *"I'd go."* means:

### Opciones
- [ ] A) I had go.
- [ ] B) I did go.
- [x] C) I would go.
- [ ] D) I could go.

### Explicación Pedagógica
> ### 📊 Info-Tarjeta: 'd
> **Análisis**: Be careful, 'd can be "had" or "would". Check the verb. "I'd go" (would + base). "I'd gone" (had + participle).
