### Enunciado
**A person who starts their own business is called:**

### Opciones

- [x] B) Entrepreneur
- [ ] C) Manager
- [ ] D) Consultant

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** *Entrepreneur* = someone who creates and runs their own business, taking financial risks. From French "entreprendre" (to undertake).

*Español: *Emprendedor* = alguien que crea y administra su propio negocio, tomando riesgos financieros. Del francés "entreprendre" (emprender).*

**Career Vocabulary / Vocabulario de Carreras:**
| English | Español | Description |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Entrepreneur | Emprendedor | Starts own business |
| Freelancer | Trabajador independiente | Self-employed, various clients |
| Intern | Practicante | Trainee, learning |
| CEO | Director ejecutivo | Top company leader |

**Famous Colombian entrepreneurs:** Luisa Fernanda Espinosa (Rappi co-founder)
*Emprendedores colombianos famosos: Luisa Fernanda Espinosa (cofundadora de Rappi)*
