## Question 1 (Part 1 - Vocabulary)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-environment-101-v1`

ID: CO-ENG-10-environment-101-v1

### Enunciado
**The variety of plant and animal life in a particular habitat is called:**

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Ecosystem
- [x] B) Biodiversity
- [ ] C) Pollution
- [ ] D) Deforestation

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** *Biodiversity* = bio (life) + diversity (variety). Colombia is the 2nd most biodiverse country in the world!

*Español: *Biodiversidad* = bio (vida) + diversidad (variedad). ¡Colombia es el 2do país más biodiverso del mundo!*

**Vocabulary Table / Tabla de Vocabulario:**
| English | Español | Root |
|---------|---------|------|
| Biodiversity | Biodiversidad | Bio = life |
| Ecosystem | Ecosistema | Eco = environment |
| Deforestation | Deforestación | De = remove, Forest |
| Conservation | Conservación | Conserv = keep |

## Question 4 (Part 4 - Grammar)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-environment-101-v4`

ID: CO-ENG-10-environment-101-v4

### Contexto
"If we (1)______ using fossil fuels now, we (2)______ slow down climate change."

### Enunciado
**Complete with correct verb forms.**

### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) stop, (2) can
- [ ] B) (1) stopped, (2) could
- [ ] C) (1) will stop, (2) will
- [ ] D) (1) stopping, (2) can

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** First Conditional: If + present, modal + verb. "If we stop... we can slow" = real possibility.

*Español: Primera Condicional: If + presente, modal + verbo. "If we stop... we can slow" = posibilidad real.*

| Conditional | Formula | Use |
|-------------|---------|-----|
| 1st | If + present, will/can | Real possibility |
| 2nd | If + past, would | Hypothetical |

## Question 6 (Part 6 - Inferential Reading)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-environment-101-v6`

ID: CO-ENG-10-environment-101-v6

### Contexto
**Passage:**
"Companies now market 'sustainable' products with green packaging and nature imagery. Yet many merely swap one material for another without addressing production practices. True sustainability requires systemic change, not cosmetic rebranding."

### Enunciado
**What does the author suggest about 'green' marketing?**

### Opciones
- [ ] A) All green products are genuinely sustainable.
- [x] B) Some companies use sustainability as marketing rather than making real changes.
- [ ] C) Green packaging always helps the environment.
- [ ] D) Marketing and sustainability are the same thing.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** "Cosmetic rebranding" and "merely swap" suggest superficial changes. The author criticizes "greenwashing"—marketing products as eco-friendly without real change.

*Español: "Cosmetic rebranding" (cambio de imagen superficial) y "merely swap" (simplemente intercambiar) sugieren cambios superficiales. El autor critica el "greenwashing"—marketing de productos como ecológicos sin cambio real.*

**Greenwashing** = when companies falsely market products as environmentally friendly.
*Greenwashing = cuando empresas falsamente promocionan productos como ecológicos.*
