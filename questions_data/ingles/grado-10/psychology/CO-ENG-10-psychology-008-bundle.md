## Question 10 (Challenge - Critical Thinking)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-10-psychology-008-v10`

ID: CO-ENG-10-psychology-008-v10

### Contexto
**Statement:**
"We judge ourselves by our intentions and others by their actions."

### Enunciado
**What cognitive bias does this statement describe?**

### Opciones
- [x] A) The tendency to be more forgiving of our own mistakes than others'.
- [ ] B) The belief that everyone thinks like us.
- [ ] C) The inability to form judgments about behavior.
- [ ] D) Perfect fairness in evaluating everyone.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**Correct Answer A:** This is the "fundamental attribution error"—we excuse ourselves (intentions) but blame others (actions). A key concept in social psychology.
