A person steals bread to feed their starving family. They are caught and face trial.

### Enunciado
**Which legal concept might a defense attorney invoke?**

### Opciones
- [ ] A) The person definitely deserves maximum punishment.
- [x] B) Necessity—the crime was committed to prevent greater harm.
- [ ] C) The person should be praised, not tried.
