# Pregunta Base: The Light Bulb

> **Context:**
> Active: *"Thomas Edison **invented** the light bulb."*
> Passive: *"The light bulb **was invented** by Thomas Edison."*

---

**ID:** `CO-ING-10-PASSIVE-001-v1`


**ID:** `CO-ING-10-PASSIVE-001-v1`

### Enunciado
In the Passive Voice sentence, the focus is on:

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Thomas Edison (The doer).
- [x] B) The light bulb (The object/action) rather than the person who did it.
- [ ] C) The future.
- [ ] D) The place.

### Explicación Pedagógica
> ### 📊 Info-Tarjeta: Focus
> **Análisis**: We use passive when the action is more important than the agent, or the agent is unknown.
