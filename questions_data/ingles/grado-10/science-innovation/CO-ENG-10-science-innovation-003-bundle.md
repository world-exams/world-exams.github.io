### Contexto
**Statement:**
"The beauty of science is that it doesn't care what you believe. Evidence is evidence, regardless of who presents it or who disagrees."

### Enunciado
**What principle does this statement express?**

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Science is based on personal opinions.
