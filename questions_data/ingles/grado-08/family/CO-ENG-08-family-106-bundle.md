## Question 8 (Grammar - Past Continuous)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-family-history-106-v8`


**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-family-history-106-v8`

### Enunciado
**I (1)_______ through some old albums when I (2)_______ a photo of my parents' wedding.**
### Opciones
- [x] A) (1) was looking, (2) found
- [ ] B) (1) looked, (2) find
- [ ] C) (1) am looking, (2) finded
- [ ] D) (1) were looking, (2) found

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** We use past continuous for the long action and past simple for the shorter action that interrupted it.
*Español: Usamos el pasado continuo para la acción larga y el pasado simple para la acción más corta que la interrumpió.*
