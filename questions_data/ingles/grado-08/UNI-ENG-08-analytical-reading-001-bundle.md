## Question 1 (Part 1 - Data Analysis - Difficulty 4)
**ID:** `UNI-ENG-08-analytical-reading-001-v1`

ID: UNI-ENG-08-analytical-reading-001-v1

### Contexto
**Survey Results (Favorite Pets):**
- Dogs: 50%
- Cats: 30%
- Birds: 15%
- Others: 5%

### Enunciado
Which statement is true based on the data?

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Everyone loves dogs.
- [x] B) Half of the people prefer dogs.
- [ ] C) Birds are more popular than cats.
- [ ] D) Nobody likes other animals.

### Explicación Pedagógica
**English:** 50% = **Half**. Direct data interpretation. "Everyone" (100%) is false.
*Español: Interpretación básica de porcentajes (50% = La mitad).*
