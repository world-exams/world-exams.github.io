## Question 10 (Challenge - Critical Thinking)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-media-010-v10`

ID: CO-ENG-08-media-010-v10

### Contexto
**Statement:** "The media doesn't just report reality—it shapes how we see it."

### Enunciado
**What does this statement mean?**

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Media always shows the complete truth.
- [x] B) Media presentation influences our perception of events.
- [ ] C) Reality TV is real.
- [ ] D) We should believe everything in the media.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Media selects WHAT to show and HOW to show it. Same event, different framing = different reaction.
*Los medios seleccionan QUÉ mostrar y CÓMO mostrarlo. Mismo evento, diferente enfoque = diferente reacción.*
