**A doctor who specializes in children is called a:**

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Cardiologist *(Cardiólogo)*

### Contexto
**Prescription label:** "Take 1 tablet every 8 hours with food. Do not exceed 3 tablets in 24 hours."

### Enunciado
**How many tablets per day?** *¿Cuántas tabletas por día?*

### Opciones
- [ ] A) 1
- [ ] B) 2
- [x] C) 3

*Cada 8 horas = 3 veces por día (24÷8=3). "No exceder 3" lo confirma.*

## Question 8 (Challenge - Vocabulary)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-health-006-v8`

ID: CO-ENG-08-health-006-v8

### Enunciado
**"Prevention" means:** *"Prevención" significa:*

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Curing a disease after you get it
- [x] B) Taking action to stop something bad from happening
- [ ] C) Ignoring health problems
- [ ] D) Surgery

### Explicación Pedagógica
*Pre-* = before, *-vent* = come. Prevention = stopping problems BEFORE they happen (vaccines, hygiene, exercise).
*Pre- = antes, -vent = venir. Prevención = detener problemas ANTES de que ocurran.*
