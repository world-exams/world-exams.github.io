## Question 10 (Challenge - Critical Thinking)
**ID:** `CO-ENG-08-food-007-v10`

ID: CO-ENG-08-food-007-v10

### Contexto
**Michael Pollan:** "Eat food. Not too much. Mostly plants."

### Enunciado
**What advice does this quote give?**

### Opciones
- [ ] A) Only eat plants, nothing else.
- [x] B) Eat real food (not processed), in moderation, with emphasis on vegetables.
- [ ] C) Eating a lot is good.
- [ ] D) Processed food is fine.

### Explicación Pedagógica
Three rules: 1) "Food" = real, not ultra-processed. 2) "Not too much" = moderation. 3) "Mostly plants" = vegetables as foundation.
*Tres reglas: 1) "Comida" = real, no ultra procesada. 2) "No demasiado" = moderación. 3) "Mayormente plantas" = vegetales como base.*
