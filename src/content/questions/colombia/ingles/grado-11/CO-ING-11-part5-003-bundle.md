# Pregunta Base: Shakira - A Colombian Icon

> **Contexto:** Read the text and answer the questions based on it.

**From Barranquilla to the World**

Shakira Isabel Mebarak Ripoll, known simply as Shakira, was born on February 2, 1977, in Barranquilla, Colombia. She is a singer, songwriter, and dancer famous for her unique voice and hip movements. Her music blends Latin, Rock, and Arabic influences, reflecting her Lebanese and Colombian heritage. She wrote her first song at the age of eight and signed her first record deal at thirteen.

While she is a global superstar with hits like "Hips Don't Lie" and "Waka Waka," Shakira is also a dedicated **philanthropist**. In 1997, she founded the **Pies Descalzos Foundation** (Barefoot Foundation). The mission of this organization is to provide high-quality public education and nutrition to children in impoverished areas of Colombia. She believes that education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty.

Shakira's success has made her the highest-selling Colombian artist of all time. She continues to use her fame to advocate for children's rights as a UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador.
