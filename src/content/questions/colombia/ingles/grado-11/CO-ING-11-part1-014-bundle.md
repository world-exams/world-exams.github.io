### Opciones
- [x] A) He advised me to accept the offer.
- [ ] B) He said that if he was me, he would accept the offer.
- [ ] C) He said that if I were you, I will accept the offer.
- [ ] D) He said that he accepted the offer.

### Explicaci√≥n Pedag√≥gica
